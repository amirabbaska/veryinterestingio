    ELI5: How deodorant works and why it "stops working" after a bit

    stevegaloshes A:

    deodorant kills the bacteria that grow in your armpits so they don't make the smell you associate with body odor, and usually also smells nice

    antiperspirant blocks your sweat glands so that they don't extrude the sweat that the bacteria feed on to make body odor

    in both cases they stop working when the active chemical isn't present in a high enough concentration to keep working
