    Q:

    ELI5: why does 3 sugar packets make my coffee taste very sweet, but bottled cappuccinos with 30+ grams of sugar taste normal?

    dwaynebrady A:

    In short, Fat is the answer.

    If you're having coffee and sugar then there is a major discrepancy between that and cappuccino.

    Cappuccino is espresso, steamed milk foam,and hot milk. Combining sugar into that and the fatty milk masks the sweet flavor to make it smooth.

    Coffee and sugar is just that.

    A similar experience can be found if you have normal ice cream vs shaved ice and flavor syrup.
