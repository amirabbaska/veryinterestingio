    covexx A:

    Yeah there are. Preconceptions about names influence our behaviour towards those people. Paired with the Pygmalion/Rosenthal-Effect you can see how names can influence our success.

    Children with names, that are connotated negatively by their teachers, will often be treated differently. To their disadvantage. The same is true the other way around with positively connotated names and advantageous treatment. This effect can also be seen in other environments, like workplaces or daily social encounters.
