    Shoboe A:

    I wanted to know about this too. I found that some women's pant sizes correspond to the waist measurement in inches minus 10 or 20. So a size 14 could actually be 34 inches around the waist and a size 8 might be 28 inches. I figured this out by directly comparing a size 14 women's jeans with a men's size 34 and also some other sizes. This doesn't explain all the different standards for different pieces of clothing but I know for at least some pants this works.

    Source: Am a fitting room sales associate at a clothing store.
