    tripperjack A:

    I just Googled this for you and I can't find it online, but if you have access to an academic library, they could either get it through their online subscriptions, or possibly special order it for you. The PEAS was published here:

    Sonstroem, R.J. (1974). Attitude testing examining certain psychological correlates of physical activity. Research Quarterly, 45, 93-103.

    Keep in mind, from what I can gather, this scale simply is a measure of the degree to which respondents rate themselves as physically fit and "attracted to" physical fitness activities, so it's not surprising that this would be a fairly good predictor of getting through a physically tough challenge like Navy Special Operations training.
