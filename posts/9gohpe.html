    Q:

    ELI5: How come if I freeze yogurt it doesn't taste the same as frozen yogurt products?

    Red_AtNight A:

    Frozen yogurt needs to be stirred as it freezes, so that air bubbles get trapped inside. That's how you get the light texture.

    If you have a home ice cream maker, you can make your own frozen yogurt with nothing other than store-bought yogurt.

    atomicdragon136 A:

    A frozen yogurt machine is constantly stirring as it is freezing (same with ice cream). So if you put yogurt in the freezer, you will get a hard ice chunk. However, the stirring in a frozen yogurt/ice cream machine causes the solids (frozen yogurt/ice cream) to separate into many tiny particles, therefore making it soft.

    cdb03b A:

    You have to stir constantly to get the proper texture, and you have to add a lot of sugar. This is because cold deadens flavor compounds and so you have to make all the flavor profiles stronger if you want them to be noticeable when frozen.
