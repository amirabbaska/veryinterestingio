    The phenomenon youre describing is your smelling senses getting "used" to the smell of the deodorant. It makes you have to use more before you notice the same intensity of it. Think of it like running a mile. After 'training' often you get used to running a mile, but 2 miles can still get you exhausted. So you train to run the 2 miles until this becomes normal, and then you'll have to run even further for the same exaustingness.
