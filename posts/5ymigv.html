    NEW
    Q:

    These things here http://imgur.com/a/MCgL1

    _Wartoaster_ A:

    Not gauges, baffles. Capillary action keeps the ink within the 'loading area' as I call it in the same way wax stays within a candle's wick.

    The majority of the ink in the pen is free-flowing in the tank, but the loading area keeps it more or less locked in there and feeds to the ballpoint at a regular rate.

    This prevents the pen from drying up or exploding, as the ink's feed rate is regulated by the baffles.

    Source: I'm a huge nerd for Pilot pens.
