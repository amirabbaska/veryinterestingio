    Q:

    ELI5: How come we get super sleepy in a hot car, but can't fall asleep if our bedrooms are hot?

    TheFirstUranium A:

    The difference is your body temperature. Hot core+hot air=miserable. Cold air+cold core= freezing. Hot air+cold core= relaxing and cold air+hot core= refreshing.

    Your body prefers to sleep with a colder core and in a warmer environment.
