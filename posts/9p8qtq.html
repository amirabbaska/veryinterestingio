    Gargatua13013 A:

    Consider the 1972 collapse of the Peruvian anchovy fishery because of overfishing and an El Nino event. Anchovetta stocks collapsed to about 10% of the previous years population.

    The impact on the Peruvian economy was immediate and devastating ... it even got the attention of the CIA at the time...
