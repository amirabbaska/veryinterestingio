  • Human Body Apéritifs and digestifs supposedly stimulate your appetite and aid in digestion, respectively. Is there any evidence/science behind this?

    NEW
    Q:

    I was reading this article on the difference between an apéritif and a digestif, and was wondering if there's any actual science behind the claims, or if it's more tied to other factors, like maybe culture?

    Prinz_von_Kirchberg A:

    Apéritifs seem like a good way to stimulate your taste buds before having a meal. Digestifs however are not necessarily useful for after a thick fatty meal. They only seem to send out signals to the brain as a muscle relaxer. Tea in contrast is much better for digestion.

    Scientists from ETH Zurich have experimented on this: http://www.bmj.com/content/341/bmj.c6731
