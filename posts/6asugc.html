    Unicyclone A:

    The things that trigger disgust reactions are usually also vectors for disease: poop, gore, vermin, and so on. Your brain is programmed to recognize them as potential contaminants - you're not only disgusted by these objects, but also by any other objects that they may have touched. 'Losing your appetite' (and, in extreme cases, vomiting) is how your brain tells you that anything found in close proximity to these things is unfit to eat.
