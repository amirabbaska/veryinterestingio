    I know its still bad to be eating junk and fast food constantly, even if you're not gaining weight from it. But, I was wondering how the food is affecting someone who is not showing any physical changes from a bad diet.

    duck_635 A:

    Have you ever heard about those people who lose weight by going on junk food diets? That's because most important factor by far in gaining/losing weight is calories in vs calories out. Everyone is supposed to have a certain amount of calories a day, the average being 2000. It can vary a lot depending on the size/age/gender of the person. When a skinny person eats and unhealthy diet and doesn't gain any weight from it, it means they are eating a small amount of food and low quality food. This would prevent them from gaining any muscle. They could actually lose muscle due to not having enough protein. They would lack energy overall, too. The most dangerous and notable effect would be malnutrition. Eating and unhealthy diet won't give you all the nutrients you need for your body to replenish itself, especially if you aren't eating enough food in the first place. Depending on how severe the diet is, your body could be affected in multiple terrible ways, such as feeling like your always sick or even a weakened immune system.
