    Lots of people got some strange answers here.

    When you film in a city, you need to apply for a permit. With this application form you need to state all relevant data. Once approved and paid for, the film crew can go about their business.

    If they are stopped by any authorities, the simply present the permit and all is good as rain.

    Authorities in the area will usually be notified although this isn't always the case.
