    Rekuna A:

    It's possibly because you didn't complete a full REM cycle (which takes around an hour or so) which is when you fully enter a dream state and happens several times in a full nights sleep.

    So you have: Stage 1 – Sleepiness

    Stage 2 – Light Sleep

    Stage 3 & 4 – Deep sleep

    REM – Rapid Eye Movement (having a dream)

    You likely woke up at Stage 2-ish and your brain was basically going "what the hell? I wanted to sleep!" It's also why (at least for me) I tend to feel tired when an alarm wakes me up despite getting plenty of sleep as opposed to just waking up naturally.

    So an ideal time for a nap is 60-70 minutes.
