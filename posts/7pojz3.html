    natha105 A:

    They don't. They feel less thirsty. If you have ever been dehydrated in a serious way it will take hours, even days, for you to "feel better". Your body actually feels terrible when it gets dehydrated. Getting thirsty on the other hand is just a handy dandy tool your brain uses to prod you to drink something and so when you have drunk something it turns off the thirsty impulse. But 99.99% of the time being thirsty is not being dehydrated.
