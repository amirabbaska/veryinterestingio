    Q:

    ELI5:Why is lifting weights more effective in muscle building than using the machines? Or is it just a myth?

    Keighlon A:

    Free weights use connecting and supporting muscles to stabilize your arms and legs and body when Using them. They give a more well round whole body work out. Machines are great for isolating muscle groups, giving you the ability to target specific muscles. So they both are good, just different. Use free weights to build overall muscle fitness. Use machines to target specific areas you want to improve.
