    davdev A:

    Despite the other posts, who are wrong, they do in fact use prop cigarettes. Traditionally they were "herbal cigarettes" that pretty much looked and burned real, but were not filled with tobacco and didn't have nicotine. Now, they use a combination of these or vapes that are designed to look real.

    The herbals still had tar and smoke and other things that weren't good to breathe, but they were non-addictive.

    I work in theater; on the stage, depending on things like actor comfort, laundry, and fire proofing requirements, we will sometimes use a real cigarette.
