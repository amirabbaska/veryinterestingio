    Aldrianson A:

    Because expiration date is actually the minimum expiration date, in Germany for example they have to write "mindestens haltbar bis" which directly translated means 'at the minimum keepable until'.

    Now about the honey, if you keep you honey dry, cool and in the dark it will probably be found in 5000 years by some archeolgist, but if you don't it will spoil, some people even do that on purpose! After 5000 years it will however not taste the same, and because the expiration date for honey is defined as how long it will keep specific properties, including taste, companies will print the earliest date it could change in taste.
