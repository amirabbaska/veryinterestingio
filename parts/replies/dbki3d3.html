    • JBenzy670

      It depends on how you and your doctor decide to go about switching you. Each case is a little bit different and more specific to the individual. It's unlikely they'll have you stop the Effexor cold-turkey and immediately put you on Prozac, so I'm inclined to say no to your question.

      If Effexor isn't working for you, or has a bunch of side effects you'd like to stop experiencing, they might try a quicker taper, but it should still be a weening process.

      You very well might notice side effects as you are tapered off and switched, everyone reacts differently, but nah you shouldn't experience withdrawal symptoms like you would if you quit cold-turkey.
