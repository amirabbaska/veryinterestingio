      • tgjer

        Pickled mushrooms can be delicious!

        And you don't have to limit yourself to vegetables either. Pickled eggs are great, especially when made with small red beats so the eggs turn a brilliant purple. I've only ever made pickled eggs with vinegar, but there are salt brine pickled eggs too.

        And corned beef! Corned beef is basically pickled beef - "corned" used to be a synonym for "grain", and the name is a reference to the grains of course salt used to make the brine.

        Basically you pickle a raw beef brisket in salt brine for 10 days (or more), then boil it. Pastrami is similar, except instead of boiling you smoke it after pickling it.
