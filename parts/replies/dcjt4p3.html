      The reflex may be autosomal dominant. Every person gets one set of genes from each parent. This means that your wife's two sets of DNA inherited from her parents both contain the recessive gene because she does not have the effect. You may be dominant/recessive if you have another child without the effect but since both of your sons have it, you could also be dominant/dominant. Both your sons with the reflex will be dominant/recessive for this trait due to your wife.

      Since it's an easily observable effect, you may be able to track it in your family.
