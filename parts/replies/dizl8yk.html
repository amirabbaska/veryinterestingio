    This is a lot of the idea of site reliability engineering. humans have a wide error range for tasks - for any complex task, you can expect 60 - 80% error rates over any significant time.

    If you build a machine to handle 90% of the cases right, and handle the remaining 10% probably right, the system and the machine will be correct in most cases. And for the remaining < 10% of all the cases, we have audit logs.

    it takes time to establish this level of trust into skynet. But once skynet reaches the critical success rate, skynet becomes our godfather.
