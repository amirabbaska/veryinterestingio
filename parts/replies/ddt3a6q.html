  • tunnelmeoutplease

    However, the main reason that company's make concentrate orange juice is that it is much cheaper to remove 70% of the volume and then transport it. After the oranges have been processed and the juice collected, thermal treatment is applied, following this around 70% of the water is boiled from the orange juice, then it is pumped into tankers and transported to the final bottling location or storage silo's. Once it gets to the final packaging and bottling location, water is added to create the orange juice you drink. Cheap non branded stuff will dilute the orange juice much more than other reputable company's which is why some brands taste like shit.

    Sauce: Dad worked as director of tanker operations for transit of ingredients (e.g. Liquid chocolate, orange juice and milk)for companies such as Cadbury's and Innocent.
