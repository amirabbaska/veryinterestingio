    The best solution is to alternate the direction you wear it each day. One day, thread it in starting with the belt loop left of your pant button/catch. The next day, start with the right. This makes belts last much longer.

    You can always tell which way to go by looking at the small hump in the middle and thread the belt in so that the hump is on the top side of the belt, not the bottom.
