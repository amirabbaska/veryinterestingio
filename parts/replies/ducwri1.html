      • ayane_m

        Depends on how large the icon appears on your screen, how bright your screen is, and how long you look at the icon.

        Since it takes energy to display an image on your screen and since calories are a unit of energy, you can calculate that number.

        Suppose you have a 1080p display, and the cake icon is 32x32px. That means the icon takes up 0.05% of your screen real estate.

        If your monitor uses 100 watts, and is at least 50% efficient, then it would take 0.025 watts to display the cake.

        If the image is displayed for a little over 46 hours, or roughly two days, then it will have used 4200 joules, or 1 Calorie.
