    These batteries can be replaced if you want to rescue an old cartridge from the trash! Unfortunately you'll lose whatever save you had before the battery died.

    There are a lot of tutorials online about how to safely open old GB/GBA cartridges, and most watch-repair stores can get you a replacement as long as you bring the original battery in so they know what size it is.

    • Triptych5998

      To add to this, if you have an original GBA you can replace cartridge batteries easily without loosing the save data. Take the cart apart and insert the board into the GBA using some cardboard to wedge it into place. Turn on the GBA and swap the battery while the power is on and then put everything back together, job done.

      Googling will get you some more in-depth guides on how it's done.
