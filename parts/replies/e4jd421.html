    There are other cases! Animal altruism and empathy has been well-documented.

    Primatologist and ethologist Frans de Waal, Director of the Living Links Center of Emory University, Atlanta wrote an interesting book on the subject, in which he argues, that "human" altruism has - and from a darwinian standpoint: must have - traceable evolutionary roots in the animal kingdom. There is much anecdodal evidence on this, but more interestingly also experimental evidence with chimps, monkeys, rats, magpies and so on.
