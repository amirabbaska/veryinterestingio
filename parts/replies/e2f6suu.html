    Interesting theory but nystagmus is not a vibrating effect. It is the eyes straying in a consistent direction and snapping back to center as your brain realizes that is not actually moving. In the case of intoxication, it has to do with specific gravity changes in the middle ear. The eyes are trying to compensate for perceived movement.
