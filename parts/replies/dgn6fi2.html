    • under_the_net

      Ali and Englis (2004), 'Quantization Methods: A Guide for Physicists and Analysts' https://arxiv.org/abs/math-ph/0405065

      Landsman (2005), 'Between Quantum and Classical' https://arxiv.org/abs/quant-ph/0506082

      Woit (unpublished draft), 'Quantum Theory, Groups and Representations: An Introduction' https://www.math.columbia.edu/~woit/QM/qmbook.pdf

      There's also a fantastic discussion of quantization in Chapter 5 of Abraham & Marsden's Foundations of Mechanics.

      There's loads of other good stuff that I'm less familiar with. I'd be interested to see others' recommendations.
