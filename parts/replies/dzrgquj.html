    The problem of overcoming the pipe head pressure on standpipes and sprinkler systems in an extremely tall building is usually dealt with by having a large main pump with additional pumps on higher floors. All things said an adequately designed sprinkler system and a building incorporating fire-resistant materials is really the best way to deal with commercial high rise fires, as most fires can be knocked down in the incipient phase by sprinklers.

    High rise residential buildings may or may not have sprinkler systems, depending on the fire code and the age of the building, but all residential highrises should have a standpipe system in place, either installed when built or retrofitted with them.
