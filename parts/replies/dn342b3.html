    • Schnozzberry_

      Charge is a measurement of how the electromagnetic force interacts. The value is a measurement of the strength of the force, so objects with higher numbers interact with more strength than those with lower numbers. Charge doesn't change with energy levels, but higher energy levels can give particles enough energy to overcome the interaction caused by charge.

      The positive/negative are kind of arbitrary terms for the fact that charge can come in two variants that change how charges interact. Two charges of the same variant, positive and positive or negative and negative will repulse each other, while two charges of opposite variant, positive and negative, will attract dach other.
