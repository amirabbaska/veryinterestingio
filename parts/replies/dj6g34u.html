    Another example of a food product that uses high pressure processing (HPP), or Pascalization as you called it, is guacamole. This is how some brands can make guacamole with absolutely no preservatives and it still doesn't oxidize or go bad. Peanut butter has also been studied as a possible candidate for this method of killing bacteria, as a potential way to prevent salmonella contamination.
