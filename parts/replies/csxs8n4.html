    A small quibble - a 'cellar' would be referred to as 'common storage' (in the trade) - 'cold storage' would refer to a refrigerated room - and then there are 'CA storages', a refrigerated room where the atmosphere is modified to lower respiration resulting in fruit being held in near 'suspended animation' - the latest advancement is the use of 1-MCP, it binds to ethylene receptor sites and prevents ethylene (a naturally occurring growth hormone produced within the apple) from precipitation certain chemical reactions, resulting in apples that age very slowly -
