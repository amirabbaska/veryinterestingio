      Basically you are asked to subsidize the development of renewable sources of energy. Building solar farms, wind farms etc requires tons of capital, so having consumers willing to pay more improves the ROI and the economic incentive to do so. It is no different than any other product: when consumers are willing to pay more because of specific attributes, a market for goods targeting those needs emerge.
