      Yup lightning is pretty messy, radio spectrum wise, and can produce some very low frequency radiation. Low frequency radiation travels around earth rather well, it turns out. So much so that the project Blitzortung uses low frequency detectors placed around the world to report timing information that is used to calculation the location of lightning around the world.
