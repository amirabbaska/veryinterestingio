  • Mdcastle

    Freedom of speech means the government can't arrest you for tweeting something racist. It doesn't apply to business transactions on private property in the course of employment, as is the case for both Roseanne and the NFL players. Similarly the right to bear arms doesn't apply on private property, so you can't take a gun into Disney World or NFL studios. Most of the Bill of Rights is protection from government, not regulating conduct of individuals or corporations. .
