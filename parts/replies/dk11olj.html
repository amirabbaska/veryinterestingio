    You can't taste capsaicin, you feel it. It binds with the temperature receptors in your mouth, not your taste buds.

    Carbonation forms a mild carbonic acid when it comes out of solution. The acid has a slight burn to it as well (usually in a very pleasant way).

    Add the two together and they accentuate each other. If you like spice, you'll enjoy this. If you don't like spice, try drinking something else.

    Sweetness decreases the burn.

    Menthol also binds with your temperature receptors.
