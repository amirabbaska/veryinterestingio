    • derminboots

      Cytokines are complex parts of the immune system. They tend to be like the ring master of a circus. They signal other immune cells to come in and can affect how strong or permeable the blood vessel wall is. In very simple terms, damage to the skin or organ --> immune system cells triggered --> release all sorts of stuff (cells to fight infection, cells to close a hole in the skin, modulate cancer cells, etc) including cytokines. Cytokines are absolutely fascinating!

      Source on cytokines and increase vascular permeability: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC2730638/
