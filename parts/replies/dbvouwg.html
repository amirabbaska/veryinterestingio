    I don't remember who said it where, nor the exact words. The original poster's idea was that our subconscious constantly weights risk and reward as a background process. So it takes the burden of rearing a child and compares it with the rewards. If found lacking, babies are automatically placed in the "disagreeable" pile of things in our mind. It does the same for the puppy and when either idea comes up, it gets compared with other related liked/disliked stuff and selects the one more preferable to the other.

    In other words, babies come with more responsibilities and personal loss than you wish to experience, while puppies don't. Therefore, you find babies to be annoying and puppies to be cute.

    Now i'm not sure how accurate this idea is and do not care enough to verify it further for you. Perhaps one day your desires and needs will change and babies will become your most important goal, but then again perhaps not. Either way, happy new year.
