    True. I posted this some where else, but I'd like to add that foods with high water content can satisfy or prevent mild thirst as long as it is truly a minor need for water. High water foods includes, fruits and vegetables, generally (Bananas and potatoes are high starch, low water so there are exceptions).

    Many foods consumed outside of fruits and veggies require more water to digest than they provide, often aiding in dehydration.
