    • ThisIsTheMilos

      Similar, it would probably be a more significant issue with dissolving sugar because sugar is so much larger.

      The polar part of both water and ethanol is around the oxygen atom. Where water has 2 small hydrogen atoms bonded to the O, ethanol has 1 hydrogen and one large ethane group. The bigger that group gets (e.g. butane/butanol, isopropane/isopropanol), the less an alcohol will dissolve something like sugar or salt.
