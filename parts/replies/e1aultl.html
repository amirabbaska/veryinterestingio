        If there are marrow transplants, specially for heme based malagencies, donor % can reach 100%, and we routinely run into cases where we have SNPs at 45-55% which are coming from just the donor even though we're testing host DNA. If the marrow transplant is not at 100%, say only 50% donor, we can see donor SNPs at a lower % as well (20-30%).
