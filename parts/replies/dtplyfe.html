• EdgyMathWhiz

    4:2:2 or 4:2:0 doesn't really affect the 'richness' of the colors - if you have a solid block of colour it won't look different if subsampled. Subsampling only reduces the ability to see fine detail in the colors.

    One of the other developments somewhat in parallel with HDR is displays that have a wider gamut, which does directly relate to producing richer colours.

    In particular, DolbyVision specifies a fairly wide gamut.
