      Jesus is the Incarnation of the Second Person of the Trinity, the Only Begotten Son of the Father. So his Father is the First Person of the Trinity, the Father.

      Also, the writers of the Gospel wrote the Gospels before the Doctrine of the Trinity (and thus, the specific understanding and language) was established.

      Also, Jesus was quoting Psalms when he said "My God, why have you forsaken me."
