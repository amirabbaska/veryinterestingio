  • Polarift

    Just want to add that for the branching model, the "proof" under the most recent formulation (2014) is interference. Things like the results of the double slit experiment is thought to be the different branches interacting, since traditionally, other universes are supposed to be beyond our causal influence. Once the wave function "collapses" or the branching occurs, then the distinct branches are no longer able to influence one another.
