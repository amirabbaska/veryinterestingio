      The bodies of the landers were made of titanium which can certainly withstand the heat and pressure, even today. Titanium could also withstand the corrosive atmosphere, but the majority of the acid is very high up and not at ground level.

      The electronics and other non-Ti components would have been destroyed or melted.

      The frames are probably still there, but everything inside is gone.
