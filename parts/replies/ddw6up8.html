    What's the evolutionary advantage of being sexually dimorphic? One would guess that a population of 100% females could bring twice as many offspring.

    • Bah-loch-eh

      The advantage of sexual dimorphism is the same advantage brought forth through any specialization within species.

      Males save a great deal of energy by not having to produce eggs and nurture them to self-sufficiency. They can then use this energy for other things, like showing their sexual prowess (and thus their fitness to mate) and protecting their mates. Just look at humans for instance. A tribe of pregnant females is unlikely to survive on its own.

      Another factor is the selfish gene theory. Simply put, it doesn't have to benefit everyone for the existence of males, it just has to benefit males. Not expending your energy/time to produce your own offspring is a direct benefit and so evolutionarily it makes sense for males to come about as opposed to hemaphrodites.
