    Fun fact - SSNs, and TINs in general are not always unique. In a data base of several million you'll likely run into several valid but not unique numbers. When you get to non individual TINs there is less uniqueness - laywers use common TINs for IOLTAs on a per state level, Girl Scouts use a common TIN per council/region.
