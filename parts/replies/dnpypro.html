      • Turksarama

        Older people, especially those in nursing homes or hospitals definitely should have stayed. An increase in background level radiation is the sort of thing which might give you a small increase in cancer risk over the next 20 years or so, and frankly many of them wouldn't live long enough to ever see negative effects and just moving them is dangerous. This is the same reason wildlife is doing fine at Chernobyl, they don't live long enough for cancer to be a big problem in their populations.

        Children on the other hand are both more susceptible to DNA damage since they have more cells dividing and will live a lot longer, so it makes sense to evacuate them immediately from an area you suspect to have high radiation until you know it's safe.
