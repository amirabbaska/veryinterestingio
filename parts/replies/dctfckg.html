    I feel like acidic fruits are very popular when it comes to things like sherberts and such. I'm surprised I don't see apple in any of those, either.

    • cocoakitties

      Oh yes, you're absolutely right about seeing more acidic fruits in sherberts! The makers would still have to do additional preparation with the apple to make it come through in sherbert due to the tartness and the dairy. It's a similar reason why you don't really see cranberry ice cream or sherbert either. You'll see them more commonly in sorbet form at restaurants. But as always, each tart fruit has to be treated a little differently because they have personalities that vary. :)

      Sour fruits are way easier to tame because all it really takes is some extra sugar. Tart fruits just need more love! In the end, if it costs more to produce then the less likely they are to produce it.
