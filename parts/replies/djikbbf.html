    • mabolle

      It's also possible your friend was talking about the specific case of gene drives, which have been suggested as a tool for this kind of purpose.

      In addition to any competitive advantage of transgenic mosquitoes, a gene drive would spread faster than normal inheritance would allow anyway, by converting heterozygote carriers into homozygotes. (Essentially, any mosquito that inherited even with a single copy of the gene drive would pass it on to all of its offspring.)
