    The volume of a sphere equals 4/3pir^3. With a radius of 1[m], the sphere equals 4/3*pi[m^3] A planck volume equals 4x10^(-105)[m^3]. That is a zero, a point, 104 zeroes and then a 4. Since the volume is between 1 and 10[m^3], you would need about 106 digits of pi.
