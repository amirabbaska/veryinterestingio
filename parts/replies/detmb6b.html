    • Zoten

      Another thing to point out is called transfer of care. You can't abandon a patient, and you can only transfer a patient to someone of equal or higher training. (A paramedic treating a patient who needs paramedic-level interventions cannot transfer the patient to an EMT-Basic. He can transfer to another paramedic, a nurse or doctor.)

      They can especially never abandon care (refusals are different). So in your scenario,the medic can't simply drive to the hospital,since there would be no-one in the back taking care of the patient.

      In theory, a firefighter could drive while one medic stays in the back and another stays at the scene, but that's usually not protocol. At the service I worked, the partners weren't allowed to be separated to avoid logistical issues.
