    • BadVogonPoet

      Generally, people don't tend to like unbiased reporting. They'd rather hear from a media entity that reports news with a similar slant as their own beliefs.

      Reuters (fd: I work for Thomson Reuters, though not in the the Reuters arm) presents a lot of long form articles that other media organizations can pull a short sound bite or more pointed written piece from. This is often easier to digest than a detail heavy report and your local CBS/NBC/FOX affiliate also has a lot of news to cram into 30 or 60 minutes which is where the majority of people get their news.
