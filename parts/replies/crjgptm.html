    The T52 and the SZ-40/42 were used for (radio)teletype traffic, and while based on a similar concept to the Enigma, in that it was electromechanical and used rotors as part of the cryptographic process, it was very different.

    The Kriegsmarine still used Enigma machines to communicate with their ships, but high command stuff would go over teletype links enciphered with T52 or SZ-40/42. Though the Kriegsmarine Enigma machines were different from the standard Heer/Luftwaffe machines (especially with regards to the M4 machines).

     

    What is very cool, is that the "first" computer, the COLOSSUS was built to crack the SZ-40/42 codes used by the OKW on their teletype links. (The Turing-Welchman Bombes not being computers in the least.)
