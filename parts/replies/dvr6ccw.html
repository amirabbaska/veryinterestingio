    Yes! When we fatigue, our muscle twitches take longer to complete.

    To maximize our torque, we fire our muscle fibers in rapid succession which allows for those twitches to summate to produce high torque.

    When we fatigue, although each muscle twitch produces less torque, we can summate the twitches at lower firing frequencies.
