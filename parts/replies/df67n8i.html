      Shower knob: < $6

      Automatic temperature adjusting shower knob: ~$71

      Average cost of a gallon of water: ~ $0.0015 (That's .15 cents, not 15 cents!)

      Average amount of water wasted, waiting for the water to get warm: 11,000 gallons anually (Based on using hot water from a "cold start", 10 times per day)

      Now we just need to do a bit of math to find the break even point.

      If you want to pay for the $65 dollar difference between the cheap shower knob and the temperature sensitive knob, you need to use:

      65/.0015 = 43,333 gallons of water.

      Recall that 11,000 gallons of water per year are wasted by the normal, cheap knob.

      43,333/11,000 = ~4 years

      This is the time it would take to save $65 at .0015 cents per gallon.
