    Is there a difference between "brown" and "white" noise? It seems like the same thing to me.

    • WattWattWatt

      Basically, brown noise is louder at lower pitches and fainter at higher pitches, while white noise is the same loudness at all frequencies.

      There are more precise technical ways to describe these things, but in the spirit of ELI5 check out the blue graphs on this wikipedia page , which do a far better job of explaining this than a text comment ever could.
