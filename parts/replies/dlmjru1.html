    • loveinthemodernera

      During a "serious" alarm, which requires the turbine to stop, the first process is slow the rotor speed by pitching the blades to its least optimal angel or denergized state.

      Once the rotor speed is low thus not making energy in the generator, the gen breaker opens and will remain in stop faulted mode until technicians or operators clear the event.

      Var control is handled mainly at the substation, so each individual turbine need not control its impedance during a power down.
