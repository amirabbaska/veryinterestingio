        They are not confusing it.

        The way you melt sugar for candy making is you add some water (it does not have to be enough to dissolve, although that's easier) and then heat it. The water boils off and you're left with melted sugar that is definitely not brown (although is usually a little yellow). If you try to melt sugar straight up, it will caramelize.
