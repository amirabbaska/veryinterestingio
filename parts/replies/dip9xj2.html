• Drink-my-koolaid

    The great political cartoonist Thomas Nast was so good at caricaturing Tammany Hall's Boss Tweed, illiterate city folk could follow what was going on. When Boss Tweed escaped prison, he fled to Spain, where a citizen recognized him from Thomas Nast's cartoons. Police were alerted, Tweed was arrested, and sent back to a New York prison for the rest of his days.
