  • ChronicallyClassy

    Reliability/safety is a huge part. They most often require redundancy in their circuits, if the primary circuit fails it needs to work but throw an error code. This makes them much more expensive and specialized/custom.

    This means they can get boxed solutions for the common multimedia functions, but they have to code proprietary software for everything specific to the vehicle.

    $25 Amazon tablet has very low reliability requirements and likely no redundancy.
