    They do not mean dust like the dust that settles on your furniture. They are talking about tea dust.

    It's the very small bits left over from the mass production of tea. It is part of the tea plant.

    In cheap teas it's probably going to add to the bitterness.

    If you want more information look up tea grades. Lower grade teas will have more dust and fannings. Higher grades will have less to none.

    So squeezing the bags can be bad because of dust. But not in the way you're thinking. It's about flavor.
