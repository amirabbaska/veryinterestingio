      There's actually a few laws involved.

      The Duty of Care Act of 1990 and Workplace Regulation of 1992 means sanitary bins are a basic requirement for female employees.

      The Water Industries Act of 1991 also states that sanitary waste should not be flushed down toilets where it can cause blockages in drains.

      This means most business make sanitary bins available in both staff and public toilets. Business generally have a contract with a waste disposal company who provide, maintain, and empty the sanitary bins. I believe incineration is the recommended way to dispose of bodily fluids and biohazards.
