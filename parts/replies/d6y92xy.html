    The issue here, as you recognize, that the number will depend on the player population.

      • erdouche

        We aren't talking about smoothing out luck as time or the number of games approaches infinity. We're talking about the luck:skill ratio. The point, which you seem to have missed as though it was your job, is that this ratio will be different for different sets of players. In other words, it is not necessarily an intrinsic property of the game, but rather is bounded by the game. The exact ratio will fall within the bounds imposed by the game, but will be dependent on the skill of the players. (Or, perhaps more intuitively, on the skill differential between the players)
