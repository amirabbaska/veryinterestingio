    Circadian rhythms again. Individuals are genetically predisposed toward waking and sleeping at certain times of the day. It may just be that your body was not built for waking up at 6am. There is also a sleep disorder called delayed sleep phase syndrome which is characterized by inability to fall asleep until after midnight or get up before late morning. So the ELI5 version: early birds and night owls are a real thing, and when you match your sleep cycle to what your body prefers, you're gold.
