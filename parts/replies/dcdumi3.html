    The problem here is that there are those who conflate "open mindedness" with "you must accept everything as true at face value," or "you cannot claim that something is false."

    Essentially they're confusing "open mindedness" with "gullibility."

    There is value to being able to accept that some questions can have multiple correct answers, even if you disagree with some of those answers for various reasons.

    But too many think that open mindedness should mean that any answer to a question must be equally as valid as any other answer.
