    I have this great version of monopoly called 'anti-monopoly'. It's basically regular monopoly however the players decide at the start of the game whether they are monopolists or concurrents (don't know the English word for it). Both player types have their benefits. For example: a monopolist can only build when he has all the streets of a city. A concurrent can build with only one street in possession but yields a lower income from it. It gives a nice extra dimension to the game of monopoly and gives a better understanding of real world dynamics.
