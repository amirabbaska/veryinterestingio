    To further complicate it refineries can service multiple crude extraction sites, and sometimes the refinery is very far from where the gas is taken from, for example Aruba's refinery for Valero, takes crude from mainland South America.

    Some gas vendors, like Costco, don't even buy from the same source nationwide, they buy from different sources depending on the area. So in Texas you might get Exon gas, and in Florida it could be Valero.
