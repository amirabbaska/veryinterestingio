    • shaiko

      There is. Black holes give off electromagnetic radiation, which is produced through pair production. The most basic way to put it, is by imagining a particle travelling along the event horizon. Then that particle splits into a particle-anti particle pair. One of those new particles falls into the black hole, and the other escapes due to quantum effects. This decreases the mass of the black hole. The radiation doesn't come from the black hole itself, but from virtual particles being pushed into existing as real particles by the black hole. If you want to know more look up Hawking Radiation.
