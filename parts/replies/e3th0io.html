    • lollersauce914

      By foundational I just mean that the idea forms the basis for the scientific method.

      Falsifiable means that the statement is testable. If I say tall people earn more money, this is a statement which we can test and either prove false or fail to prove false. If I say tall people see the color red differently from shorter people, it is not falsifiable because we have no way of testing whether or not it's true.
