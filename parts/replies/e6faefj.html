    • cheddarbroccolisoup

      It matters when determining what you can do with the paper. Water color paper is usually around 120-150 lbs because you need really thick paper to absorb all that moisture without falling apart. But if you're doing origami, you're going to want really thin paper as you can't fold thicker paper much. So paper weight gives you an idea of whether or not it's going to be suitable for your specific project.
