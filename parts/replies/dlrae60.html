    • voxanimus

      It's also how older thermostats worked. Two metals with different thermal expansion rates were glued together to make a bimetallic strip that curls up and down in response to the ambient temperature. This can then be hooked up to a circuit such that it turns on when the temperature exceeds or falls below a certain value.
