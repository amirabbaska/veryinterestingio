    Freezing any food harms it.

    Freezer burn is water that was pulled from the item in question.

    The other major issue is freezing cells. Are you familiar with frostbite? Same thing happens to your steak. Ice forms in cells, rupturing them. That is why frozen vegetables are so 'mushy'.
