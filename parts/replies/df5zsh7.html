    Fake smiles also show in a difference of the ring muscle tension around the eyes.

    Apart from that, the size change of the pupil can reflect emotional responses as well. Sexual arousal widens the pupil, for example.

    The fact that the pupil changes its size (or appears to) is also often used in animated films, where e.g. “sad” or “dreamy” eyes have huge pupils:
