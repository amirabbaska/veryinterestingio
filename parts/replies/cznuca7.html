      The Pegasus can only carry small payloads, with a max of 433 kg to LEO. For context, that's less than 4% the mass of the Hubble Space Telescope. It would be possible to scale this concept up, but only to a certain point. It wouldn't be effective to launch any large or manned payloads like this.

      Side note: both the North American X-15 and Scaled Composites's SpaceShipOne (and SpaceShipTwo, if all goes well) achieved manned suborbital spaceflight with a launch like this, but could attain nowhere near the velocity required to achieve orbit.
