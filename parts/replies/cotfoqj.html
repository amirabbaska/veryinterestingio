      Keep in mind (for english at least), it's not just the 'ee's' we stress. Think about a mother saying, 'can you say 'Maaah-mah'? Can you say 'DAH-Dee'. The 'mah' 'bah' 'mo' 'ah' are all fairly common phonemes in English on top of the 'ee's'. We're cueing babies in on what to listen for by talking to them like that. Even before they're born, they're listening to people talk all around them while they're in the womb.
