    A hot air ballon does not work on lift. Lift is a result of Newton’s third law of motion e.g. air is pushed down by a helicopter’s blades and the air pushes the helicopter up as a result.

    A hot air ballon works on buoyant force created by the displacement of air. The hot air inside the ballon is displacing colder air. This colder air is denser than the warm air hence the ballon will rise until the weight of the ballon+basket+contents+warm air inside ballon=cold air displaced by the ballon

    Actually, if you really wanted to know the exact altitude the hot air ballon will rise you will also have to account for the volume of cold air displaced by everything being carried by the ballon like the basket, etc.
