    I always have to chuckle at the false sense of security we get when fast food workers are wearing gloves. They're not protecting us from their germs; essentially they're protecting their hands from germs because many of them rarely change gloves especially if they have a lazy shift manager.

    I once saw a worker wipe the sweat from her brow using her gloved hand (the finger side, not the back of her glove) and continue preparing my food. When I asked for her to remake my food because of what I saw, she sighed annoyingly as if she was put out by my request.
