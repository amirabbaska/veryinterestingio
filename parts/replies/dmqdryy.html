      • PeabnutBubber

        I think you've got it backwards there. The blue area is the oldest seafloor that is about to be subducted under the less dense continental crust. New oceanic crust is made at the mid-ocean ridges, which are the blue lines running through the white areas. The mid ocean ridges are the edges of the tectonic plates that are moving away from each other. Magma is constantly rising and filling in the gaps with new crust.
