    Do you have a source for that last claim? I don't think there is any evidence of dental xrays being associated with increased risk of brain cancer. All of the major international organisations' (BEIR, UNSCEAR, etc.) guidelines suggest that there isn't any solid evidence for increased cancer risk for doses under 100 mSv. The dose from a full dental xray exam is in the order of 100 uSv. They do, however, recommend the use of the Linear No Threshold model in order to remain conservative for radiation protection purposes.
