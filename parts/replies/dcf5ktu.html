  • nagurski03

    Robertson (square) bits actually came before Philips (cross). Ford realized that using Robertson bits instead of flat head, would save them hours of time on each car built. Ford tried to license the design from Robertson but Robertson refused. Philips was perfectly willing to cooperate with Ford and so that is the one that really took off in the US.
