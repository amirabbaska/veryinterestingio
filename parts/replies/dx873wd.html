      IIRC, POTS is Postural Orthostatic Tachycardia Syndrome - the two are related but not the same thing. POTS refers to an increase in heart rate that results from the drop in blood pressure.

      EDIT: Apparently it tends to be sustained, ie most people experience postural orthostatic hypotension from time to time due to the sudden change in BP, but not everyone's response is severe enough to be considered POTS.
