    • MealReadytoEat_

      Quartz can be grown relatively cheaply via hydrothermal synthesis, significantly cheaper than through epitaxy; have some long, pressurized chamber with a bunch of silica sand on one end and place a seed crystal in the other, heat the sand side and because quartz is less soluble at the unheated side, the seed crystal will grow.
