      • vilhelm_s

        For the most part, but there has been at least one secret stealth satelite. Prowler was launched in 1990; since a rocket launch itself cannot be hidden, it was put as a secret second satellite on a military space shuttle launch. It apparently used radar and optical stealth features (details yet unknown), then in 1998 it became visible (probably it ran out of fuel and started to tumble), so it was spotted by amateur astronomers as a new unknown object. Around 2010 people were able to put the pieces together and realize that was launched by that space shuttle mission.
