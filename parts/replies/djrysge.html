  • craftingwood

    Also related is kinesthesia, which is your brain's sense of your body's motion without seeing it (whereas proprioception is static position). Kinesthesia and proprioception together allow for muscle memory (knowing the current position and desired of your body parts and sensing and actively, but subconsciously, controlling the motion from one to the other.
