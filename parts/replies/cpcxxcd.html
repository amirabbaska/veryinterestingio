      The ring occurs when the lens (the gravitational source) is symmetric about an axis, and if there is perfect alignment of the viewer, lens, and light source. A spherical lens and a sliiightly little misalignment produces two images. Generic shapes, e.g. ellipsoids, produces more than two.
