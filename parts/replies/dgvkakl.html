    Other harms:

    • Overdoses caused by lack of knowledge on how to use drugs safely
    • Overdoses and other illnesses caused by drug impurities
    • Violence from resolving disputes over drugs
    • Violence from people on drugs which promote violent behavior and bad decision-making

    The first can helped with education and making drugs legal with mandatory dosage and safety information.

    The second can be reduced by regulating drug purity and allowing the legal system to prosecute people for providing drugs that cause harm.

    The third can be reduced by providing access to the courts to resolve disputes. Now, the only option available is violence.

    The fourth can be helped by education on the effects at improper dosages.
