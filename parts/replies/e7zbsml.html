    Smart phone advancements. RAM capacity makes a huge marketing headline for a new flagship.

    So flagship phones all went from 3-4GB of DDR4 to 6GB of DDR4. The Note 9 has 8GB of DDR4.

    Mid tier phones went from ~2GB of DDR3, to ~3GB of DDR4 this year. Even the Samsung J6+ coming out right now has 4GB of RAM.

    So while total shipments of smart phones for the year grew only at single digit % when SSD makers expected +10% or more, the actual demand for DDR4 from smart phones increased by >50%.
