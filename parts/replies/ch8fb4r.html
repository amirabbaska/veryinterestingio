    New Horizons

    Just to be clear, though, New Horizons is a much, much cheaper mission as it's part of the New Frontiers program while the Voyagers were part of the Flagship program. For comparison, New Horizons will cost somewhere in the neighborhood of $700 million over the life of the mission; the Voyagers were about $1 billion in 1977 dollars, which is closer to $4 billion today.

    Point being, New Horizons is very, very scaled back from what it could have been if it were a true Flagship mission.
