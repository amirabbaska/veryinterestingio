    All of the interchange rates can be negotiated by the merchant. So for example, Walmart has a lot lower fee that your local mom and pop's store since they have a lot of leverage.

    Interchange fees are not negotiable. Even companies like Walmart have set interchange fees, it's tiered.

    The markup that the processor charges on top of interchange is what's negotiable.
