      The fans are wrong, though it's not really their fault. Years of sportscasters saying things without having a clue how it works has made this a "fact" about special teams. Nothing changes from one kick to another no matter how far away it is.

A big reason for this is that the kicker then has a cushion. If he doesn't hit a perfect ball it'll still probably have enough distance to get through.
