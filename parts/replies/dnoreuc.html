    • wanna_be_doc

      Most solitary liver cysts are benign and a fairly common finding on abdominal CT. They are not a cancer risk.

      If you have multiple cysts, then we may look for other causes. If you're lived in or recently travelled to a developing country, we may consider a parasitic infection. We also may consider parasites if you have a compromised immune system (e.g. positive history of HIV). In the developed world, finding multiple cysts may be a sign that cancer in another part of the body has metastasized to the liver.

      If you've been diagnosed with a cyst in the passed and your doctors cleared you, then I wouldn't worry too much about it.
