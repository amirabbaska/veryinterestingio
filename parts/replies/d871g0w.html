      Yes, there are a lot of different factors that make up the character of a sound. Tone, timbre, and dynamics are the fundamentals but these are often discussed in different nuanced ways and sometimes interchangeably. Tone does not vary from inner to perceived voice, period (assuming no crazy things like Doppler effect). Timbre and dynamics can change considerably from recording to recording, through the "inner" perceived voice, etc.
