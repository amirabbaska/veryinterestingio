    • JCAPS766

      They wanted to test the devices as weapons.

      They knew that the atomic bomb was a huge future superweapon, but they still didn't really understand how well it would work.

      The first nuclear test was with an implosion-type plutonium device. A similar device was housed in Fat Man. The implosion-type device was far more intricate, and so they decided that if they were only gonna have one test, this is the one they would do.

      Little Boy was comparatively simple. It was essentially a cannon that fired a uranium slug into another piece of uranium in order to make the uranium reach a critical mass. This is as opposed to the implosion-type device, which needed to be detonated so that the uranium collapsed into the exact needed density at the exact right moment.
