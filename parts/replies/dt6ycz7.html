    Don't confuse the electrons and the electric field and energy transfer.

    I've seen this question several times. Most people work under the assumption that the electrons actually move from the switch to the bulb, like tiny balls that need to make the their way to the bulb.

    As you say, it is not the electrons, but the electromagnetic field the one that carries the energy in an electric circuit. It is a difficult concept to grasp when you are just starting to study electromagnetic field theory.
