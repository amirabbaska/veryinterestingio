      • TotallyNotNew

        That's absolutely not true.

        Primary blast injuries are caused by the over-pressure wave of the explosion. Secondary blast injuries are fragmentation injuries from "shrapnel." Tertiary blast injuries are blunt trauma secondary to a body being physically propelled into an object or the ground. Quaternary blast injuries are all other associated injuries (thermal, inhalation, etc.)
