    The term emotional response can also be a bit misleading. Modern pain science certainly addresses non purely physiological processes, but rather than just emotional, it's more the brain weighing a whole lot of factors (previous experience, underlying knowledge, current context and yes, individual emotional response to pain). Because it's all happening at the pre-conscious level, it's very difficult to consciously change.
