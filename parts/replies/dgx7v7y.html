    I would like to add on the pure water is not exactly hydrating. Your intestines need sodium to facilitate absorbing water. This is especially important when you have diarrhea and try to rehydrate by drinking water only.

    • Maiscornngo

      Great comment. That's one of the reasons why tap/bottled water is never electrolyte free.

      The best way to treat the symptoms of diarrhea is to drink a solution high in sodium and glucose (glucose increases sodium uptake). You can buy an Oral Rehydration Solution (ORS), or make one yourself.
