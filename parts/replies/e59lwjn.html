    The issue with this explanation is that the bulk liquid also experiences a higher pressure. Even if you were able to raise the gas phase pressure, say by gasketing and valving, boiling will stop temporarily until the liquid phase heats up sufficiently to have the vapor pressure equal to that of the gas phase pressure, and then you will get the same bubble sizes again.
