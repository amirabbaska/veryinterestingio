  • Xeneron

    There is science behind it! Because your stomach sits in the left side of your abdomen, laying on your left side (left lateral, or recovery position) causes gravity to pull the contents of your stomach away from the esophagus and reduce the feeling of reflux, reducing nausea. That's why if you have someone who's drunk and you're afraid might vomit/asphyxiate, you put them on their left side so that you reduce the chance of vomiting, and the vomit will not go back into their airway.
