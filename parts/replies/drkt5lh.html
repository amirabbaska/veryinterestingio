    Also note that it MAY be illegal for Twitter to do what it is doing.

    Twitter is based in San Francisco, and in California, political affiliation IS a protected class.

    If Twitter is exhibiting a pattern of discrimination against people based on their politics, it is probably breaking California law.

    So you can say "I'm only kicking people out when they do something offensive", but if every person you kick of for "doing something offensive" is black, you have committed a crime.
