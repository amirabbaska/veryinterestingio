    • cbrown1311

      That's actually a different muscle called the stapedius, which instead of contracting on the eustachian tube, contracts to pull one of the bones from your middle ear, called your stapes away from the oval window to dampen sounds that would otherwise damage your cochlear cells. Fun fact, this muscle and bone pair are the smallest muscle and bone in your body.
