      Why it always looked blue is the part I really wanted to know, and the part I missed

      I think that's down to the relative colour temperature of the TV. CRT/LCDs tend to have a high colour temperature (making the light "bluer") but at night we're usually surrounded by artificial light with a lower colour temperature ("yellower") which makes the blue tinge even more noticeable. Somewhat confusingly we perceive higher colour temperatures as cooler and lower temperatures as warmer ("warm white" bulbs have a lower colour temperature than pure white bulbs!)
