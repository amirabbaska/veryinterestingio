    Typically for gas planets, astrophysicists will use the level where the gas pressure is equal to 1 bar, or 100kPa. This is roughly equal to the average atmospheric pressure on Earth at sea level.

    Like the definition used for the sun, it's kind of arbitrary, but you have to define a "surface" somewhere or else the language you use to describe things gets very difficult.
