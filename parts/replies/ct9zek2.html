    • Tufflaw

      The judges I appear in front of would release you, but I'm not in Dutchess County.

      Ultimately, the questions is - would the hardship you described affect your ability to be a fair and impartial juror? That is, if selected for the jury despite your reasons for wanting to get off, would you be able to focus on the trial and the evidence presented, or would you be worrying about your students, your job, your business, your family, etc.

      And bear in mind, some trials last only a day or two. You'll know right from the start how long it's expected to last, the judge should state that before asking who has a hardship.
