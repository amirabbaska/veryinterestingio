    Hello fellow MCO.

    I'm in community TV so our standards are around -12dB for everything. We don't air commercials but most of the PSAs we receive are in that ballpark. Though some shows come in only at -20. Each of our stations seems to have a different way of doing things. So yes, it's often the case of the show being too quiet rather than the commercial being too loud. But it's all relative as far as the viewer is concerned. Unfortunately (Gr)ass Valley broke our ability to adjust audio levels on the fly in the last update for our automation software.
