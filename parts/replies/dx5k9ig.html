    Proof of Aspiration you can Try At Home

    If you are one of those people who can't hear it, well, I can't help you. Not unless you learn a language that requires you to learn to listen for it. I know it's there and still can't tell by listening.

    But I can prove we have both 'p' s.

    Stand in a room with no fan or moving air. Light a match, hold it close in front of your lips, and say Spot. Normally, like you always do. Don't try to ensure you pronounce it right, or say it slow. Just say it like normal. Then say it louder. And then louder. You'll be pretty loud before you see that flame flicker, and it's from more air to sustain the volume.

    Now light another match. Hold it close in front of your lips. Now whisper, quiet mind you, the word 'pot'. Watch that flame dance. You might even have put it out.

    That is aspiration in action. And if you're like me, you're either astonished, or pissed off that after ten years of knowing thus you still can't fecking hear the fecking thing.
