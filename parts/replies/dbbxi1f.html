    • KnightHawkShake

      Good to hear stories like yours. However, the vast majority of back pain tends to resolve on its own over time. It's likely you would have improved just as much without the acupuncture. Additionally, there's a very good chance the scoliosis on the XRAY didn't have anything to do with your pain. Lots of people have abnormalities on XR. Often it is attributed to abnormalities found on plain film which have nothing to do with the cause. Problem is more likely muscular or ligamentous or even disk related, which wouldn't show up on plain films. Important to keep an open mind on that, too.
