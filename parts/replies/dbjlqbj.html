    Yeah, it is crazy how fast your brain adapts to using different increments. Working in crafts (metalwork) made me think in millimeters for almost anything (besides 'long' distance).

    Meters become 1000 millimetres, centimetres become 10 millimetres and sometimes I might have once labled as about 25 centimetres becomes 250 millimeters. I just don't think in larger units anymore, just like I didn't think in a small a unit as millimeters before.

    I am sure that if I worked with something like gigameters for a while my brain would switch around because of the ease of labeling.
