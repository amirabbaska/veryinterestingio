    • JustMeRC

      "Forget" is being used loosely in this instance. The autonomic system regulates breathing, but it's still a system of communication among many areas of the nervous system in conjunction with muscles, etc. There are lots of opportunities for interruptions in signal sending/processing, which can degrade the pathways of communication, thereby "unwiring" or "rewiring" the autonomic system. So, in essence, the body can "forget" how to breathe, if the system is somehow degraded.
