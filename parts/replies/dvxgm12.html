    What’s the benefit of IO over IV?

    • Snake_Staff_and_Star

      The circulation system in a bone can't collapse due to low blood pressure like a surface vein can and therefore can be obtained in any situation where blood pressure is compromised. It is particularly common in trauma and heart attack scenarios.
