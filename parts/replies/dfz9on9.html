    I suspect the efficacy of a given ejaculate is partially influenced by the peak force with which it is propelled towards the cervix. It is energetically demanding and probably provides no benefit for the entire volume of semen to be expelled at the 'peak force'. Therefore a system has evolved which ejaculates in spurts rather than streams.

    By comparison organisms are selected to expel urine more quickly as well as energy efficiently. Thus there is pressure to select for a stable rate of micturition rather than wasting energy on peak flow rate.
