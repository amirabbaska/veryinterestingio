    I'm not sure what point you are trying to make. The internet is by and large a download heavy format. The vast majority of everything you do online will be in the form of downloading content, with very little uploading. This is why you dont get/need much upstream bandwidth, because for the most part uploading is really just used to confirm what you downloaded from whatever server you are being fed from. If ISP's were you offer symmetrical download/upload rates by default for every single user then they'd have to further improve upon their infrastructure to support it, something they dont care to do since running servers is not something commonplace for their users.
