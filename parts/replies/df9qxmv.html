      • esqueletohrs

        Loperamide is actively transported out of the endothelial cells that make up the BBB by a drug efflux pump (P-gp). A much easier way to get loperamide across the BBB should you care to do so would be to take a P-gp inhibitor at the same time as loperamide.

        This is the reasons the government listed it as Schedule II when it was first released in the mid-70s. The government de-scheduled it in 1982 when they realised that there were much less shitty ways to get opioid highs.
