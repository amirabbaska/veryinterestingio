    • Lukinfucas

      Spinal/Epidural Anesthesia can lessen contractions. General anesthesia can halt labor due to muscle relaxing effects of medicine. If it’s a true emergency than you’re talking a matter of minutes before the baby is out. After the baby is out of the uterus and the placenta has been removed, we start a drug called Pitocin (USA anyways) that actually stimulates contractions forcefully to help shrink the uterus and decrease bleeding.
