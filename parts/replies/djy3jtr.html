    My favorite is this.

    If you have a long list of things you want to get done but wind up not doing them day after day. Just go and do one short task; something that takes 5 minutes or so. Don't have any requirements to do anything besides that short task.

    By taking this approach, one of two things happen. You either get 5 minutes worth of work done that you otherwise would not have done or you keep working past the 5 minutes and get more done.

    You can commonly break big tasks down into smaller ones too:

    • A giant pile of dishes that are so precariously stacked that OSHA would shut you down? Go clean, like, 5 dishes.

    • Need to vacuum the floor or be fully engulfed by pet hair? Shittly vacuum one room.

    • Laundry getting out of hand and you are tired of buying replacement clothes instead of cleaning them? Just toss a load in.

    Just make your arms and legs do shit until something you want is done.
