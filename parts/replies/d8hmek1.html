    Just a slight correction on this great comment - Nature's materials are not built perfectly. There are flaws/defects at multiple scales just like engineering materials. However, they are high performing because Nature has developed micro structures that are extremely flaw tolerant. This is seen in silks, nacre, bone, etc. We can actually replicate spider protein sequences quite accurately... We just can't assemble them in the right structure.
