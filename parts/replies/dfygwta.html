    The message is fear, and fear can be used to control people.

    Let's say they bomb a subway. Now, there are a lot of subways in the world, so a person's chances of being on the next subway they bomb are very, very small. However, many people aren't going to want to take that chance. So people stop taking the subway, which means more traffic, which then hurts the economy as people have trouble getting to and from work or other destinations.

    It's not just about the bombing, it's about what all the frightened people do after the bombing. That's what people mean when they say things like "If we do X the terrorists win." If they bomb a subway and you stop using the subway as a result, you've given them what they want.
