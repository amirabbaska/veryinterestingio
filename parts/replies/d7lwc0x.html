      • WildZontar

        The scientific definition of epigenetics is currently in flux, however it is moving towards (semi) permanent (semi) heritable changes in DNA structure which occur in a manner besides actual base-pair mutation. Things like methylation patterns and histone/chromatin structure are absolutely epigenetics.

        Stochasticity affecting early development by subtly changing developmental trajectories without actually affecting the structure of DNA is not really considered to be epigenetics within molecular biology. Neither is changes in gene regulation which are transient and due to regulatory elements interacting with environmental signals.
