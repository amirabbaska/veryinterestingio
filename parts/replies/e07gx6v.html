    Following up on this, the charge speed issue is one of the reasons various groups have been working to improve capacitors for use as primary power sources in electronic devices. A capacitor stores electric charge rather than storing energy chemically, so it can be charged very quickly. Unfortunately, capacitors don't store nearly as much energy in the same size/weight as high density batteries, so your phone would need to be much larger to use capacitors as a power source.
