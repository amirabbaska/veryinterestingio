    Stercobilin (from stercobilinogen) is actually the waste product excreted to make our turds brown and it is formed from half of our gut’s urobilinogen that yup was a product of the natural elimination of old red blood cells.

    But the other half of urobilinogen is reabsorbed into the blood and then excreted (sent to the bladder) by the kidneys in the form of urobilin and is responsible for the yellow color of our urine.

    So it’s pretty fun to think that our poop and pee are both based on the same color (brown is like dark yellow? but I’m no color pro) for that reason.
