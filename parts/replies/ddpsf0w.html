      What you are talking about is called blacking in, and it happens if you're awake long enough to outlast the dunkest part of your night. In other words, sobering up a little before falling asleep.

      It's not an instantaneous moment, not even a little bit. It's more like you've just been grooving and riding the drunk and then you start to wonder why it's so late, and you talk with people about earlier parts of the night that you were there for but have absolutely zero memory of doing. Then comes the realization that you were blacked out for a couple hours earlier.

      Last time this happened was a couple months back. Around 4am, when I was laying on the couch with my girlfriend after a night out drinking, we were talking about all the things we did that night.
