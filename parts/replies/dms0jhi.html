    Be careful. "Weight increases twice as quickly as strength" isn't quite right. If you enlarge something with a linear scale factor of 3, then its volume increases by a factor of 27 while its cross-sectional area increases by a factor of 9. Notice 27/9 = 3, and not 2. So "Weight increases three times more quickly than strength" in this case.
