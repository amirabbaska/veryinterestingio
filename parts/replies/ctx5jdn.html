    • shadowthunder

      It's worth noting that you lose out on a ton by having a local account instead of a Microsoft account:

      cross-device syncing of settings and information (super helpful if you have multiple Windows devices)
       Cortana
      app store access (there are at least a few worthwhile apps at the moment)
       OneDrive file syncing
      * even more stuff that hasn't been announced yet for a Fall update

      It's like not having a Microsoft/Google/Apple account on your phone - I simply don't think it makes sense if you want an OS to act in a modern manner. Of course, if you don't want any of those things, feel free to turn them off and use a local account, but then the OS will feel much more incremental than it actually is.
