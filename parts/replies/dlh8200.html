    Because mom and pop are constant and straightforward whereas there are a million Johns, Jacks, etc...

  • mister-rik

    Relationships between people were more important in the past to identify who someone is. E.g. 'I am Steven, Son of Roland of Warwick'. There's also the whole Scandinavian thing of your surname being your father's name + 'son' (E.g. Ericsson). Makes sense to call someone by the relationship they are to you rather than a name tag that doesn't mean anything.
