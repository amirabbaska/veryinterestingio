    • Plaetean

      Not all the information, with one detector you are still massively limited in terms of source direction. To see this, imagine our 3 armed detector in space. If you imagine a GW event in the exact direction of one of the arms, you would have no way of telling whether this event happened above or below your detector (because one of your arms is redundant in this case). And you would still have degeneracies between polarisation and position, and even certain polarisation and direction combinations would pass entirely through the detector without triggering a signal.
