    As someone who works in phone repair there's a few possible reasons.

    From most to least likely (note it can be more than one)

    Cable damage from improper use. Damaged/worn/dirty charging port. Bad power block (the thing between the wall and the cord). Bad battery. Bad Charging IC chip (depending on iPhone model, this can be more or less likely).

    Those are the most common scenarios I see.
