      Yes. Although they take care to avoid potential dangers like reefs, wrecks or uneven land. There isn't much in the sea to worry about other than man-made activities. (although Sharks like to munch on the cables sometimes)

      Yes and no.

      Special casings, rubber(?) sort of, partly. All casings are "special" but it's not just rubber.

      Deep sea cables are thinner than shallow and they are all generally wrapped in wire mesh and non-permeable protective coatings, but it's not like they are bomb proof or anything.
