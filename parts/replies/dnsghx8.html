    The two tests you mentioned are actually testing two different visual functions though:

    Retinoscopy is a measure of refractive error (glasses prescription strength).

    Teller Acuity cards are used to measure visual acuity (i.e to what level of fine detail you are able to distinguish- equivalent to logMAR or Snellen visual acuity in older patients)

    Both are possible in babies 6 weeks old and above

    Source: Orthoptist
