  • morphinapg

    Basically, "species" is more of a comparative term. You say something is the same species if it's close enough to one thing, but if it's not close enough you say it's not the same species. Keep doing this and eventually you start getting some classification of species, but it's not perfect as there can still be the blending between. Usually over time the in-between things die out so it's less of an issue, but occasionally you still have stuff like ring species that make the idea of species less about an objective specific definition of one thing, and more about the comparisons between two things.
