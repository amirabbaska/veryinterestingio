    • Snatch_Pastry

      "In chemistry isomerization (also isomerisation) is the process by which one molecule is transformed into another molecule which has exactly the same atoms, but the atoms have a different arrangement"

      Short version, you have chicken eggs in your eye. The light fries the eggs. When your body senses the fried eggs, it understands that it's interacting with light. But in order to know about more light, your body has to un-cook the egg back to raw, so that it can be fried again.
