      I don't know if interest is allowed.

      The amount wouldn't be counted towards net worth. As the card has not been redeemed, the money "technically" doesn't belong to the store yet. Think of them more of a custodian of the cash until the customer makes a purchase decision. The money only gets to enter their revenue (and count towards their net worth) when they make the sale.

      But of course the business would be aware of this outstanding balance, and if they've been around for a few years then they can use it to help forecast future sales.
