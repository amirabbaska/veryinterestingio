    • Kotama

      When creating viruses designed to "leak", the number one rule is always "better safe than sorry." The people who create these sorts of things do so in safe environments with no outside connections and no wireless communication. If those are the types of equipment that need tested, then they're often faked. badBIOS is one such virus that got out of hand, but it's been isolated and contained. It can jump from Linux to OSX to Windows very easily, and can even be transmitted through speaker/microphone set ups. It can even jump air gaps. Reportedly.
