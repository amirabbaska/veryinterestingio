        You don't even need to drill down that far if you drill the holes into a subducting continental plate, like off the coast of the Pacific Northwest. The movement of the plate will slowly carry the waste way down into the mantle. It would take about 10 million years before any of the disposate reappeared in inland volcanos, by which time essentially all the radioactivity would have decayed, if there are any humans around to care.
