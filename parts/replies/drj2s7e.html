    In the early 1970s Volkswagen marketed in North America what they promoted as an "Automatic" they got sued because it was NOT a true automatic Instead it simply used a very small torque converter to do nothing more than work the clutch, you still had to manually flip it from one gear to the next.

    This resulted in them being label with the oxymoron "Automatic Stickshift" with "Automatic" in block letters and "Stickshift" in Cursive

    So the concept iof using a small torque converter to simply operate a clutch and do nothing else
