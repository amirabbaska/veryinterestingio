      Basically gravity isn't instantaneous so waves are the changes in the gravitational field propagating.

      E.G. If the sun were to suddenly disappear, under newton's theory the earth would immediately fly off into space but under relativity, we wouldn't know the sun had disappeared until about 7 minutes later, when the last of its light reaches us, and when the resulting gravitational wave reaches us.
