        As far as energy costs, my point is that metabolically costly traits (like venom production) are costly whether you're an endotherm or ectotherm. It's true that there are fewer venomous endotherms (birds, mammals, some fishes) than ectotherms (all other life), but the trends governing venom evolution (or lack thereof) in mammals and birds are complicated, multi-faceted, and different for different groups.
