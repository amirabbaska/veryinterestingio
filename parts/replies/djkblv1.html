      Shepard–Risset glissando What am I supposed to hear?

      That ooooooo should sound like it's getting lower and lower pitched (other variations sound like it's getting higher and higher pitched). The trick to it is that it keeps repeating. It's often referred to as the auditory equivalent to a barber's poll (that spinning poll outside a barber shop where it looks like the stripes are moving up or down, but in reality they're just spinning). If you keep playing it for a long time and think of how much lower you think it should have gotten. But then start it over and realize it hasn't gotten lower.
