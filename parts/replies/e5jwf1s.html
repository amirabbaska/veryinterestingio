        To add to this, even if you take out all the biological differences between mice and humans (different proteins), you'll still be left with dosing/metabolism issues. Drugs can have different effects at different concentrations, and it's difficult to tell what the effective dose conversion from a mouse to humans. You can scale drugs by body mass to administer doses, but this doesn't account for differences in how fast the drug is processed between humans and mice.
