      • fermiondensity

        We did exactly this as an experiment of measuring air pressure with high-school kids who were visiting our faculty. Basically the Toricelli experiment times 10. It does actually happen, but one thing you have to consider is that degassing of whatever gases are disolved in the water will also happen as you climb up, so the actual boiling might not start after (ideally) 10.3 m. Boiling the water on stove prior to the experiment might help a bit with lowering the amount of gases present.
