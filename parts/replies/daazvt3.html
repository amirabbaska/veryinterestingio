    • ElMachoGrande

      Modern electronics are designed to withstand that interference.

      The main reasons they don't allow active cell phones on aircraft are:

      • The same reason many airlines don't have a row with the number 13. It's a non-problem, but it makes people nervous.

      • On lower altitudes, the cellphones will connect to a large number of base stations, and switch between them rapidly, causing a large load on the mobile network. Nowadays, many aircraft have their own base station, for improved service, and then transmits all the phone traffic to the gorund through one channel, so it's less of a problem.
