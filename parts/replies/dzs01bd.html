    • Nurio

      When I use the term colored noise, I am referring to the actual classification of noises, which use colors:

      https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Colors_of_noise

      (I'm using this term a bit colloquially, because I think these terms refer to actual signal noises, instead of something we consider noise, like background murmurs, rain, or thunder)

      How pleasant a noise is has to do with the contrast between lower and higher tones. If something switches from low to high quickly and back again, it's often perceived as annoying. Especially when you have several of these patterns all mixed together in one noise.
