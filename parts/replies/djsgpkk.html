    • camokittyy

      Temaril P has a steroid in it; I would not use that long term if it was my personal pet. Far too many long term side effects

      Apoquel isn't a steroid. It can have immune-modulating effects so it isn't recommended to dogs undergoing cancer treatment or with immune-mediated disorders. I haven't seen much in the way of side effects from it.

      Cytopoint is the safer option, side effect wise. But in my opinion, it doesn't work as well.

      My work was involved with the clinical studies of both Apoquel and Cytopoint. We have been using Apoquel for many many years and have had no issues with side effects. There is a lot of scary stuff out there about Apoquel, but we have not experienced any of that.
