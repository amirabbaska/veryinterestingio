    • spanischnuessli

      you used the formula the wrong way round.

      The 2068/2142 sec are actually what earth/jupiter/outsider measure. So ship A arrives first.

      Now you solve for the other T (multiply by gamma instead of dividing), and you get the times measured on the ship. In words: "The passengers will experience the flight as much shorter than it actually was". Because ship A is faster, the effect is stronger, making the flight even 'more shorter' than before.

      A: 524 s

      B: 766 s

      note: this calculator is meant to be used for the following scenario: on earth we play a game of soccer (takes 90 minutes). jebediah watches the game from his spaceship and on his clock it took 200 minutes as his clock is ticking faster.
