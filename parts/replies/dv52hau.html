    That's not why they are shaped like that. They wanted to address the problem of customer complaints of broken, greasy, and stale chips in the industry and came up with the shape while using dough so they could stack them. That way they wouldn't be broken and the shape actually makes them more crispy. The dough helped keep them from being greasy.
