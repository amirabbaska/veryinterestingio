    I should point out, one exception to the cold feet/cold body relationship you're describing is when there are circulation problems- sometimes feet are cold (and or numb) because not enough blood is getting to them. This can abruptly turn from mere discomfort to a very serious problem very quickly if you have some kind of progressive circulation problem, like arterial disease or diabetes.
