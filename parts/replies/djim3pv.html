    Since N+1 has to have a prime factor greater than p_n, you could just check each number between p_n and N+1 since at least one of them is a prime. But in fact there is always a prime between p_n and 2p_n. On average the distance from any number n to the next prime is about log n, so if you want to find primes, just checking consecutive numbers until you find one is relatively quick.
