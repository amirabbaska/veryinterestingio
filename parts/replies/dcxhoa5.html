    A college course I took called organisational behaviour covered this. Using a questionaire, the class was split into 3 groups. Most people could identify primarily as a starter, a mover, or a finisher.

    Starters would do just that. Start. Unlikely to finish, or move the project along they were great at starting a project.

    Movers wouldn't start a project. Or finish it off, but we're great at inproving a project, advancing it significantly

    Finishers didn't do to much starting, and might not do to much of the middle work, but shined at 'putting the finishing touches' on a project.
