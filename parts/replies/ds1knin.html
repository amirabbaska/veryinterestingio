    • Duq1337

      The mass doesn’t change when the drone flies. If the drone is hovering, it’s exerting a force (lift) upwards equal to its weight. The air displaced by the propellers therefore has a downforce equal to the weight downwards, which causes the weight of the truck to remain the same. When the drone initially accelerates, there will be momentary fluctuations of course, but when deceleration happens the fluctuations will happen in the opposite direction and average out to the initial weight. This is because the truck is a closed system.
