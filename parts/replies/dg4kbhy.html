    Yes. This is the same reason where if you simultaneously drop a hammer and a feather at the same height on the moon, they hit the surface at the same time. Newton's laws of motion state that an object in motion will remain in motion until a force acts upon it. With no air resistance to provide force, objects will perceive no acceleration (change in either direction or speed).
