    Only the partial pressure of the gas above the solution will have an effect. Not the nature atmosphere. In this case, the partial pressure of CO2 is a little lower at higher altitude, but since its such a small percentage ~0.04% of standard air, the small reduction of atmospheric pressure will have a minor impact on its solubility.
