    I was taking A&P and our prof used the example of when the arctic fox pokes its nose out of the grass next to the nesting Canadian snow goose the goose has to decide between a) try and fight the fox or b) flee and survive but lose the investment of her eggs. Instead of the limbic system being able to decide between the two monumental decisions it opts for option c) the goose puts her head under her wing and takes a nap. And the fox gets both the goose and the eggs.
