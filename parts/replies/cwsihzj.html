    • WRSaunders

      At some point, they got the idea that "the Moon Landing was a fake" in their head. It's sad to say, but it was probably on the Internet. When discussions of the Moon Landing come up, they trigger memories of the notion that it was a fake, and they say things like "It was a fake". The memory that it was a fake is self-reinforcing, particularly when folks get upset that the person holds such an obviously false opinion. That critical reaction reduces the impact those people's recollection will have on the re-consolidated version of the persons "Moon Landing" memory.
