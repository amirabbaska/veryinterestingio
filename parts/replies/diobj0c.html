    • patroclus2stronk

      No. Perjury is its own charge that requires a separate charge and conviction, where a jury or judge (if a bench trial is requested) has to find beyond a reasonable doubt that they lied under oath.

      In your situation, the person is essentially saying they didn't do it, but the jury doesn't believe them; that's not proof that they lied.
