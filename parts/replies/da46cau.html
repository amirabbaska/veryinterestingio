    Finally, their kidneys are built of nephrons, urine producing units, whose structure and cellular structure though another counter-current mechanism to recover much more water from the urine stream.

    Just to clarify - all mammal kidneys are built of nephrons. Animals in water-restricted environments have much longer loops of Henle, which allows for higher concentration gradients to be created, resulting in better resorption and retention of water.
