    As a heads-up for women reading this thread: some research shows that emergency contraceptives are significantly less effective for overweight women:

    Women with a body mass index (BMI) of 25 or higher experienced decreased efficacy, and the pill stopped working entirely in obese women with a BMI of 30 or higher.

    But, on the bright side, research has not shown decreased efficacy for regular birth control in overweight women.
