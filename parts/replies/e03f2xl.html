  • brouwjon

    One important clue for "intelligence" is the Cognitive Revolution. There were anatomically modern humans on Earth for a long time, and they left very few artifacts of tools, pottery, burials, art, etc. On an evolutionary timescale, the transition from this state to one where humans were burying their dead, making tools, large campsites, and pottery, was very abrupt. The state transition was very quick, and there is little anatomical difference between the populations (before Smart vs after Smart). This suggests that the genetic changes were very minor, and thus only small adjustments in brain architectures were needed to go from Not-Smart humans and Smart humans.
