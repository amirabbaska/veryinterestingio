      • maxjets

        Most adhesives act via Van der Waals forces. This is a completely separate phenomenon from chemical bonds. Only a few adhesives (like epoxies) actually chemically bond with their substrates, and even then only with some substrates. Van der Waals forces are caused by random molecular charge distribution patterns.
