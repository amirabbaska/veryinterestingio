    An alternate version that cuts out the idea of playing with recordings (which in my mind obfuscates the issue): a car is on a road that goes east-west. The car facing east is the positive direction and traveling east is positive velocity, traveling and facing west is negative. The car being in (forward) drive is positive, in reverse is negative. Then consider the combinations of (facing east or west)*(being in forward drive or reverse) on (traveling east or west).
