    So let's take system with a star and 3x Jupiter a good distance away. Say it has a Hill radius 100 Mkm (Neptune in real life exceeds that). We'll give it a 'moon' with the mass of Neptune and put it at the extreme limit, the 100Mkm. It will itself have a Hill radius of 18 Mkm. Then we put a super-Earth (10x) at that distance, which has a Hill radius of 10 Mkm. Put Earth around that one, 3.2 Mkm. Mars around it, 1Mkm. Then Luna, 336,000 km. Ceres, 53,000 km.

    So clearly, this can continue for quite a while, if you're willing and capable of engineering it yourself.
