    • andero

      Oh, I was not saying that we dream about useful things, or even that we dream about the things we are trying to learn. Sometimes we do, and other times nonsense.

      The answer really is we have no idea why we dream the specific content of what we dream. My idea is just that whatever you are learning, your brain needs to simulate experience to test the new connections in order to make sure it didn't "break" some other functionality. You would not want to be learning a new language and find out your forgot an old one, or forgot how to process faces or something.

      In this idea our brains are like computers in that there is a software-update every night and the dream is the testing-suite to minimize the presence of "bugs". There is basically no way to format and reinstall, so we need to test as we go.
