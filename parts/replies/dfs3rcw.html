      Under the Montreal Protocol, the manufacturing of R12 was banned in developed countries in 2005, and in the developing world in 2010. The phaseout of R-22 is ongoing. The EU has completely banned it, and the USA is in the process of phasing it out. At this time, in the USA, it can only be used to service existing equipment that depends on it.
