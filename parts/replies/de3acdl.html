      To put it bluntly: there is plenty of time to die; there is never plenty of time to be pregnant.

      Remember that evolution does not happen at the species level but the individual level. Every individual is "trying" to maximize their inclusive reproductive fitness. It's not that only a few men "need" to make it, but that the situation incentivizes men to be risk takers. Doesn't mean they need to go off and die as soon as possible (after all making a person is expensive, male or female), and since they don't need to get to the task of being pregnant ASAP, they can afford to take a little longer to grow and learn.

      Also remember that this discussion by definition fixates on how men and women are different. They are similar in perhaps many more ways than they are different, but that main difference is that women gestate children, and it is significant.
