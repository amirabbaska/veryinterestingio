    The basic breakdown of blue/green screening as I gathered it:

    On emulsion film, blue was used because the chemical that was sensitive to blue wavelengths was the finest, and therefore held the most detail.

    On digital, we made the CMOS sensors to favor green, since our eyes favor green. So green became the ideal color for masking, as that was what held the best resolution and detail.
