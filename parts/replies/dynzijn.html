      It’s actually pretty likely that tyrannosaurids were at least somewhat social animals. We have trackways of at least three similar- walking in the same direction, a bonebed of Albertosaurus with at least 12 individuals of various growth stages, and face-bite scars (most complete specimens have these and they are almost assuredly from other tyrannosaurids) provide sufficient evidence to suggest they were indeed gregarious.
