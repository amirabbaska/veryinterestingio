  • Sands43

    The thing that has always cracked me up about dogs is that they will make location associations with training. If you only teach your dog to "heal" in the living room, they will only heal in the living room. But extend that training to the drive way and the back yard, now it's an "everywhere" command.

    I knew a woman that tried to teach their dogs to crap only in one place, and would reinforce that with treats. The problem was, that was the only time they got treats. So the dogs crapped when they saw the treats, not when they where in that "spot". So they would crap in the house, the car, etc.

    So don't teach your dog to "speak" in the house, just outside.

    (not a pro dog trainer, just owned, and trained, 7 dogs as house pets.)
