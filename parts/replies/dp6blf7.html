    • robbak

      The speed of things in orbit around the earth, and their distance. We can use radar and clocks to measure both things to a very high confidence, and from them we can directly calculate the earth's mass.

      This is why astronomers get excited when they find that a planet or asteroid has a moon. It means they can calculate the mass.
