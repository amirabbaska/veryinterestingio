    A terrifying way to look at this is that your head or umbrella or tree you're under also sends out a positive streamer.

    If that connection is completed first that object will get struck by lightning. Lightning rods intentionally setup to act as a more likely target and safely grounded conduit for things around them.

    The taller the tree or structure the less vertical distance from the negative charge. That's why you are told to not seek shelter under a tall tree or stand on a very flat area like a golf course without any cover.
