    • Feetyoumeet

      Wouldn't hurt having him checked out! You can go to www.acvo.org and find a veterinary ophthalmologist near you. If he doesn't have any other issues (redness, squinting, etc) they can do what is called an ECR exam (used to be called a CERF). It is usually cheaper and is done for breeding dogs or litters of puppies, service dogs, etc. They check for inherited conditions, cataracts, and do a fundic exam (check retina).
