As an indie label, they signed many of the Seattle grunge bands first, like Nirvana before they moved to a larger label. So they are sorta in between a huge label and indie label, but have a gigantic roster of the most popular 'indie' bands in the last 20 years. Nowadays I tend to think of Alt as more of a sound (distinguished by the alt sound of the 90s) and Indie as more of a type of band that doesn't fit most of the molds (where this was Alt in the 90s).
