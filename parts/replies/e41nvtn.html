    • Prasiatko

      The choleea toxin attacks a chlorine transporter (CFTR) on your cells jamming it open. This causes chlorine to flow out of the cell followed by water hence the diarrhea that accompanies cholera. If you have the cystic fibrosis mutation in CFTR then these transporters don't function correctly and the cholera toxin can't affect them.
