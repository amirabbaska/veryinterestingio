      • DaraelDraconis

        Actually, it's lumens that measure luminous flux. Lux measure illuminance. The name is not derived from luminous flux or from light flux, but from the Latin lux, meaning "light".

        But the point really is that the meaning of light in the standard register of English covers several concepts, luminance (SI unit: cd), luminous flux (lm), and illuminance (lx) all among them. For this particular question, I consider illuminance to be the most applicable one, and hence lx to be the appropriate unit.
