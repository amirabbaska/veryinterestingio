      I'm not implying that the Ruy Lopez is the only opening played, just that it is currently the most popular at high levels. If you look at the 2016 World Championships, the final battle between Carlsen and Karjakin, for example, was 16 games with 11 or 12 being Ruy Lopez openings.

      There are many openings and variations of openings that are really strong, and there are many to choose from, but high level play tends to reward consistency and patience, and certain openings don't really profit much from that, e.g. the Indian Defense.
