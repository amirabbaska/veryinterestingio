      Imagine taking a knife (the cosmic ray) and a piece of paper (your sensor). The ray isn't really affected by anything in terms of orientation, so when it hits the paper the resultant streak will really just depend on where the knife was coming from and what angle the knife was hitting the paper. For example, it could hit point on, which would look like a spot, or it could come roughly perpendicular, which would create a long cut as it passed through.
