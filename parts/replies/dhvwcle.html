    I think the green tint comes from accumulated travelling through a lot of glass. Second surface mirrors are typically a sheet of glass with some kind of coating on the back. So every time a reflection happens it is happening after going through a layer of glass. After many reflections, the faint tint of the glass starts to show in the same way it is difficult to build a two feet thick wall of glass without that same green showing.
