    So what do I do if I rotate from back to both sides through the night?

    • pepe_le_shoe

      You are symmetrical, thus, the needed height of pillow will be the same for both side. You just need to find the perfect thickness of pillow.

      Alternatively, there are foam based pillows, and also ones filled with things such as buckwheat husks, which can be manipulated or will deform, and then stay in whatever shape you need. I make a little dent in my buckwheat pillow to get it the right thickness for my head/shoulders, works just fine.
