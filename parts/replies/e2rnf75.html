    You sound convincing but this is actually debated in the scientific literature.

    1) On the longer run, not eating will deplete you of your energy resources.

    2) The metabolic pathways engaged in digestion are active in cells of the digestive system, such as enterocytes, not immune cells such as neutrophilles.
    3) Loss of appetite is universal, not just for food-borne infections.

    4) Anorexia is accompanied by a slew of other symptoms, activated by the same biological pathways, such as fatigue and muscle pain.
