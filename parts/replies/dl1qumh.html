      • Rakuall

        Humans can see at least 400 'FPS' as well, though the term is slightly inaccurate when describing human vision. Look up fighter pilot vision testing. They'd be shown a 'static' image on a very high refresh rate monitor (over 300 Hz / fps IIRC, some as high as 600), and one frame would have a plane added. The pilots could reliably say when the plane blinked, and often could recognize the make and model off of it's silhouette for 0.25 of one percent of one second.
