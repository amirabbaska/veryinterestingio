    Yes and maybe.

    Fertility is pretty low for people with downs, only about 20% for women - for a while it was suspected that it was impossible (combination of isolation within the family and stigma kept it quiet). Males with downs have even lower fertility.

    The chances of the baby having downs is quite high - about 35-50%. It would still be possible for a downs parent to produce a non-downs child though.
