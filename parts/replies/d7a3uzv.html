      Hemoglobin has a molecular weight of about 64400 atomic units per protein. The average total amount of hemoglobin per red blood cell is around 30 picograms.

      64400 atomic units = 1.06938789* 10^-7 picograms per protein.

      (30 10^-12) / (1.06938789 10^-19) =280534316 hemoglobin proteins per red blood cell. I think /u/bremidon is right, if he means million instead of billion.
