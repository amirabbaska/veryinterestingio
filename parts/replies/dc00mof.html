      But the placebo effect does not care if the effect measured is caused by biases or a "mind over body" effect. It simply means, "In research Y, patient in the control group report/have the effect X" noting else. It says nothing of the sources of X. It is irresponsible to attribute this to a "mind over body" effect when the tool used isn't meant for this. You can end up with an idea of the placebo effect as a therapeutic tool and this is dangerous.
