• Merfstick

    All good advice, but just to add a point/tip/pun:

    What you're throwing actually doesn't have to be sharp. Don't play with razor sharp axes and knives. If you throw it with enough effort to make it stick in the first place, it's going to stick, regardless of how sharp it is. There's no need to put yourself at a higher risk.

    The 'hawk I learned on was dull as shit, and only got worse over 3 years without it ever becoming an issue.
