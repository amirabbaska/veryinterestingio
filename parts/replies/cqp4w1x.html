    • _Gills_

      My pleasure :) A lot of myths around breath-hold, it mostly boils down to the fact that there is a difference between the urge to breathe and the need to breathe.

      The urge to breathe comes (mostly) from CO2, the need to breathe comes from low O2. Until people have done some training it's hard to figure out which is which.
