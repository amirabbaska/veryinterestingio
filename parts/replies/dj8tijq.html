    Aloe does nothing with regards to DNA repair. You can't really do anything in that regard. For that matter, the effects of aloe vera gel on sunburn is disputed - there's one study that found some positive effect, but multiple others that found no correlation. It might make it feel better, but it may not do anything to accelerate healing.

    As for your lack of peeling, it may simply be that the burn was not severe enough to necessitate sloughing off the upper layers of the epidermis (due to destruction of lower layers).
