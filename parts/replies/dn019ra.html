    I like restoring old tube radios, and you run into this issue often. It's called a "hot chassis." Old AC plugs were not polarized, so you had a 50/50 shot that the chassis was at line potential even when off. That's why you always unplug before opening anything up. I always install a modern plug or modify the old one so it's polarized correctly.
