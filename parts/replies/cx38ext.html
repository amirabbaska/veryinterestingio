    • loljetfuel

      All such form rules limit the brute-force search space (easier for attacker), but exist because people are trying to increase the number of guesses required for a dictionary search (which should make things harder for an attacker).

      All security is made up of these sorts of trade-offs, and some people make fairly stupid ones -- but the mistake is usually being overly-worried about a particular attack rather than seeing the bigger picture.
