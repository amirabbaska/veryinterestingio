    • MultiFazed

      Since when are URLs case sensitive?

      The portion up to and including the TLD is not case sensitive, but anything after that is internal to the specific website, and can be case sensitive if they design their site to treat them differently.

      For example, here's an imgur URL: https://imgur.com/dQpr2

      And here's the same URL with the first 'd' changed to upper-case: https://imgur.com/DQpr2

      The second one doesn't work, because it's not referencing a valid image.
