    As a previous oral care researcher who worked with fluoride on a daily basis, I believe many of the concerns around fluoride are misguided due to the relatively low exposure levels we actually experience.

    It cannot be stated too often that the risk of fluorosis is mainly to young children (a) because their odontoblasts are vulnerable to fluoride and (b) they are notorious for swallowing toothpaste (indeed, manufacturers flavour it to encourage this).
