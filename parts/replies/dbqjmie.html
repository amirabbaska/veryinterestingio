    Now, if the Earth was on a circular orbit, all would be fine, and every 24 hours the Sun would return to the same location in the sky (negating seasons).

    Even if Earth's orbit were perfectly circular, the Sun would still appear to travel "slow" on some days or "fast" on others throughout the year because of our axial tilt.
