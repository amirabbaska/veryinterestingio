    • ThePolemicist

      There have been studies on supplements benefiting certain medical conditions, like anemia and some cancers. However, there have also been studies on supplements that show a correlation between supplements and certain medical conditions, like prostate cancer. So, the question is, do the benefits outweigh the risks? The answer appears to be no, unless you have an actual deficiency. Numerous studies now have found a strong correlation between people who take supplements and lower mortality. At first, people thought that maybe people who take supplements are just more sickly, which would explain lower mortality. However, more studies have taken other factors into account and still show a correlation with supplement use and an increase in mortality.
