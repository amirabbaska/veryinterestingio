      • ProjectSunlight

        These ultra low temp machines use a cascade refrigeration system. Essentially one refrigeration system is used to super cool a second system. If used in just a freezer type setting there is no additional heat transfer systems, merely the evaporator of the second stage. Most systems now use a mixture of R290 (chemically pure propane) and R508B, this is just in the second stage. Additionally, this super cold refrigerant can be used to chill a low temp liquid which is then pumped through shelves. A popular liquid used is called Syltherm XLT manufactured by DuPont.
