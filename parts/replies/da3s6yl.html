    What affects the power draw of the air conditioning in a car?

    • Heat rejection capacity of the condenser, which is a function of vehicle speed.
    • Heat transfer into the cabin from outside, which is a function of vehicle speed.
    • Effect of radiation on the vehicle, which is a function of vehicle speed.

    The air conditioning on a vehicle is very dependent on vehicle speed.
