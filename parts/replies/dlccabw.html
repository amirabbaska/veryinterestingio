      Think of the body telling your brain to eat as input and the brain making you crave non food items as output. Under normal conditions the brain would make you crave regular foods, but for some people their brains mess up and make you crave weird non food things. We don't know what is happening exactly that makes the brain mess up, but one popular theory is that your brain is working overdrive to deal with all the physical processes involved with pregnancy that it can interfere with psychological functioning.
