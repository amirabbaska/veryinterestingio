    First, the "signal strength" bar on your phone is entirely the receiving signal from the tower to your phone.

    This isn't true for CDMA or LTE. The phone knows the level of signal from the tower, but also the tower gives feedback of how well it can "hear" your phone.

    Sidenote: Usually when you have low to no signal, the problem isn't that your phone can't "hear" the tower. It's that the tower can't "hear" your phone.
