    This is a good point. People (like many animals) shout or yelp when hurt so that anyone that hurt them immediately knows and stops the behavior. A ancestor that did not have a vocal response to pain would have been more likely to be accidentally maimed or killed by one of their own, and therefore less likely to pass on their genes. This is one possible evolutionary mechanism.
