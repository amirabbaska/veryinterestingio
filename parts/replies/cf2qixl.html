    • colondee

      Reformatting a hard drive does not make it new.

      All it does is gets rid of the software that was making your system seem slow.

      Hardware is mechanical and a hard drive especially has moving parts. These things wear down. Your hard drive is 7 years old. While I have a hard drive from 1997 that still works, it is much slower than it was in 1997 (and this is not just psychological)

      If you know more about cars than computers, then would a car with 40000 miles be slower than the same car with 0 miles?
