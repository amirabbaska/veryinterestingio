    Inflammation is caused by increased immune system activity - increased cancer from chronic inflammation is probably more due to the direct effects of inflammatory mediators: increased cell turnover (cytokines) and direct damage to cell DNA (reactive oxygen species).

    Suppression of immunity does also predispose to cancer as well, like when people are on immunosuppressive drugs they often develop multiple skin cancers. These people would have less inflammation though, not more.
