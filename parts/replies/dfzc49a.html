      Some reasons: diffusion of gases down, light energy down (or even 0, because of snow blocking it), less water available (think winter: ice and snow are useless for trees in sense of photosynthesis), temperature down which means enzymes work slower, less heat means less water gets "soaked" up into the leafs to be processed.
