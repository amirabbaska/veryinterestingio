    Could you expand on that last part? Is there a HJR1 (hot and jalapeno receptor 1) on our tongues?

    • Tak_Galaman

      Yes, capsaicin is a chemical in jalapenos that binds to TRPV1 channels on your tongue and causes them to open. These channels also open when exposed to high temperature.

      So the effect is analogous, but the proteins involved are different.
