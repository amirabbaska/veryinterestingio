    Tip for anyone reading this: If you want to donate blood after a disaster, wait until roughly a month after the disaster.

    When the disaster first hits, a lot of people will be out donating blood to help. After about a month, all the unused blood is going to be too old. Feeling that they already did their part, people are unlikely to donate again so soon. This means a serious need for blood donations, and is the perfect time for you to donate.
