      So i'm by no means an expert, and i doubt my answer even belongs here, but your question intrigued me.

      As far as i know, humans do not like to be surprised. Music for example is all rythm based, as soon as you screw up the rythm in a song you will notice it and dislike it.

        My father-in-law flew the charter plane for Yes' 30 cities 40 night tour back in the day. In conversations with the band, they told him, in concerts, music puts the crowd in a euphoric state. They would build the crowd up and up until the energy was at a tipping point. Then they would purposefully skip or mess up a beat to keep the crowd in check and bring them down a bit. If they didn't do that, that's when crowds turned and would get out of control and people get hurt.
