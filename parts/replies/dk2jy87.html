    • newosis

      Mainly because you couldn't have the same type of can made resealable. Remember the strength comes from the pressure of the liquid within it. So a standard can has to have a fizzy liquid in it to provide pressure.

      Making this resealable hits two major problems, first once opened the gas vents, the fizziness decreases, you'll reseal and the can will have lost all it's strength.

      Secondly you wouldn't be able to open it with a resealable tab as you need to first vent the pressure, then open the can.

      So to make a standard can resealable is impossible, you instead have to create a different can (although it may look the same with a different tab the manufacturing process will be very different) which will require thicker walls and more materials etc. Doing this negates many of the benefits a standard can provides and so you may as well at this point use what's already designed for the job, a plastic bottle.
