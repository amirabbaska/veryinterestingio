    • Fluid_Angle

      It’s physically that hard to do. Your central ventilators drive won’t allow you to hold your breath indefinitely. In addition, you exhale a lesser amount of oxygen with every breath, so the air you rebreathe into the bag has O2 that would would miss out on by holding your breath. Further, a paper bag on the face is not a closed circuit, so air (including oxygen) is entrained into the bag every time the pressure in the bag decreases with your inhalation.
