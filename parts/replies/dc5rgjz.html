  • Captainroy

    Used to work in a major retail pharmacy and I can confirm this is true.

    The commonly used medications are always in stock, it's the more name brand and less commonly used medications that require placing an order or having to check if neighboring pharmacies have supplies. Also, we received shipments daily because they keep a very accurate computer database that's updated several times a day due to the amount of medication supply being tightly monitored. Every time a prescription is filled, a bottle is scanned and it knows the amount taken each time.
