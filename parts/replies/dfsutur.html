      Pain is different, because to the brain that's always something urgent.

      If you smell something new, it's interesting. If you've been smelling it for the past hour, if it hasn't killed/affected you so far, you'll probably be fine staying there longer.

      If you're missing a leg, or bleeding, no matter how long that lasts it's going to be bad for you for it to last longer. Pain is your brain's way of saying "Something's wrong, do something about it!", regardless of if you are even capable of doing something about it.
