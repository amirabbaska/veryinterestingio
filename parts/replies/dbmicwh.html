    • FlamingTonfa

      They can't because an egg is only viable for about 24h. During this time, it's lurking in the Fallopian tube, which is difficult to access. By the time it comes out with the shredded blood and tissue, it's not really viable anymore due to age and hormone changes. Also, it's not feasible to sort through all the blood cells, uterine lining cells, etc. to find a single cell.
