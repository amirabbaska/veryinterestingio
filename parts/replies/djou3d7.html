  • wfaulk

    Interestingly, I make my own microwave popcorn and I've found I have to use a plate or it doesn't work right.

    For the record:

    • put a heaping quarter cup of popcorn kernels in a brown sandwich bag, but keep the bag folded flat
    • put popcorn salt in bag to taste (I don't measure it, but it's just enough to see inside the bag; it kinda looks like a slightly frosty morning)
    • place 1 paper towel, folded in half, (or two half-size paper towels on top of each other) on top of a microwave-safe plate
    • put 1 tablespoon of liquid cooking oil (canola or whatever) inside the bag
    • give the bag a good shake to distribute the oil and salt over the kernels
    • fold over the top of the bag and pierce the fold with a bamboo skewer to hold it closed
    • place the bag on top of the paper towel with the folded bottom pointed up
    • place the plate, paper towel, and popcorn bag in the microwave
    • microwave on high for about 2 minutes
    • remove the bag (careful, it'll be hot) and empty the popcorn into a bowl
