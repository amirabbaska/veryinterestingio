    Serious question, would the ring/disk material around the star be melted or vaporized because of its close proximity to the star?

    • Articulate_Pineapple

      If we use our sun as an example, the ring material would be found inwards of Mercury's orbit. The ring may be scorching hot, but I doubt it would be vaporized. Heat transfer in a vacuum (via radiation) is quite inefficient when compared to convection and conduction. Unless the ring hits the corona of the sun, it won't be vaporized.
