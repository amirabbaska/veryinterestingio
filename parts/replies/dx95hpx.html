      You'll need to go to the gym for like a solid two weeks (2-3x a week) before you reach the "addiction" phase.

      For me, I get DOMS pretty bad. Repetition of the solid day or two afterwards of just stretching every opportunity I get to settle it turned into me relishing in the tightness of my muscles. It turned into addiction pretty quickly. It sounds weird to say, but I don't actually do it for the body figure anymore, I do it so I can get DOMS and stretch. I get antsy and agitated if it's been too long since my last good gym day.

      ... Is this a weird road I'm going down? Is this a doorway best left closed? Ah well. I'll enjoy it while it lasts.
