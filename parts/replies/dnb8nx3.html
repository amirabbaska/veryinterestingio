    Simply put OTF is better for advanced typology or use of special characters

    This is not true. CFF-OTF and TTF-OTF have the same advanced typographic functionality. The specification for both fonts is the same (it was developed jointly by Adobe and Microsoft).

    The difference is in outlines (Quadratic vs PS) and hinting rules (TT hinting vs PS Blues).
