    An immunologist I work with coined the term 'barrier hypothesis' which implies that a breakdown or loss of maintenance of barriers to the environment will expose the immune system in a non-tolerogenic manner. This can lead to allergic response.

    It's common to see children with atopic dermatitis developing food allergy for instance. That hypothesis suggests that happens because they are getting sensitized through the skin.
