    So the answer is it depends. It possible to run right off the charger, but it's cheaper and easier to pass through the battery.

    Passing through the battery is a misleading way to describe this. The current passes through various switches and protection circuits for the battery. But if the battery is at 100%, and is neither charging nor discharging, the current going to power the phone does not flow through the battery cell itself. There's a T connection, with the power source from the wall, through charging circuitry, on the left, the battery at the bottom and the phone on the right. The current in the two top wires is equal and the current in the battery is zero.
