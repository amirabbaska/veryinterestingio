    • hashiusclay

      Warming up does not necessarily mean stretching. Studies have shown a correlation between pre-cardio static stretching and increased injury risk during cardio, so most fitness experts now recommend dynamic stretching (like walking or other low-intensity cardio) as a warm up for high-intensity cardio. This, combined with static stretching after cardio, has been shown to greatly reduce the risk of injury during exercise.
