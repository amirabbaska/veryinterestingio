    Yeah I was looking for someone to point this out.

    When you refer to a foetus passing waste it's really not waste in the way people think.

    Meconium should by and large remain in the baby until after birth (and even then isn't really 'poo')

    as for 'peeing' it's really just cycling amniotic fluid in and out so the organs can practice at what their supposed to after birth.

    It's not 'urine' in the same way we produce urine...after all the foetus isn't technically eating or drinking, just passing nutrients back and forth across the placenta...
