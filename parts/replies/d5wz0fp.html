    Anecdotal example, since this exact thing happened to my wife. One of them is called a Uterine Arterial Embolization, or UAE. Basically they ran a tube through the artery in her leg into the area around her uterus and clotted/sealed it up. Then they stick what is essentially a big water balloon into her uterus to keep it tight, wait some amount of time (in her case about a day) then decrease water pressure / take it out and make sure it doesn't bleed again.
