    > A judge can choose to offer no bail and hold a defendant until trial

    I think that's the part that confuses people. The Eighth Amendment prohibits excessive bail (along with excessive fines and cruel & unusual punishment). That doesn't mean someone has a right to bail, just that bail can't be indirectly denied by setting it to an absurdly high value. Either bail is denied, or it is set to a reasonable amount to ensure the defendant will appear at court.
