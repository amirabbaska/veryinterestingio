      The signal only goes so far, so most undersea network cables will have a powered signal repeater every X distance (I Don't remember the distance)

      Its also usually not 1 cable, but even in a single "cable" there will be a dozen or more Fibers which each can carry separate signals, and through the use of different wavelengths (colors), each fiber can carry different connections.

      Effectively, its so damn expensive to run these cables, they stuff as many fibers and wavelengths as possible in there to get their money's worth.
