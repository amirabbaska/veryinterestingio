    Another big issue that is always looked at in aircraft is performance during an engine failure. If you lose a single engine in a quad you will essentially have no operational control or stability whatsoever, and you’d get flipped upside down pretty quick. Also in a giant quad you would have 4 driveshafts powering the roots (because batteries aren’t powerful enough for use in aviation... yet ) which means 4 times more shafts/bearings/joints/etc that can possibly fail, as well as a lot of lost energy in the transfer from engine to multiple rotors.

    Source: I’m a current commercial (and former airline) pilot.
