    • kflave249

      There are different types of sensors for different sensations: light touch, deep touch, pain, temperature. And there are different pathways they take in the spinal cord to the brain. One pathway, the spinothalamic tract carries crude touch as well as pain sensations. They will typically all travel through the thalamus, which you can think of the train station to the brain. A signal goes to the thalamus and it tells it where to go in the brain.
