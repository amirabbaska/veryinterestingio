    To be a little more specific: "and if they're large enough or moving fast enough, they can create gravitational waves that we can detect".

    Any two masses moving around each other generate gravitational waves, it's just that these waves are generally so small, we have no ability to detect them. It takes very massive objects moving very quickly to generate gravitational waves large enough for us to measure.
