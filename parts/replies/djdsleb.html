    As someone in the business of inspecting and estimating cars, if you knew what I know, you would never be able to buy a used car again, because the salesperson would have you kicked off the lot. I have seen cars with less than 10,000 miles on them be almost completely previous repairs that were undocumented. And just to get it out there, Carfax and other related services are completely useless. They only show what was reported to them. You will almost never see a car with any miles on it that hasn't been messed up somehow. It just won't happen. In the odd occasion it does happen, expect to pay a good sum for it. Taking care of your vehicles really pays, folks. And for the love of god; don't buy from Craigslist unless you are expecting a hunk of junk.
