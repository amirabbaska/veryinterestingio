    • 99beans

      Do this for (5 min):

      • Step 1: Concentrate on feeling the tip of your nose
      • Step 2: Inhale and count one
      • Step 3: Exhale and count two
      • Step 4: Repeat until ten and start over
      • Condition: When (not if) you lose your concentration, return to breath and start over

      Do this every day for twenty minutes. This is just like exercising a muscle.

      After a few weeks or months of daily concentration exercise, it will have profound life-altering effects on all aspects of your life. It rarely takes more than a few months for people, who consistently do concentration meditation to see great benefit. So don't take my word for it - see for yourself.

      You can say this first big step because it means having a clear mind. You could say this is a tamed monkey.

      But of course, it doesn't end there. Your monkey will still act up sometimes.

      If a tamed monkey is an enlightenment, then that process is simple too.

      Once you have good concentration described above, you can start to concentrate on three things

      • The impermanence of everything
      • Suffering
      • Your concept of having or owning a self

      And once you make good progress with that, you can concentrate on these things happening very rapidly (faster than ten times per second).

        1. Material forms
        1. The sensation of either good or bad
        1. Labeling things (eg. green tree frog)
        1. Conditioned things (eg. avoid that poisonous frog)
        1. Consciousness

      The enlightenment formula at the end of the path is:

      Concentrate on consciousness

      WHILE

      Having correct understanding of suffering, impermanence, and self

      At this point there is no monkey left to tame and you can know the reason for all of your emotions.
