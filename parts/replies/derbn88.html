    Thermal depolymerization is interesting, but right now it's not economical. TD takes a ridiculous amount of energy. There have been a few companies hat have tried to commercialize it, but even when they were using high-yield stock (mostly chicken offal), which they got for nearly free, they just barely made a profit during the height of oil prices (when gas was hitting $5 a gallon in the U.S).

    If we're going to do TD, it needs to be used in conjunction with waste management, and for the foreseeable future it's just going to be an energy sink.
