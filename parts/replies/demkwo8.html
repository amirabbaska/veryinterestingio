      • Subtitles_Required

        Cords is the colloquial term, just like "voice box." Your vocal "cords" are flaps of tissue made of a jello-like outer layer and a muscle-y inner layer. Because they are flaps, the way air pushes through them causes them to vibrate a certain way, depending on how loose/taut the flaps are (edit: and how close they are together).

        Picture them like your lips. Put your lips together but not tightly, then blow air through them until you blow a raspberry. This is a very dumbed down demonstration of how your vocal folds work (this is ELI5 afterall!). They aren't literal cords like a cord of rope.
