      • grmmrnz

        It was widely accepted, although technically not proven. Plato (200 years before Eratosthenes) knew the Earth was round, just because that was the general opinion at the time ("If you could fly above the clouds, the Earth would resemble a ball"). Pythagoras, Aristotle, Achimedes ("Liquid at rest is part of a sphere with the centre of the Earth as its centre) all thought the Earth was round.
