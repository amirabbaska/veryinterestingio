    Your hot and dull equates to how oily the food is, and how cooked down/chopped up the peppers are. All the heat you get from peppers is fairly close to the same.

    While peppers are oil based in their capsaicin storage, things like wasabi are actually water based, so that is why you may notice the heat from wasabi burning all throughout your nasal passages.
