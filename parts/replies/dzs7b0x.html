    • LearnerPermit

      Because Argentinian ants are invasive, and formed out of a few initial introduced queens, there is very little genetic diversity. So nearby colonies are not hostal to each other. Individual nests can have 15-20 queens, ants move back and forth between different nests. Combined with mild winter, the cold season die off is less.

      There is effectively one colony that spans from San Diego to San Francisco, another that spans across most of the Serria mountains.
