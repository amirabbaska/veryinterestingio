    • BroomIsWorking

      That's a hand-wavey answer. Yes, animals get sick, but by and large not from drinking from mud puddles.

      Instead, their guts are simply better prepared to handle the microbes they are likely to pick up from those sources - both by immune system defenses aimed specifically at them, and often by having shorter digestive tracts (which gives less time for intestinal poisons to build up, when something like botulin is ingested).
