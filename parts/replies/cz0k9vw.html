      • TheJaice

        There doesn't really seem to be a lot of scientific evidence linking stress and a loss of hair pigment. There have been verified cases of extreme stress affecting the hormones responsible for hair production, and leading to bald patches, etc, but in general, hair colour is predetermined.

        There is a lot of anecdotal cases of hair going grey at an early age because of stress, but if you think about it, most human adults are constantly under stress. When people go grey early, it is almost always easy to find some sort of new source of stress that can be used to explain it, instead of understanding the biological reason behind it.
