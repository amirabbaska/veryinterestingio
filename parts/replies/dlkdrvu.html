    • Nakiranda

      Ah the craziness of allergies. Did you know that people who are allergic to horses will get an allergic immune respons from seeing a picture of a horse? The explanations isn't always easy or straight forward. It could be that your body have drawn a line between the smell of oranges and the allergic respons, so you are now hyper aware of that smell. The smell of oranges could be your version of the picture of the horse. You may unconsciously be looking for that smell and therefore react at the odor detection threshold.

      I don't really have an answer to your question but citrus fruits are special. If you want to measure the VOC (volatile organic compounds) no citrus fruits can be present in the room or they completely ruin the sample by being so abundant in the air.

      A VOC measurement is about detecting which organic compounds are present in the air. The problem with these measurements are that we react so widely different to different compounds.They have different potency to us. So simply knowing which compounds are present in which amounts isn't getting us very far. We also have to know at which levels different compounds will affect us and we really don't have that data.

      And if someone is pealing an orange there isn't just one compound thats being released. There is an entire bouquet of compounds.
