    Piggybacking on top comment:

    I work at an inpatient psych hospital so maybe can provide some insight.

    If a pt comes in late on a Friday night, they will not be meeting with their assigned treatment team until Monday. The normal psychiatrists, therapists, and social workers work a standard Mon-Fri schedule. The pt may only be evaluated as needed by the weekend doctors/practitioners.

    For us (in Minnesota at least) the 72 hour hold will not begin until the pt has start receiving their individualized treatment which in this case would be on Monday.
