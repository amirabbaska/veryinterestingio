      Submarines have typically avoided halogen typically because with military submarines it produces a hum that is detectible so that's one of the reasons not to use halogen. So moving on to marine biologist submarines, a halogen lightbulb is ALOT less reliable than an LED light, and a loss of light would be catastrophic. An LED light produces zero UV emissions, last up to 60x longer, consumes less energy and produces about 10% of the heat a halogen does. So cost wise and looking at reliability, an LED light system is a no brainer.
