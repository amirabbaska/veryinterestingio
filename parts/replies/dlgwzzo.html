    The Mongols are avid drinkers of milk. Also, recent research is finding that many folks that think that they're lactose intolerant, in fact, are not. Most dairy cows (especially in North America - Holstein cows) have a gene mutation that changes their casein protein (the variant being called A1); other cows still have the non-mutated form (the variant being called A2). Humans, sheep, goats, and most breeds of cows still have the A2 variant of the casein protein and most folks do fine with this when the A1 variety of milk is removed from their diets. This A1 variant has been linked to many issues including contributing to autism and cancers, not to mention symptoms similar to lactose intolerance.
