      To answer that question: water just isn't very compressable. Water decreases in volume by about 2% at the bottom of the ocean. The bottom of the ocean is only around 40 MPa of pressure. For reference atmospheric pressure is 0.101325 MPa so it's a huge change. You would need pressures an order of magnitude greater than that at the bottom of the ocean to form ice above 0 deg C.
