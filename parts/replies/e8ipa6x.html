    • Hocusader

      Yeah, RPM is limited by tip speed. Generally speaking, you don't want your blades breaking the speed of sound because you get shocks on the blades. This can be damaging, reduce efficiency, etc.

      So a larger blade will have a faster tip speed than a smaller blade if they are rotating at the same rate. The larger the blade, the slower your max rotational rate is. A smaller blade can spin faster before hitting the limit.
