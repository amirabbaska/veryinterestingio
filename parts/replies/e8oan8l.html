    • Deano1234

      Slightly different. Time restricted eating (Tre) is only eating at certain times. It doesn't mean fasting for longer than traditional fasting. Intermittent fasting (if) is long periods without eating while Tre is windows of eating. Example: Tre =eating at 30 minute windows at 8:00, 13:30 and 1800. While IF is: I'm going to stop eating at bedtime and won't eat again til at least 14:00 tomorrow.
