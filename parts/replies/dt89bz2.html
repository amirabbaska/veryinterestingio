    Anyone can correct me if I’m wrong, but I believe it’s because fighting the infection is prioritized over making you feel okay. Your body is under a lot of distress fighting off this infection- your temperature rises for viral infections in order to denature the virus proteins. They “die” (are inactivated), but your cells also don’t function optimally at those temperatures. The added mucus is unpleasant in your throat, but is one of the first barriers in preventing further infections. Your body is using a lot of energy to fight, so you feel tired.

    I don’t think it has much to do with evolutionarily trying to make you feel bad, but instead there was no reason for evolution to “fix” feeling bad, because you don’t die of feeling bad.
