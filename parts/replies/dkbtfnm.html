  • fcuriois

    The federal sentencing guidelines are illegal when they take away a judges discretion. The current ones are non binding.

    Judges in the United States are supposed to take an active interest in the resolution of the specific problem. Think if it like if your car breaks down, you want your mechanic to find the problem and fix it rather than going through a script which doesn't fix the issue. Judges aren't supposed to make unwarranted assumptions but actually look at the facts.

    Since judges have different rules that apply in both criminal and civil trials you may see a difference there, but the main issue generally comes down to what is fair in the specific situation rather than what is expediant.
