      A fuselage is the main body (basically the entire cylinder) and unless you are wiring, fuel, or aluminum you're always in the fuselage of the aircraft.

      The conflicting reports were whether she was found in an intact portion of the middle part of the fuselage or in the tail section (any part of which that is occupiable by passengers/crew is still part of the fuselage).

      There was never a question of in/not inside, but rather which section of the plane.
