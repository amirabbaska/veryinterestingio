    Even with TIG, welding aluminium is different from welding steel.   • YouProblem

    A few small corrections

    • Aluminum oxide forms quickly, in a very thin, basically invisible layer. This layer is unlike the the oxide that forms on steel, which is flaky and porous. Aluminum oxide forms an impermeable layer that prevents further oxidation.
    • The acetone removes oils from the metal before welding, not aluminum oxide.
