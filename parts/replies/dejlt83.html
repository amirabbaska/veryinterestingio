    isolate the protein.

    And even in your super simplified step instructions, keep in mind that this one step is hard. Purifying a protein so that you don't have any other proteins the E. coli normally expresses typically requires several steps. Or you have to adjust the sequence in Step 2 to add a tag to make it easier to purify later, which you have to snip off anyway and you have to make sure adding this tag doesn't fuck with your protein's structure or activity.

    Even if you have the sequence and you can somehow manually add amino acids to a chain by hand, it still won't fold as it would in a cell. Protein folding is insane and I don't believe we're anywhere close to doing it synthetically.
