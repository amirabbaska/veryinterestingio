      Assuming OP was referring to visible light, the visible spectrum of light (the wavelength at which a photon can be observed by the human eye) is about 400-700nm, so objects of about that size or larger can block a photon (either by absorption or reflection) and effectively cast a shadow. Obviously this would be impossible to see with the naked eye, as it's on the order of a single photon.
