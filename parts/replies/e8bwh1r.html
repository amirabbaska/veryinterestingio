    • StaysAwakeAllWeek

      You can test this effect for yourself. If you hyperventilate for a minute or two you will clear out virtually all of the CO2 from your blood. You will find that you can hold your breath for 1-2 minutes afterwards with absolutely no urge to breathe while your body's CO2 levels return to normal. You can get the opposite by breathing the gas from a coke bottle (dilute it with air a bit or you'll get a nasty shock). If you hold that gas in your lungs for a few seconds so it can diffuse into your blood you will end up completely out of breath as if you just sprinted up 3 flights of stairs.
