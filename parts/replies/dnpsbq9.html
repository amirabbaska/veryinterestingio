      You may interested in this journal article summary. There are a lot of factors like age, sex, and genetics.

      To answer your first question, let me explain primary and secondary fat storage sites. Primary sites will be the first to store fat. This is usually the stomach and/or hips. Secondary sites is where the fat will go after the primary sites are (for lack of a better term) "full."

      Men with a fatter face or extremities have a lot fat in the secondary sites. This fat is actually easier to lose and will typically "go first" when the weight loss journey begins.

      Women with a fat stomach rather than hips could have a hormone imbalance, but it could also be genetic.
