        Yes, you can get CBD over the counter. No it doesn't work well as CBD with more THC and the actual terpenes in it. Through study of individual terpenes we have come to realize that those terpenes provide benefits. For example Linalool, found in lavender, coriander and others, exhibits anti-anxiety, antidepressant, sedative, anti-inflammatory, anti-epileptic and analgesic properties.

        CBD is found in cannabis and it is non-psychoactive.

        No, it will not show up in drug tests. Drug tests look for the metabolites that are present when THC exists in your bloodstream. Edit: Caveat, if you are ingesting over the counter CBD it will not show up on a drug test.
