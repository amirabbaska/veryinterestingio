      Just a few use cases I'd have:

      1. Literally see your fatigue curve in real time as expressed in 02 sats and pulse rate
      2. Higher dip in 02 = more intense rep or technique, letting you hone in on more explosive, powerful, or optimal technique, or better HIIT
      3. Lower dips in 02 = more optimized cardio technique (inverse of above)
      4. More objective measure of fatigue / cardiovascular load than "perceived intensity"
      5. Can track and aggregate for later analysis, and greater macro trends across your workouts / training / matches
