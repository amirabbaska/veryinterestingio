  • Qwazzerman

    Minor correction, but otariids (sea lions) are not vocal learners. Phocids (true seals) are, the most famous of which was Hoover at the New England Aquarium. However, the evolutionary distance between the two families is quite large: at least 25 million years.

    (Interestingly, sea lions are capable of entraining body movement to rhythms, including music.)
