    • in8nirvana

      According to Wikipedia, the Tardigrade (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tardigrade) can withstand temperatures between 1K and 420K (-272C to 150C). It can also survive a wide variety of what we might consider as extreme conditions (e.g. 30+ years without food and water, ionizing radiation 100s of times greater than the lethal dose for humans). However, it is NOT an extremophile since it merely survives in extreme conditions rather than thriving.

      I think there is a range of finding life that goes from thriving colonies to expired survivors. The thriving colonies point would be ~4.2 km (~125C) and the expired survivors point would be ~5.2 km (~150C).
