    Excellent answer; I learned a lot.

    I would also add that most small animals are prey animals and there is strong selection pressures for them to have the capability to be able to accelerate very quickly. A species that has evolved to move constantly, but at a lower speed, would be eaten by a predator that can accelerate quickly. Larger animals, like horses or moose, don't rely on the need to accelerate away from danger, since they're Too Big To Mess With^TM

    Ergo, to avoid predators, you need to accelerate. And when you're built for that, then it doesn't make sense to move around in a different way.
