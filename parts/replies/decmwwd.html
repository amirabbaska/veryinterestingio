    There are a few other parts here too with sand issues.

    1. Dunes are not static, they can move and shift overtime and would require monitoring and expensive excavation if they moved.

    2. Sand storms/wind carrying sand is essentially sandblasting. Over a short amount of time they would likely erode the surface of the panels (and everything else) but this would drastically reduce the efficiency of the panels and require expensive resurfacing/maintenance.

    3. Deserts have a huge range of temperature from day to night. This could cause damaged parts to break even faster.
