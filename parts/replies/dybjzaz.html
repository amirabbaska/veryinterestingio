    • mfb-

      (Due to the protons slowing down in the target lung, and the slow protons interacting more readily in the non-target lung.)

      That is a completely different energy range.

      Some cosmic rays have a huge amount of energy, collisions with nuclei lead to multiple secondary particles, which can again collide and produce even more particles, and so on. For these particles a thicker shield is harmful until it is thick enough to absorb everything. On the other hand you shield all the low energy stuff, and the thicker the shield the higher the energy has to be before anything goes through.
