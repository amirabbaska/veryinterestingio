    • stuthulhu

      We're explicitly not made of material from our sun, really. That material is still trapped in the sun by and large. Previous stars that have gone nova have seeded space with their elements. The sun is not going to go nova, and if it did we wouldn't be around to benefit from the seeding :)

      Note that this is not the only way other elements are produced. For instance, any radioactive decay is also producing a new element, and this is happening all over the place on earth at any given time.
