    • Tethalamin

      Yes it does. Reflection is absorption and re-emissions of photons by electrons. When an electron absorbs a photon, it is pushed to a higher energy state, which is what excitement is. It then relaxes and gives off a photon. The only difference between reflection and absorption is that the photon given off in reflection is the same wavelength and maintains coherence (amplitude is reduced), whereas an absorbed photon's energy is radiated off as lower energy photons that don't maintain coherence (thermal radiation).
