      • Strawawa

        Time dilation means that time is different in different reference frames. This means that, theoretically, from the inside of a black hole, a person would experience time passing much faster than a person would from outside of the influence of the black hole. One second on the inside of a black hole could be equivalent to 1 billion years on the outside.

      • ZippyDan

        The point is time is relevant. From the perspective of the blackhole itself, it is instantly destroyed (bang) as soon as it is formed (crunch). From a perspective outside the blackhole, nothing ever reaches the center of the blackhole before the universe "ends". Time flows at different rates depending on your reference frame.
