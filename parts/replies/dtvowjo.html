    • CDPool

      Sort of, The Red Book CD standard still ensures a good playing experience across all types of players even today, on both crappy and high quality players. Similar to web design you want to maintain user accessibility through compatibility with varying kinds of browser.

      It's just CD tech improved fairly quickly and a 2 second pre gap is not as essential today. You could probably get away with little to no pre gap at all on a decent player today but I'd still at the very least leave some amount space between.
