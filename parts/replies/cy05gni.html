    • melligator

      a) No, the label doesn't get anything for radio plays either. Performance royalties are a 50/50 split of an amount between artist(s) and label, or writer(s) and publisher.

      b) Producer royalties will be 1 or 2% usually alongside the artist's percentage but they can be anything agreed upon depending upon their involvement. They don't partake in performance royalties usually - I'm sure a producer could be registered so to receive performance royalties but it would have to be at the acquiescence of the label and artist.
