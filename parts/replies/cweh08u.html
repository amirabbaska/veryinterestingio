    Basically, a long time ago we used the vegidesimal system (base 20, opposed to our decimal system, base 10), and word like quatre-vingt-dix are just remains of this old system.
    Other crazy words existed, like vingt-et-dix (twenty and ten) for 30, deux-vingt (two-twenty) for 40, trois-vingt (three-twenty) for 60, etc. !

    You'll notice this doesn't explain why not use septante instead of soixante-dix, but hey, we never claimed to be entirely logical ! (There's probably a good reason hidden somewhere in history though)
