    And yes, your guess is correct---it's very complicated with a lot of ambiguity. The public, many reporters, and (grumble) Neil DeGrasse Tyson are convinced it's the impact and only the impact. But within the field of paleontology, we're actually split on the matter and mostly recognize that it's not one thing and only one thing.
