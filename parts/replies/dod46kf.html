  • ArenVaal

    Minor quibble: plastic explosives are not used in nuclear weapons. It has to be rigid, like TNT, HMX, or RDX, because the explosives have to be machined to a very precise shape in order to turn the multiple spherical shockwave created by the initiators into an imploding planar wave (ie, one that is the shame shape as the nuclear fuel)--otherwise, the fuel gets squeezed into a "star burst" shape, and "squirts" out at the points of the "Star burst," resulting in a dirty bomb, or at best a nuclear fizzle.
