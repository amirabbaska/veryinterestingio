      Maybe someone will post this to the top comment. Cheap vacuums make a lot of noise because they don't have sound dampening materials like premium vacs do.

      Vacuums are loud because they use high-speed motors with fans attached to move air. The motors make their own noise, the brush roller systems can also be noisy. Finally, air being rushed into a narrow duct system will also create noise by itself.

      The best vacs try to minimize all the reasons vacuums are noisy.
