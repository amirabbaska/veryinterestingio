    I was doing a transport for shelter animals once, and one of the cats had had an identical brother whom he hadn't seen in months. Just by chance, this completely-depressed cat had a cage that was half next to a mirror overnight.

    Completely different cat in the morning. So much more social and outgoing and had a much healthier appetite too. We'd noticed him playing with the mirror, but were distracted with our other transports (one of whom was, erm, requiring a lot of attention.) Took us a few hours to put it together that he'd seen "his brother" in the mirror and that would explain his behavioral changes.
