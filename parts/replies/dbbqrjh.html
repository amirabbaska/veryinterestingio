      Here you go;. Androgens are a class of hormones that cause masculinity(testosterone, dihydrotestosterone, androstenedione, ect). The presence of these androgens causes both the prostate and the seminal vesicles to produce fluids which help carry the sperm to the egg within the girl. Now the actual SPERM is produced in what's called the "seminepharous tubules" and it travels through this pathway within your balls (SNT-> vas deferins-> prostate-> seminal Ves-> urethra) to be mixed with prostatic fluid from the prostate and SV fluid from the SV and then ejaculated. As we get older testosterone and other androgen levels decrease and sperm production also diminishes.

      TLDR: For a normal healthy male in prime reproductive years the most probable factors influencing the quantity of your loads are; hydration, prior sexual stimulation and androgen quantity.
