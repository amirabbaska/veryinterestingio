    This doesn't quite work because the orbiting objects would no longer be spherical. For example, if you had a planet orbiting a star in a circular orbit, in another reference frame you see an ellipsoid planet orbiting an ellipsoid star in an elliptic orbit. This is not comparable to a spherical planet spherically orbiting a spherical star. The shape of the gravitational field of a spherical star is different from that of an ellipsoid star.
