  • myyusernameismeta

    THIS. And sometimes the damage they take is worse than the damage of aging. Most people's lungs will easily last their whole lifetime, but they last 5 years on average after transplantation. Bowel also doesn't last long. The theory is that because lungs and intestines are in contact with the outside world (and all the germs and foreign materials that are in the outside world), the immune system has to pay much closer attention to those organs, to fight off germs before there's a serious infection. That means the body quickly realizes that those organs are foreign and rejects those organs much faster, unfortunately.
