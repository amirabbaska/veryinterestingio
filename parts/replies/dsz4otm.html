    • DalvaMozzerX

      What you are referencing is bioavailability and drug absorption. These are quantified by measuring the concentration of a drug at a given dose in the blood when given orally, and dividing that by the concentration in the blood after intravenous administration. By definition, the bioavailability of a drug when given intravenously is 100% (i.e. 100% of a drug is present in the blood when directly infused into the blood)
