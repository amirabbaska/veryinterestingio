  • whistleridge

    The technology of the medium mattered too. Artists today have pencils, charcoal, pastels, paints, brushes, and above all paper and canvases that are orders of magnitude better than what was available in the past. Those don't make painting or drawing easier, but they do eliminate a significant barrier to expression. A modern comparison might be the difference between creating a photoshopped image on an old Pentium 1 running Windows 95, and creating a photoshopped image today - in theory, the same knowledge on both could produce the same image, but one makes it much easier than the other.
