  • MiffedMouse

    I'm not sure if Idaho Power can charge whatever they want in a practical sense, but there are legal limits. This is because they are a public utility, and almost all public utilities have more regulation than other industries.

    They are regulated by the Idaho Public Utilities Commission which has power under Title 61. The section of interest is 61-301, which requires that charges are just and reasonable (as determined by the IPUC).

    I'm not certain how often this power to regulate is actually used (call your congresspeople!) but it exists.
