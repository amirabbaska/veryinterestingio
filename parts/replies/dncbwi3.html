    Do-it-yourself experiment time for the 5 year old (and a parent)!

    • You'll need: 1 straw (needle), 1 knife or scissors (the tip of the needle), 1 piece of paper (the skin)
    • Take the paper and cut a short single line in it. About as wide as the straw is thick. This is the needle piercing the skin.
    • Now push the straw through the hole.
    • See! No paper is stuck in the straw.
    • Pull out the straw, and what was a round hole a second ago, is now mostly back to being a line.

    If you want to, you can put a band-aid on the paper.
