  • ---TheFierceDeity---

    Its not specifically cause it requires more skill or knowledge, at this stage it's almost because its artistic and will produce a unique blade. When you mass produce stuff they're generally very familiar, where as a hand-forged blade is going to be different from every other blade.

    This can be seen most obviously on Japanese forged blades with the "hamon" or those waves seen along the blade. Different black-smiths used different methods and clays when forging the blades and thus could produce an almost trademark pattern.

    Western blades also have tell-tale differences. They make the weapons more personal.
