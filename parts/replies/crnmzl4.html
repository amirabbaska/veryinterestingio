    You can actually have self supporting 100nm aluminium foils, and over a significant aperture, not sunglass sized, but a cm^2 is achievable, and you can certainly see though 100nm. We use very thin Al filters (and other metals) for XUV filters. Of course, if you so much as breath near them they break, and if you put your finger on them you've just got yourself an aluminium coated finger tip. But they can self support.
