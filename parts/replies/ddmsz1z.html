    A black hole is like a little pocket Universe with no way out.

    This is wrong. Virtual particles pop in and out of existence at the event horizon all the time. The whole notion of Hawking Radiation (the process by which black holes decay) is that virtual particles which would otherwise annihilate pop in and out on the event horizon with a very slight preference for the anti-matter side to go into the black hole (made of normal matter - the opposite if the black hole were made of anti-matter) while radiating matter with a similar process for light. The net effect of which is the black hole decaying over time - meaning a matter + anti-matter black hole colliding would in fact annihilate.
