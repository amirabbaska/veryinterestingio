      Don't forget the huge refugee crisis that will erupt when people in the crappy places try to go to the still-ok places. Bangladesh is on track to be in this state in 10 years, and that is one of the most densely populated places on earth. When your country can no longer produce crops or supply enough drinking water then there will be mass exodus.

      The European refugee crisis has arguably triggered Brexit and killed the EU with other nations arguing over free movement. That's a few million people - a drop in the ocean compared to what's coming.

      There will be people alive on this planet for a long time, but life as we know it may not survive another 50 years unless we find technological solutions. Small self-sufficient communities in remote areas will be fine - but imagine living without modern hospitals, factories and global trade and transport.
