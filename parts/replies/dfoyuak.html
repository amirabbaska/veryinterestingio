    A deer's natural predator is a wolf or mountain lion.

    If they see two glowing eyes coming towards them, it's already too late to run straight away. They can outrun their predators in a straight line, but not starting from a stop while the predators are already moving full speed. Therefore, they are programmed to wait until the last minute and dodge to the side, causing the predator to overshoot, at which point their superior speed and endurance will let them escape.

    Unfortunately, they're programmed by evolution for mountain lion speed and size, not car speed and size.
