    There's a third contributing factor: the absorption characteristics of the atmosphere. It may absorb/scatter certain frequencies of light so that they don't participate in making the rainbow. A thick atmosphere (Venus for example?) may scatter out all of the blue before it gets a chance to be refracted in the raindrops.
