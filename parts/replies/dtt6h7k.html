    Can you recommend a stretch or exercise to help this problem?

    • CodeBrownPT

      That's a very loaded question.

      How often does this happen? Are there certain movements/positions that cause it? Do you have other symptoms? Any other neck pain? Headaches/jaw/arm pain? Numbness or tingling?

      There's a lot that goes into prescribing exercises.

      In general, a lot of the muscles of the neck are actually anti-gravity, and don't actually benefit for more than a brief moment from stretching. The neck requires strengthening of the shoulder girdle/upper back, as well as some specific strengthening of muscles called your deep neck flexors. Which exercises are right for you depend on your injury and its irritability - ie working deep neck flexors too much too early can worsen headaches from your neck. There's also a huge posture component.

  • Crsestmn

    Dealing with this as we speak, bulging disc c6/7 pinching the nerve... Hurts like hell across the shoulder, down the arm, in the elbow and forearm... It's the worst, and really not much I can do about it...

    • CodeBrownPT

      There are tons of things you can do for it.

      A therapist can mobilize/manipulate (stretch joints), perform soft tissue (muscle) releases, dry needling, and prescribe exercises.

      There are some medications that can help reduce inflammation and nerve pain.

      General activity (like walking) as tolerated is important. Modifying activity to avoid irritating the nerve further is important.

      Other pain modifiers such as heat/ice/topical ointments can help slightly with pain so you can do more.
