        The spherical shell above the ball exerts zero gravity -- tugs from various location on the shell cancel out.
        Assuming constant density, mass of the sphere beneath the ball is density * 4/3 pi r^3 where r is the ball's distance from center.

        And gravity g is GM/r^2. Substituting above expression for mass:
        g = GM/r^2 = G(density * 4/3 pi r^3 )/r^2 =
        G density r .

        Gravity scales with r, the ball's distance from center. So of course at the center gravity is zero.
