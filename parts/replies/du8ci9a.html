• Nabeshein

    Turn off any surround sound options, and that should make it a bit better. With a surround sound system, sound effects and music come out of the left and right channels, and dialogue comes out of the center channel. Since your ttc had no center channel, out gets dropped, and you only get bleedthrough sound from left and right for dialog. Due to the way some stations handle sound, that won't help, as they're stripping out the center channel before it even gets to you, but it will help a LOT for dvds/blu-rays.

    Tl;dr: only use surround sound options if you have a surround sound hooked up, or your problem happens.
