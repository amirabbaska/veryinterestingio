      It's not actually the amount of info that makes it impossible. We actually have the technology to synthesize and assemble an entire bacterial genome from scratch.

      The problem arises when you try to make that genome functional. You need RNA polymerase to make RNA. You can't make that protein without RNA and a ribosome, but you can't make ribosomes without RNA polymerase and other ribosomes.
