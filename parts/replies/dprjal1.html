    Don't forget noethers theorem that states that symmetries in a system lead to an invariant (conserved) quantity. Pretty much the underpinning concept of a large amount of QM (particularly quantum numbers) as well as classical mech in a single theorem. Space-time translation invariance of a system implies energy-momentum conservation.
