    • adaminc

      https://jamanetwork.com/journals/jama/article-abstract/286369?redirect=true

      I found that it seems that first reaction is about 1/5th of a second after the flash happens. Then it can take upwards of 1/2 a second for a significant portion of the pupil to be covered.

      So the delay between the red eye reduction flash and the standard flash should be at least 1/2 a second.
