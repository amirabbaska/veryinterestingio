    Top Soil and nutrients wash away year by year especially on the edges. If you were using any sort of till year by year that only promotes top soil disruption and the cycle continues. One of the many reasons large scale agriculture is a downward spiral. Sure we have corn syrup for days, but that's about the only thing that will grow in that plot of sand and clay that has lost its nutrishous topsoil years ago and was only feed roundup and heavy ferts since. *I am an organic farmer and fixed the issue described above with a high break wall near ditches,thicker treeline around property and fields and let the soil build over time.
