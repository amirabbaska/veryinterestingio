    To add to this, companies making peanut butter are buying their ingredients in bulk. So, the sugar, oils, and any other ingredients are being bought by the truckload versus the homecook buying a bag or two. There's not much R&D overhead as peanut butter has been around for quite some time and the companies making the peanut butter have the resources to create new formulas quickly and cheaply, if they needed to. The processing equipment will all be the same, just different cleaning procedures to meet certain standards.
