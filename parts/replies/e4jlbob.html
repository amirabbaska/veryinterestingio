    This happens to me so frequently. For a piece I have played quite often (and should have memorized) I find that I still need the sheet music in front of me, although I barely reference it, to play the whole song.

    But pattern recognition is very real. And sometimes, even when there are notes I cannot easily identify (far above or below the staff), I quickly play them in the context of the song. But If I were to look at it out of context, it would take me considerably longer to tell you what note it is.
