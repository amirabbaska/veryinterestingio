    • onioning

      Not normally. Sometimes. It happens in other countries too. Sometimes raw cuts are brined. Most pork chops and loin steaks aren't brined or otherwise cured.

      You can tell by the label. If salt, water, and maybe sugar and other things are listed, it means they're brined. If it's not listed then they're not. Most raw pork sold is not salted (other than salami and such that's aged but not cooked).
