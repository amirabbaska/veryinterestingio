      • lasershooter

        With any of the gravity wave detecting instruments, you need both very precise knowledge about your laser as well as very precise control of the laser. Within that nest of reflecting and split beams, you have several specific systems. You have a source (with secondary beams split off to measure intensity and other parameters) Then you have timing and interferometry measurements to determine changes in distance between the satellites and rough absolute distance. Then you have the return path and path to the test mass (gold cube in the center) that you have to ensure that no matter how the satellite moves (from solar wind or station keeping) that you are always hitting the test mass squarely and independent of vibrations within the satellite from other systems.
