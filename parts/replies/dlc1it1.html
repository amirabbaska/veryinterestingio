Internships in the U.S. are like a horrible sieve that holds back poor and disadvantaged people. Not everyone can afford to take on an unpaid internship, unless they're lucky enough to live with their parents and have an internship in the same area, or have parents who are wealthy enough to just pay for rent and living expenses, or to have a second job that doesn't interfere with the internship schedule and also provides enough money to live off of. Working for free is very expensive.
