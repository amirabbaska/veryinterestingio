    yep. plus one to this explanation. IAA is one of a class of plant hormones called Auxins which, among other things, promote cell elongation. If you express that hormone highly on one side of a cell, it elongates more on that side and effectively steers away, allowing a stem to tilt leaves and flowers depending on the direction of light (or other inputs)
