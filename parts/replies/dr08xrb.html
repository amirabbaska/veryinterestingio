    • buried_treasure

      Evolution did not stop. Evolution is not prevented from affecting the human brain by magical force fields.

      Absolutely correct.

      However we have no direct evidence about the levels of intelligence of early homo sapiens (as opposed to that of other hominids, who do appear to have all been less intelligent than us with the possible exception of h. neanderthalenis).

      Therefore all three of the following possibilities are equally likely:

      • early humans had the same level of intelligence as modern humans
      • early humans had a higher level of intelligence than modern humans
      • early humans had a lower level of intelligence than modern humans

      However given that any radical departure from the norm in intelligence would likely have resulted in some kind of cultural record in found artifacts, the most likely conclusion is that average human intelligence has not significantly altered in the evolutionary lifetime of our species.
