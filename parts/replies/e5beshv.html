    Without too much detail, it had to do with the proteins charge. Using copper bowls releases tiny amounts of copper ions. These are charged particles that interact with the proteins and change how they behave slightly. You can get the same effect by adding a tiny amount of copper, or by adding cream of tartar (which lowers the pH and also affects protein charge).
