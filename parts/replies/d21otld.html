    A much simpler proof is a proof by counterexample:

    Hypothesis: The cap of a ball-point pen is intended to prevent the pen from drying out

    Proof by counterexample:

    1) Not all ball-point pens have caps, some are click pens.

    2) Click ball-point pens do not dry out faster than capped ball-point pens

    3) Therefore the cap on a ball-point pen is not intended to prevent the pen from drying out

    QED
