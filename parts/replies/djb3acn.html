    why some songs don't impress us initially, but can grow on us after repeated hearings.

    This is called the Mere Exposure Effect. Basically the more familiar you become with (exposed to) something the more you tend to like (tolerate) it.

    Music is interesting in that there are familiar elements nested within most songs, such as rhythms and tones.
