    There are a few critical things in the preservation of minority languages. One is media. Another is officialdom. If you can't interact with your government, schools, etc, there's huge pressure to be literate in the dominant tongue.

    But most important is culture, and which culture is dominant. If the majority language dominate the local culture, then the natural tendency is to switch to the majority language. If the local culture dominates, then majority language speakers are encouraged to learn the minority.
