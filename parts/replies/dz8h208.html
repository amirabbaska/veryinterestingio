    • Hypothesis_Null

      Actually the pushing thing hasn't failed. A device called a Magnetorquer is used to control an orbiting object's rotation/orientation by pushing off the Earth's magnetic field. Thus it doesn't need limited propellant, or flywheels with limits to how much angular momentum you can dump into them.

      A lot of systems are combo systems that use flywheels to adjust quickly, and use a magnetorquer over time to despin the wheels.

      Haven't gotten to pushing objects around based off the field, but we can rotate things with it.
