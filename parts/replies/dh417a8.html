    Just to add a small amount of extra detail, SO(3) is the Lie group of rotations in 3 dimensional real space. They are given by 3x3 orthogonal matrices with determinant 1.

    SU(2) is the Lie group of 2x2 unitary matrices with determinant 1, and is isomorphic to the 3-sphere S^3 . The 3-sphere (like the 2-sphere) is simply connected since any closed loop can be shrunk to a point.
