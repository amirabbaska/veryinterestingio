      • Black_hole_incarnate

        This is not what is meant by having an empathy switch, mimicking these emotions is something else altogether and is something at which those with aspd are adept. Rather, the empathy switch means that psychopaths do not categorically lack empathy as was once believed. It means that by default they do not empathize but when instructed to empathize, they can display levels of empathy almost indiscernible from those of nt's. It is also important to note that there are two types of empathy, cognitive and affective. Cognitive empathy is the cerebral ability to put oneself in another's shoes and affective ability is the negative emotional response that accompanies that awareness. The latter is what psychopaths typically lack.
