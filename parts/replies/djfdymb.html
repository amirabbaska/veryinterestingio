    Touring is really only lucrative to mainstream musicians and orchestras, able to fill entire stadiums with seats. Smaller musicians typically lose money due to the prevalence of "pay to play" agreements. And both advertising and merchandising are really only lucrative if a musician has enough broad appeal. I think we'll see more smaller musicians trying to garner larger payments from their few fans by selling one-off albums and EPs, similar to Wu-Tang's Once Upon a Time in Shaolin.
