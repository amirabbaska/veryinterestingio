    • blbd

      That's a subject of extremely intense controversy in the dental community.

      https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amalgam_(dentistry)

      So far it's never really been shown to be majorly dangerous in a relatively long period of use in many people with many studies.

      This doesn't prove it's entirely safe but does prove that whatever amount of unsafety it may have is pretty limited in scope or some smoking gun issues would have been found already.

      That's about the best you can do sometimes in science. Because it's difficult or impossible to prove a negative, but you can place boundaries on a positive over time with diligent effort.
