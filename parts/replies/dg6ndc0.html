    • Hemolies

      Sorry have to correct a bit. The 80 is absolutely not venous pressure. Both numbers are arterial. Your arteries are not like a rigid pipe, they are an elastic tube. When your heart beats it stretches the tube and brings the pressure to systolic ( top number). Blood flow from artery to capillary to vein is not instantaneous, so the pressure does not drop to zero between beats. Instead it drops to the bottom number, called diastolic.
