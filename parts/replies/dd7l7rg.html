    This is correct- but I'll add on that the neurons aren't "getting tired"; they become less sensitive ("inhibited") to repeated stimulus because sending the same message over and over again, at the same intensity, is almost always unhelpful or harmful. The failure of this mechanism, resulting in all or part of your brain transmitting the same signal over and over again, seems to be the cause of some seizure disorders.
