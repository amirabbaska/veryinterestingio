      That was more due the the gyroscopic effects of their rotary engines. Unlike radials where fixed pistons turning a crankshaft turns a prop, in a rotary engine the entire engine block is bolted to the propeller and spins around a fixed crankshaft, that amount of spinning mass generated serious gyroscopic torques.
