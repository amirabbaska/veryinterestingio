    • MemeMeUpFamilia

      Socialism is an economic system, the context of socialism is an entire society. Just because a company is a cooperative doesn't mean socialism. Is every family household in the world communist because the family owns everything in common? No. It's nonsensical and a useless application of the word.

      Basically, an economic system is the sum of all economic activities and social relations within a society.

      There is a Marxist argument that also shows why a cooperative isn't socialist, but its a bit complicated I think and requires its own ELI5. If you are still curious I would ask /r/communism101 or /r/socialism_101
