    The Alcubierre Drive doesn't really break the light barrier. The drive uses the bending of space to make space push you. The ship itself is actually not "moving" in space, but space itself is pushing the ship. There is no known limit on the expansion of space moving faster than c, only things within space. IIRC, precisely because of this, a ship using an Alcubierre Drive wouldn't even experience the special relativistic effects (at least not normally) you would see at the extreme end of the spectrum.

    Wormholes also wouldn't break the light barrier as you are just doing the equivalent of passing through a tunnel cut into a mountain instead of going all the way around.
