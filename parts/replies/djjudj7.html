    I find that it isn't so much the device, but what you're doing with it. If I read a book on my phone or pad, I generally doze off after awhile, just as I would with a paper book. But if I'm surfing click-bait on the internet, I'll stay awake. Similarly, if I do a Sudoku puzzle in bed (even with pencil & paper), I'll stay awake.

    So I think it's letting your mind relax by doing passive things (get sleepy) vs keeping your mind active by doing things that require input (stay awake for hours).
