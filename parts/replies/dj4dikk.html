      I mean, the truth is that we never really know how other people see us. But we're so hard on ourselves, it's unlikely anyone has ever seen us as badly as we feel at our worst. And everyone has their own complex beliefs and experiences and habits that inform how they see things. For everything you don't like about yourself, there's someone who loves it, or doesn't notice it at all. For everything you do like about yourself, there's someone who thinks it's annoying. And just because someone does or doesn't like something about you, doesn't mean it completely colors how they see you. My best friend is a complete slob and going to her apartment stresses me out.
