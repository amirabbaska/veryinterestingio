    As someone who has lower back pain from arthritis and disc issues this is totally true. The best position to lay in is on your back, with a pillow under your legs. The fetal position helps because it is elongating your spine, allowing your discs room to breath and let the spinal fluid flow openly. For when you're not sleeping and need a quick relief, "child's pose" is a great yoga position to help with pain momentarily. Also stretching the hamstrings of your legs helps as well.
