    • mechooseausernameno

      Not so far as I am aware. 95% of men's testosterone is from the testis, produced by Leydig cells in response to Luteinising Hormone (LH). LH comes from the anterior pituitary, as does FSH. Testosterone provides negative feedback which decreases the secretion of both, but only LH stimulates testosterone. Remaining 5% of androgens produced by adrenal gland.
