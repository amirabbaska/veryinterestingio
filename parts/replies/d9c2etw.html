      Im guessing this is just many parts of the brain interacting.

      You have an idea. You think about sharing or formulating this idea, and speech brain prepares a framework for your idea. You dont speak it but it still gets build because thinking happens ultra fast. Like you cant talk if formulating an idea or answer takes a minute. So every idea you focus on gets prepared into something resembling a sentence.

      Now, speech brain just generated an idea or sentence structure, and even tho it isnt ''used'' by your mouth the rest of the brain still get to spot the existence of this idea you just built. Analytical brain checks that idea and starts to ponder on it. It doesnt matter that you said that, or in fact didnt say that. The moment the speech structure is created the speech recongnion will spot it and send it to be analyzed like anything else you'd hear. So you ponder on that idea and react to it.

      Then it loops. Because reacting to this idea will prompt a new speech structure lightning fast, in case you need to speak it. Which you ''hear'' thus analyze thus react to. This is how you ''talk'' to yourself.

      If your brain is busy or on autopilot, then it may opt to forego that specific approach for somethimg more conceptual. Like thinking in numbers or sounds or ideas or a 3d image of something. If you drive and see a dude coming at you, then your brain is not reallu focusing on makig a valid statement thats syntaxically correct, it just wants to receive data from your senses and react to them instantly without speech brain getting in the way and sucking processing power. Because your brain cant actuaply multitask much, it just switch between all tasks super fast and lose efficiency doing so. This is also why you arent always talking to yourself, it only happens when you focus kn it and nothing else gets in the way or is more important.

      When I play music i visualise concepts and note locations but i dont have time to ''talk to myself'' because hiting the notes take less time than generating a speec structure and reacting to it. Same for when you make split decisions in a game. Afterward, you can take time to formulate an idea and react to it, and debate it with yourself. Because we think at lightning speed, but tslking to ourselves takes a bit longer since you need to wait for the speech structure to ''play out'' before the brain can hear it and react to it.

      This is why you cant interupt your own inner monologue because you know what you are going to say. Because you dont. Its being generated while the rest of the is on standby, waiting to react to the new speech strucure.
