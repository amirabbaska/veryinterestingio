      • skilliard7

        Local politicians love to overtax commercial property, because only a tiny portion of voters actually own commercial real estate, whereas a much larger portion own housing. Voters want to see quality schools, but at the same time they don't want to pay high property taxes.

        This is why commercial real estate almost always gets taxed the hardest. And this model won't work forever. As retailers start to die out as a result of online stores and delivery, and offices start going remote and workers work from home, eventually towns will need to find new ways to fund their public organizations such as schools.
