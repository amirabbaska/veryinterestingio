    Just a clarification on the movement of water. It's not a simple up and down at a steady rate. It's more of a sine wave... The rule of thumb I learnt for sailing is the rule of twelfths. High tide Hour 1 - 1/12th Hour 2 - 2/12ths Hour 3 - 3/12ths Hour 4 - 3/12ths Hour 5 - 2/12ths Hour 6 - 1/12th. Low tide, and so on. https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rule_of_twelfths
