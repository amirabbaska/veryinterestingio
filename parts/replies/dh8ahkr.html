    To expand on this a bit: This is called assimilation, where one sound becomes more like another sound. Your mouth has less repositioning to do when going from "o" to "n" rather than from "oo" to "n". Try rapidly alternating saying "doont" and "don't"; you'll probably find that "don't" is easier.
