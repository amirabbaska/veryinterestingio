    The draftsman would create the technical drawing on tracing paper (or similar). A bright light passed through the paper to turn the chemicals blue but was blocked by pencil or ink lines (the areas that remain white on the blueprint).

    I've done this exactly once in my life. It was interesting to do... but luckily we moved on to plotters and then large format printers which are so much easier to deal with.
