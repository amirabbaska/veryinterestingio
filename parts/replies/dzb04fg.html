    Had to scroll forever to find this. Analog video is frameless. There is only one pixel and its chroma and luminance adjust continuously. The illusion of frames come from that pixel being raster scanned over a 2D phosphor tube. If the recording were made with one moving pixel the whole process would be frameless.
