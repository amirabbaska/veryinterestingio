The actual experimentation is performed on those variables related to c and not on c itself.       Absolutely, it comes to c=1/(mu_0*epsilon_0)^0.5, where mu_0 is a constant related to the strength of a magnetic field and epsilon_0 related to the strength of an electric field, and we can readily measure both of those constants in a lab.
