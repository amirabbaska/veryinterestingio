      This varies greatly by jurisdiction in details, but what you are describing is the practice of forcibly increasing business expenses to lower the overall taxable earnings, needing to pay less corporate income tax. At a very high level, as a business owner, you would rather invest the money in the business rather than let it become profits you don't use/dividend and pay income tax. It basically comes down to whether it is best to spend the money now on business needs than to pay taxes but keep the remaining money available. Plus a lot of details like what is taxable, what is deductable, etc. It's an accounting maze.
