    If you're going to make that claim, you really ought to cite meta-analyses as opposed to a single study. Fortunately, what analyses I did find did support this notion, though the effects are small (sleep onset reduced by a few minutes, sleep length increased by a few minutes). Additionally, these results, though interesting, do nothing to explain the mechanisms by which melatonin has these effects, so bear that in mind.
