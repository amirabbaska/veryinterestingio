    Yep - it's always easier to lose weight initially because the calories that you cut/burn are a bigger proportion of your "bonus energy" for the day, after you spend your "just staying alive" energy. Eventually, it gets harder and harder to diet off or burn away more calories, because your base "just staying alive" needs go down and it becomes easier for energy in to equal energy out.
