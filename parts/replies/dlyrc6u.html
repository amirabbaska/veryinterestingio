    Detectors that look at multiple spectrums (usually around the UV to visible light to near infrared range) and detect in bands in divisions of 5 or 10 or so is called multispectral imaging.

    The same systems that perform this for 40 or more divisions are called Hyperspectral imaging systems.

    And then you have just crazy shit like Fourier Transform Infrared and Raman chemical spectroscopy.
