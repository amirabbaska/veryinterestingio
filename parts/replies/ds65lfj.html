      Yep, NAS is very big right now. Every year start aging 2 to 4 times what your estimated demand will be for the aged bottles when they mature. Then over the years, "steal" a little bit every year to create NAS bottles to meet current demand. Something like Talisker Storm will likely be:

      20% 4 Year / 20% 6 Year / 20% 8 Year / 20% 10 Year / 10% 12 Year / 10% 14 Year

      Either that, or take some 4 year out of the normal barrels and put it in a sherry cask for 2 additional years and sell it that way. Lots to options, but even that takes years of advance planning and stock control.
