    • TyphoonOne

      Basically both the Roche Limit and the Hill Sphere are proper boundary phenomenon, but cease to be distinct things once you're considering gravitational field dynamics instead of patched conics. Essentially, the smaller the distance between the two bodies, the less accurate our standard 2-Body approximations are, and orbits begin changing in very unpredictable ways. This is because the influence of Extra bodies on the gravity field around the bodies becomes more significant.
