      • CheezyXenomorph

        It's also an adaptation in terms of how they feed.

        Rather than swallow a jellyfish then move it straight into the stomach along with potentially seawater (which is not good) - the turtle swallows lots of jellyfish and keeps them all in their oesophagus. When that's full they squeeze all of the sea water out, and then move the jellyfish into the stomach.
