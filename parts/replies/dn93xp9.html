    • mofo69extreme

      Are you familiar with Gauss' law? By measuring the electric or gravitational field's flux through some surface, you can determine the charge/mass within that surface. This way you can non-locally detect the charge or mass within the surface. This is sort of the heart of the no-hair theorem: black holes only have properties determined by something like Gauss' law (as well as spacetime properties like linear and angular momentum).
