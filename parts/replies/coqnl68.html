    • broezellbub

      The difference is that the CJ4 oil is low ash oil. Ash was used in CI4 oils to combat or neutralize acidity caused by the high sulphur fuel/combustion products that slipped past the rings and ended up in the pan.

      Soot in the EGR system is just unburned fuel and particulate matter. EGR will continue to play a large part in reducing emissions until they can really improve the DEF system like you say. EGR is used to reduce sky rocketing combustion chamber temperatures caused by two or more flame fronts colliding, which in turn reduces the NOX generated.
