      I'm sorry but I believe you have been misled. I work in a psych hospital (am actually at work now), and signing a release IN NO WAY guarantees release in 24-48 hours. Once you have been admitted there is a mandatory 72 hour hold to make sure you are not a danger to yourself and others.

      What we do have are requests for releases. They typically have a five day period (excluding weekends and holidays), and at that point if the psychiatrist deems you unfit for discharge you go to court.

      I would not recommend allowing it to go that far, however. If the psychiatrist decides that they will not discharge, rescind the request for release immediately. Almost never does the patient win over the psychiatrist in court, and the courts have sentenced people up to three months that I've seen.

      Good luck and let me know if you have any more questions!
