    • monkeyselbo

      Nitrogen narcosis is also termed "the rapture of the deep." It is thought to occur because of the behavior of N2 in the body at high partial pressures, resembling somewhat the behavior of inhalation anesthetics (e.g., isoflurane) at lower pressures. These anesthetic gases have extremely high fat solubility and are thought to get into the brain tissues quite easily and disrupt neurotransmitter function. Interestingly, helium at even higher pressures (higher than the pressures at which N2 does this) displays this tendency, too.
