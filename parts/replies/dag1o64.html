      That's actually because your eyes aren't a perfect camera, but do all sorts of post-processing.

      Imagine it's a bright day, and you see something that kinda dark, just a bit, and has stripes. Your brain tells you "well since everything else is brightly lit, the thing has to be dark in color, or else I'd see it bright as well. Black and blue!

      Now it's night, pretty gloomy light. Everything is more or less dark. Your brain now sees a dress, it's dark, but brighter than the rest of the dark stuff around you. So brain says, huh that's a pretty light color, might even be white or gold, but it's difficult to make out in this illumination. White and Gold it is!
