    Ok, so what's an area? :)

    From an intuition standpoint, the area of a surface is something intuitive and straightforward, but from a foundational standpoint, you're going to want to define multiplication first, and area second, along the lines of:

    • The area of a rectangle is the product of the length of the sides.
    • The area of an arbitrary (bounded) surface is the lower limit of the sum of (finite) sets of disjoint covering rectangles.
