    Different things. Lactic Acid buildup has a very short duration and is the "burn" that people talk about during exercise.

    DOMS is felt between 4-48hrs after strenuous exercise. The time delay varies by load and person, which is why the OP is asking the question. Others have pointed out that it's still an area of research.
