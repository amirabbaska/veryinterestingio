    The reason for the reduced stability is because as the element number increases there's more electron screening (inner electrons preventing outer electrons from feeling as much attraction from the nucleus). Because of this the bonds aren't as strong. So even though the valence electron number is the same, they're further out from the nucleus and less tightly bound.
