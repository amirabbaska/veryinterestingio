    The / and NUL byte (0x00) are actually reserved on a *nix system, but with those exceptions, you're right that everything else can be escaped. As you say, it's dumb to do, but I've seen hacks where people put ANSI escape sequences in their file-names to get particular results (usually for hiding files from the listing by moving up a line, but sometimes for color or clearing the screen).
