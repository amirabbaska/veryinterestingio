    Does exercise lessen or prevent bone marrow fat?

    • big_pink_loser

      Yes, mechanical stimulation through exercise can lessen accumulation of bone marrow fat. Right now the exact mechanisms are unclear, but it's likely a combination of better caloric regulation as well as shifting the balance of differentiation towards osteoblasts that improve bone health with exercise. However you can't prevent it entirely as it is a natural process of aging that begins in adolescence.
