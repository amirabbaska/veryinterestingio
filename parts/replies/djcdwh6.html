    short answer, depends on what kind of cancer you want to fund and what country you live in. send me a PM and I can maybe give you some better suggestions

    long answer for those following along: always look into and check out a charity before donating. https://www.charitywatch.org/home is a really good resource for the american ones... some barely give any money raised to actual cancer research...

    another thing is, where do you want your dollars to go. If you are passionate about a specific cancer type because you or a loved one is effected there are going to specific groups and institutes that do that kind of work and donating directly to them is going to have biggest impact than some of the fundraisers.

    here are a couple I really like:

    Canada --> http://thepmcf.ca/Ways-to-Give/Donate-Now

    USA --> https://www.cancerresearch.org/

    UK --> https://www.cancerresearchuk.org/support-us/donate (these people do amazing work)

    Aus --> https://donate.cancerwa.asn.au/donate/ (we have people in our group that get money from them to fund their research, mods please delete this link if this is not allowed as self promotion etc)
