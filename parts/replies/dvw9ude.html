    Fighters don't use turbojets, they use low-bypass turbofans - e.g. the EJ200 for the Eurofighter Typhoon.

    Edit: Also, afterburners don't introduce an additional stage of compression. That wouldn't make any sense thermodynamically since the point is to reheat the exhaust gas to be able to expand it more, not compress it further.
