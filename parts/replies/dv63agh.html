      • Varean

        Kind of, the idea is to take your body off glucose which is the most easily used fuel source as it doesn't have to spend as much energy/time to convert it to usable fuel.

        Unused Glucose is converted into fat, so the idea with the Keto diet is to cut out sugar, bread and anything else that converts straight to glucose and just consume fat. This means your body has to adapt and start focusing on burning fat for energy. When it runs out of the food you eat it goes to the next available source, the fat on your body.

        To prevent muscle from being used, they always suggest to pair the Keto diet with some form of light resistance training.
