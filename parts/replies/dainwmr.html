    • telperos

      Caffeine is the most widely consumed psychoactive substance worldwide. As such, it might generate a certain dependency. Fortunately for many of us the effects of caffeine are more of an alertness thing than anything elseâ€”yet some of us will be very, very cranky without the usual dose of caffeine, especially in the morning. Caffeine withdrawal, like any other dependency-generating drug, may have physical manifestations other than mood alterations (there was a time when I would develop a mild migraine if i skipped my morning coffee). However, by the time dependency begins to be an issue the taste has been acquired and integrated into a behavioral pattern that triggers the reward pathways of the brain: their lack of stimulation upon caffeine withdrawal is what causes the withdrawal symptoms.
