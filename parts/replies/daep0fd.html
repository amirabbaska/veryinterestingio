      It might be better to explain that negative exponents are defined that way to satisfy the multiplicative property: a^b a^c = a^b+c. So 3^-k 3^k = 3^(k-k)= 1. Thus 3^-k = 1/3^k. This is the more general explanation that also covers fractional exponents, and is a parallel for a lot of how mathematics is created: Definitions are extended to satisfy existing useful properties.
