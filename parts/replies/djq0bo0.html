    I worked in a pharmacy for a bit and you're dead on the money. It didn't make sense to stock every dose of some medications when some people could take 2-3 pills, or someone could take half.

    One weird one is the higher doses of Synthroid. We could not order it in a bottle size that made sense. It either came in a 10ct bottle, or 1,200 ct.
