    Pirate stations will often try to avoid detection by having their transmitter in one location, the "studio" somewhere else and a radio link making several hops via other locations to the transmitter. The authorities can easily find the transmitter and may disable/confiscate it, but will have more difficulty finding the studio.

    In the UK it is OfCom that deals with pirate radio and they are reported to have shut down 400 pirate stations in the two years up to 2015.
