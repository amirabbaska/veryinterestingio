        What frustrates me the most is investment in fossil fuel exploration, we already know and have shown that if we use the fossil fuel we currently know about then we will be in trouble.

        I know you're coming at this from the climate change perspective, but you're discounting the trouble that we would face immediately by not investing in fossil fuel extraction. Energy needs, especially in developing countries, cannot be met without increased fossil fuel use in the coming decades.

        There are two major disruptions that must be balanced at the global scale - the decrease in fossil fuel use must happen in order to manage climate change impacts, but that decrease must be managed effectively in order to manage geopolitical stability. It's a trade-off between near-to-medium term disruption and medium-to longer-term disruption.
