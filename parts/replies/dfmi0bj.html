    • IronBear76

      The Norse in Greenland fed themselves primarily off of herding and were heavily dependent on imported wood and metals. The Inuit knew how to fish, whale, make clothes, and gather other foods using just resources that are available locally in Greenland.

      Without the walrus ivory, the Norse had nothing to trade the European traders willing to come out that far. And without the metal and wood to trade the Norse could not survive off the land like the Inuit.
