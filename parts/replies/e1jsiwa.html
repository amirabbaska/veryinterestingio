  • Harsimaja

    Essentially the operative word, which takes the stress for this reason, is the verb, so contracting it away misplaces the emphasis by putting them on an equal footing. You're already leaving out the rest of the arguments, to leave out some of the verb itself so no stress can fall on it is a problem. With "I am not" the stress falls on "not" - both phonetically and semantically this is the operative word (in many languages, e.g. Finnic Ines, "not" is a modal verb of its own rather than an adverb).
