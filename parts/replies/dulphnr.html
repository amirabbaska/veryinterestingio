    • Wjyosn

      Move, yes.

      Move continuously? no.

      Assuming the outside magnet is stationary, there will be an equilibrium state that it will move to then stay in. Wherever the forces of repulsion balance each other out. It's possible to find a sweet spot that creates a pattern that takes a while before it settles into equilibrium, but probably not particularly likely in your set up.

      The outer magnet will have to either move or have its poles switched back and forth (such as with alternating current) to be able to create a cyclical movement in the fan.
