      This particular study that you linked has been popping up a lot lately. The problem I have with it is that it has not been peer reviewed yet, since the study has not yet run its course. They say they expect to publish somewhere around late 2017.

      Here is the study itself.

      The rodents were exposed for 10-minute on, 10-minute off increments, totaling just over 9 hours a day from before birth through 2 years of age.

      NTP found low incidences of tumors in the brains and hearts of male rats, but not in female rats. Studies in mice are continuing.

      The complete results from all the rat and mice studies will be available for peer review and public comment by the end of 2017.

      I think it's worth waiting for the study to be completed and for it to be peer reviewed before we start drawing conclusions.
