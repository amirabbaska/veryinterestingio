    I used to breathe in cadence with my running...in for two steps, out for two steps. I frequently got stitches in my side. I switched to three-step breathing with a faster foot cadence and the stitches all but disappeared. Then I learned to just focus on my footfall and let breathing take care of itself, and I never get stitches when running.
