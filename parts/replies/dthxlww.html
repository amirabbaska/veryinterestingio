    How does one measure grip strength? As a musician (bass, guitar, and piano) I'm interested where mine ranks.

    • thetreece

      With a grip dynamometer. You can get a decent quality on Amazon for like 30 bucks or so. Or, if you're a student, go over to your exercise science building/department and ask around if you can test your grip on a dynamometer. They would more than likely be happy to do it.

      The most I've hit was like 151 lbs with my right hand, and 132 on my left. The 151 was sort of a one time thing though, I usually hit 130s. I have a 585 lb deadlift and have pulled 445 lbs with a double overhand grip. Mark Henry has easily maxed out a 300 lb grip dynamometer, and I'm sure other top strongman competitors can do the same. Head over to r/griptraining if you're interested in becoming a gripster.
