  • smashingmellons

    All numbers are in Filipino except for multiples of 10 but only from 20. For example, 10 is sampu which is Filipino but 20 is bente (or veinte in Spanish). After that all multiples of 10s are in Spanish. (30 = treinta, 40 = cuarenta, etc.) The rest are mixed with Filipino (21 = benteuno, 22 = bentedos).

    When you're dealing with money, all numbers after 19 also become Spanish.

    We also tell time in Spanish.
