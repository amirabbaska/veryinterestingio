    • Sk1rm1sh

      I think it's been mentioned but your alternatives are a high-density material formed at high pressures as in the core of a neutron star, or a low density neutron gas.

      Neutrons don't bind together alone under "normal" (ie. lower than neutron star core density) situations, they only form atomic nuclei with the presence of a roughly equal amount of protons which under normal circumstances will be surrounded by the same amount of electrons.
