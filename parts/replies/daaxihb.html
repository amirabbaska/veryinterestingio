  • hans_molemanny

    There is a book called "A whole new mind: why right brainers will rule the world" by Daniel Pink. He argues that as we outsource more and more of the left brain (analytical) jobs to other countries and artificial intelligence, right brain (creative/empathic) roles will have more value placed upon them. Examples of left brain/ analytical jobs; accounting, farming, factory work. Examples of right brain/creative jobs; Sales, arts, counselling etc... basically anything that involves creativity, emotion and intuition.
