    These pipes, called 'standpipes', are located on every floor and by building codes ( depending on the country) define how much water needs to be delivered to each floor. The standpipes my use existing water stored on a rooftop in a highrise, but typically their is an exterior connection on the ground floor where a fire engine with a large capacity pump will push water up to every floor.
