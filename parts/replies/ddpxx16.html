      • m0therrussian

        Even if we assume same size and body fat for male/female, women have a lower level of alcohol dehydrogenase which is an enzyme that breaks down alcohol in the stomach.

        Basically, booze is absorbed best in the small intestine, the longer it stays in the stomach, the more alcohol dehydrogenase has time to break it down which causes slower absorption into the blood and lower BAC.

        FYI: Also why drinking on an empty stomach gets you wasted. Food holds alcohol in the stomach longer for more of it to break down through first-pass metabolism.

        ELI5 - Women's stomachs aren't as good at dealing with alcohol due to lower levels of an enzyme.
