    Also, the "shortage" is a side effect of how mining companies plan for the future. At any given time, our proven reserves of a material generally range between 30-100 years, as it is not economical to continue searching for more after that point has been reached.

    The current fears of a shortage are caused mostly because the US filled global reserves decades ago, and has been sitting on it as generally happens with large excesses of a natural resource. Exploration has been nonexistent as we really don't need more for a while, though that may shift over time.
