    Actually the overkill hypothesis, that was generally accepted in the past is now considered to be unlikely. More dating on the extinctions has placed them both well before and well after human arrival meaning that we are not the cause. The solution that is most accepted now is that climate change in the Younger Dyras warming period forced the species and their food sources to extinction.
