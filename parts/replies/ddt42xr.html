      For one thing, whale sharks are nonagressive filter feeders (like baleen whales - hence the name). They aren't prone to eating their tankmates like big predatory sharks.

      They are also very slow, unlike white sharks which are always on the move. While whale sharks also have to keep swimming at all times in order to breathe, they do so at a much more leisurely pace (no ramming into the glass).
