    • b4ss_f4c3

      Its a certification granted from CAADE (California Association Alcohol/Drug Education) that enables me to be a SUD (substance use disorder, which is the clinical term for addiction) counselor. A counselor helps addicts through individual and group counseling, as well as all the aspects of case management (writing notes about the clients progress, providing referrals to social and government resources, etc.) Basically its like a degree that qualifies me to help people with addiction. It's obtained by completing a certain amount of units in addiction education classes, completing a set amount internship hours, and passing a state exam.
