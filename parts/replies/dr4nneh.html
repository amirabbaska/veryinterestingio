    This isn't exactly right. Platelets have primarily COX 1 which is irreversibly inhibited by Aspirin and reversibly inhibited by Ibuprofen. COX 2 is inhibited in the same manner but is present primarily in vascular endothelial cells. These can regenerate COX 2 but platelets will not regenerate their COX 1. COX 2 is usually responsible for inflammation/pain which is why COX 2 selectivity will help with inflammation/pain but not with your heart.
