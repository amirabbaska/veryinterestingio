    Lots of things cause High blood pressure, and your body (and meds) uses all those mechanisms to lower BP.

    For example, if your blood pressure is high, your body will basically pee out some of it. We can actually give drugs that exacberate that effect, causing you to pee a lot every day (called diuretics).

    Your body (kidney) will also stop making enzymes that causes vasoconstriction when you have a high blood pressure. To help with that, we also give drugs that inhibit enzymes in that pathway. This prevents constriction.

    We can also give drugs that directly dilate the vessels, such as CCBs or Nitroglycerin.
