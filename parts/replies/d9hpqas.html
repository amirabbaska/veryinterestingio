    This is really cool. I sometimes work with the CARIBU source at ANL, for which you guys provide the 252Cf.

    One technical point: I'm not sure that "some nuclei that should not be able to fission will suddenly fission without any apparent cause" is quite accurate. As you've said, the way spontaneous fission works is not really a mystery: a neutron tunnels out of one nucleus and if there are a lot of fissionable nuclei around, then some of those other guys fission.

    The spontaneous nature of the fission has to do with the source of the neutrons. 252Cf will fission just as well whether you shoot a lot of neutrons at it or wait for neutrons to tunnel out.

    But, as you say, this is a pretty sticky concept to explain :)
