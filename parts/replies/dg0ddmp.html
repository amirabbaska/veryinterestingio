    Is piebaldism the same as vitiligo?

    • OperaRiot

      It looks similar, but piebaldism starts way early in embryonic development with issues related to neural crest cell migration (when an organism is pretty much just a tube of barely-differentiated cells)--- the melanocytes just never get to work making pigment in the white patches. Vitiligo involves formerly "normal" cells ceasing to produce pigment, and people can develop vitiligo as late as midlife.
