      • 7LeagueBoots

        Other plants move at similar speeds. Look at sensitive plants and false sensitive plants. They are not carnivorous but they have rapidly moving leaves that fold up when disturbed. Many, many plants open and close their flowers in the morning and evening, some within a few minutes. A wide range of plants change their angle during the day to stay facing the sun and vines also actively seek out structures to climb on and can do so quite rapidly.

        The mechanism for movement in plants, even rapid movement is widespread across a number of different species. This suggests that it is either very easy to evolve (if it is independently evolved in all those different lineages) or that it is an ancestral trait that some plants have lost or have in a reduced capacity.

  • Meases_Pieces

    To add on, we can see a spectrum of carnivory in plants. there are plants that do advantageously harness the nutrients of decomposing animals but do not have any particular structures or mechanisms that indicate seeking out prey. One could extrapolate how moving from an ability to simply digest surrounding animal matter, random mutations could allow individuals in a population to attract those animals to their surround more frequently. The species would hone various small mutations that arose within the population over time.
