    • SherbetHead2010

      I've never thought of it that way! That is pretty poetic. You could say that melatonin is responsible for sleep, DMT is possibly responsible for dreaming, and adrenaline is responsible for alertness. Funny thing is, melatonin and DMT are almost identical, chemically speaking, to serotonin. While we're at it, have a look at psilocin, the active component of magic mushrooms.

      Meanwhile, adrenaline is very structurally similar to dopamine. Similarly, check out the structure of amphetamine.

      Basically all of the chemicals responsible for our mood are minor variations of 2 basic structures: catecholamines and tryptamines.

      I'm leaving out opiates, but I really think they are more a physical feeling than mood or emotion.
