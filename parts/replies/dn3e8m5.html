    Another thing to keep in mind is that in many of the older Germanic languages they categorized colors differently than we do today. They seem to have categorized colors based not on hue but on perceived warmth or coolness. Dr. Jackson Crawford explains some of this in his Youtube video on Colors in Old Norse. You may also want to read his paper Bleikr, Gulr, and the Categorization of Color in Old Norse.
