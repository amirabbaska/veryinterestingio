    • stoppage_time

      Rotating shifts are pretty bad from a health perspective, unfortunately. At the extreme end, medical professionals are starting to recognize something called shift work sleep disorder. But all is not lost--if you can, talk with someone in sleep medicine to see what you can do given your schedule. For example, shifting your sleep over a few days can be less harmful than suddenly changing your sleep schedule. There are also some good results using light therapy BUT definitely talk to a doctor before you try that one on your own.
