    • Red_Icnivad

      It really depends on the specific food, and how it is processed. Even among a specific type of food, they aren't all processed the same. For example, you can buy ultra-pasteurized half and half packets that don't need any refrigeration at all, while normal half and half still needs to be refrigerated even before you open them.

      Mayonnaise is unique not because it is jarred, but because it has a high vinegar content, which resists bacteria growth. Note that this doesn't completely stop bacteria growth, just slows it. If you go through mayonnaise slowly, (like I do) you might still want to refrigerate it.
