    He is in a tv series

    He is on tv

    He is on a tv show

    It all depends on context but all of those can be said and they all generally denote the same concept. "In a tv series" means a recurring actor in a weekly show. "On tv" can mean one time or recurring, depending on the context, but usually as a one time meaning it means now. "Turn to channel 9. Mom's on tv!" If it doesn't mean one time, "he's on tv" can mean "he works in the tv industry as an actor." It still denotes this currently active thing. Much like you are also "in a play." Note no one ever says you are on a play. In or on tv. In movies. In plays. Never on movies or on plays.
