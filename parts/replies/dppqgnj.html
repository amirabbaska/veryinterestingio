  • classicsat

    The USA also has A and N assigned to them, likely for Army and Navy.

    Their morse code symbols are ".-" for A, and "-." for N. W is ".--", K is "-.-" (A and N with a "-" added).

    W,K,N,and A are used for amateur radio callsigns.
