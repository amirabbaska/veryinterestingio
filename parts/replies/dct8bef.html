    You generally have to use apple flavoring to get a strong enough apple taste for ice cream. Things like malic acid and hexyl acetate. You can extract these flavorings from natural sources and get "natural flavorings" but it is insufficient to take something like apple cider and reduce it to a syrup because it still won't impart enough of the flavor we associate with apple to the ice cream.
