    If it's a loud groaning that is usually when a valve is slightly open it may be a water hammer. One remedy is to drain the pipes by shutting off the water and opening all the faucets. The water lines usually extend several inches above the valves to trap air that should hopefully absorb vibrations. Eventually the air may be absorbed by the water reducing effectiveness.
