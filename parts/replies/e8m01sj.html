    Dirty rotary encoder. The volume knob isn’t really a “knob” in the sense that it outputs a low or high volume signal, it outputs “I’ve been turned” and “which direction”. If it is malfunctioning, it might get the direction wrong.

    The buttons on the wheel are discrete up and down. They either work, or they don’t.
