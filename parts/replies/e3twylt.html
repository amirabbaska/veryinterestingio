      Again, not my specialty (and I doubt I usually make this distinction), but this UFL webpage of food scientists (and others including wikipedia) differentiate between the words:

      • Taste refers strictly to the sense that happens from your taste buds in your mouth (mostly on your tongue but also soft palette, cheek) and detect the basic tastes (salty, sour, bitter, sweet or umami).
