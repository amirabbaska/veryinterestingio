    You're a bit off on one major point:

    GTIN14 is just a (14-digit) extension of UPC and EAN. It's an identifier for a specific product, just like UPC and EAN, but extended to include a "packaging level" which can be determined/decided by the company producing them.

    GS1 specifies the Application Identifiers, and GTIN14 is just one of the datapoints that can be identified with an AI (number 01, as I'm sure you know).
