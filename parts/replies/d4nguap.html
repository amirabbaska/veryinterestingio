        Sounds like some serious tongue tension. If I had to hazard a guess, I'd say your tongue placement is rather high. A high tongue cuts off some of the low/mid range resonance out of the voice, producing what some refer to as a "froggy" sound; additionally, the hyoglossus attaches at the hyoid bone, pulling the larynx upward. That could be causing your pain.
