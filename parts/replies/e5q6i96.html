Modern agriculture, using modern, non-organic processes, produces more food than an organic farm of the same size. Since the demand for food is huge, these less-efficient organic farms are actually less ethical as these farmers chose to produce less food than they could to meet the world's needs. Organic farms also harm the environment due to their inefficient use of land, since more total land is needed to produce organic food than a modern farm. It's a similar deal with the non-GMO trend. GMO crops produce more food per acre than non-GMO crops. Plus, there is really no good reason not to buy GMO foods similarly to why there is no good reason not to immunize your children.
