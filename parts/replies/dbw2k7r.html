    • -muse

      Declarative memory is generally the first to go. I don't have the book with me, but in "Physiology of Behaviour" experiments are described wherein a patient is asked to learn some kind of game. The more you play, the better you get. When after some period of time he is asked to play again, his performance remained better than when he first start playing, despite him not remembering ever having learned this game.
