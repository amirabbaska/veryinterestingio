      Yeah, this can easily be noticed by turning to look at the reverse side of the material, if it's porous, it will be brighter.

      So if your shirt gets wet, the side facing the sun will be darker, but the side facing away from the sun will be brighter, as light has penetrated the material and more of it exits the other side than it usually would if dry.
