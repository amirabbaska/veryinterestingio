    The flow rate is proportional to g^(1/2), and thus the time taken to empty an hourglass is proportional to g^(-1/2). This is not an exponential relation. (Indeed, exponential functions are finite everywhere, so an infinite value at g = 0 precludes any exponential relation immediately.)
