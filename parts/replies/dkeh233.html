    This is literally the same story applied to Tasmanians - they use the same method of dragging fire around - and it was proven they did have fire making capability. The source in the Wikipedia article is an observation from a Brit in 1880. Read the article - it lists all the known contacts with the Sentinelese. Lack of eye witness testimony is not the absence of a crime, and the lack of any successful contacts is not proof that they don't know how to make fire. The assertion is just speculation. The case with Tasmanians prove, it was totally possible for a casual racist observer to conflate portable fire transportation technology with the "primitive inability to make fire." The correct answer is no group of people have been found surviving in the world without fire.
