    While the bullet fragmenting does occur, I think OP's question has more do with the ceramics themselves shattering. This 'breaking the plates' diffuses a lot of the energy, hence why getting hit in a SAPI plate is a lot better for the underlying tissues than an equivalent thickness of say steel (gotta stop the bullet and waste as much of its kinetic energy too).

    Dropping them can 'pre-break' the ceramics and remove this capability from the material.
