    Root beer used to be made with sassafras root, which caused the foaming (actually brewed like beer, iirc). Sassafras was found to be carcinogenic, and when it was removed, a foaming agent was added to duplicate the foam of proper root beer.

    Also where the name sasparilla comes from.
