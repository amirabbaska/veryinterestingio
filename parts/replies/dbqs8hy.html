    When i was in 6th or 7th grade, it struck me that the differences between the squares were sequential odd numbers. Moreso, the difference of two squares came to be equal to twice the smaller square plus 1. Making it into an equation, I realized that I was basically taking (x+1)^2 - x^2 and getting 2x + 1. When you set the equal to eachother and FOIL out the former term, it becomes the right side of the equation.

    Sort of a neat little thing I discovered on my own just by thinking about numbers. Not really relevant to your question but I was proud of myself for at least a couple years for that one lol.
