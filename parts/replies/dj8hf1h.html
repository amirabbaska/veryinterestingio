  • HeatherPride

    What are the different types of allergies you mention?

    • seasaltandpepper

      There are broadly four types of hypersensitivity which have different mechanisms. In laymen's terms, most allergies are type I (food allergy, hay fever, etc.), but some are actually type IV reactions (many skin "allergies" like those to poison ivy and non-precious metals). What people refer to as "allergy to sun" can be either type IV (common) or type III (somewhat rare, lupus). And then there are certain types of "food allergy" which are not allergy at all, so some distinction needed to be made.
