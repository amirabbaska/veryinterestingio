    Right, getting an equation of the form f(x)=y can be difficult to impossible, but f(t)=(x,y) is pretty easy.

    Illustrator and Inkscape (among others) have sketching tools that take a mouse path and turn it into a NURBS curve, which is essentially piecewise Bezier. Beziers use the Bernstein basis, but if you prefer it is pretty straightforward to convert the output to the more common piecewise parametric power basis.
