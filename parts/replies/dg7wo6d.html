      Here and [here] (http://www.cnn.com/2014/07/30/tech/innovation/undersea-drones-revealing-oceans-secrets/)

      A space craft orbiting a moon or a planet doesn't deal with the hazards you will find under the sea (terrain, currents, animals).

      A rover on Mars can survive years using solar power and they are not on autopilot. They receive constant monitoring and commands from ground crews.

      Underwater vehicles would require to carry their own power source and remote communication would be very difficult. Radio waves do not travel well through good electrical conductors like salt water so most underwater transmissions (e.g. deep sea oil wells) rely on cables or acoustic transmitters.
