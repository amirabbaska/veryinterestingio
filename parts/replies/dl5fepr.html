    In the little that i've read about movie production, the movie is often not shot in anything even close to the order that it appears on the screen. Part of this is because the director / editor might change things in post-production, but a lot of it is just the logistics of getting the shots. If the star needs to be in a location 3 times in the movie, they just shoot all 3 scenes regardless of when the scenes appear in the movie timeline.

    So, if it turns out that doing a tattoo or other thing is time consuming or difficult, they just re-arrange the shooting schedule to get all those together.
