    Can we clarify what you mean by "shots"? Spirit (liquor) measures vary from country to country. In the UK it can mean either 25 or 35ml - both are legal measures. At home, it could mean a finger shot which is a completely variable amount.

    Other countries have 50ml or more spirit measures. Depending on who's reading they could get quite a skewed idea of the quantity involved when you say "24 shots within 1 hour".
