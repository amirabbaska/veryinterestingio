    • ArenVaal

      The warhead catching fire is a concern, but not like you might think. These warheads contain both radioactive material, and high explosives. If the HE cooks off, you suddenly have a massive dirty bomb--it won't go nuclear, but it will spread radioactive material into the surrounding environment.
