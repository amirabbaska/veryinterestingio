    Bonus: Ear popping is a real sound that's not just in your head! When your ears pop, your ear drums shake back and forth from the air rushing around them. It's a bit like the balloon springing back to its normal position. This movement of your ear drums actually produces sound waves. These sounds can come out of your ear and be heard by other people! A good way to make your ear pop is to swallow, as this tends to make those little tubes to open - this is why chewing gum on planes is good, as it gets you to make and swallow spit.

    So if you put your ear against someone else's and they swallow you'll be able to hear their ears pop!
