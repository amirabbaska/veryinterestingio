    The crazy thing is that 76% of the corn thats grown isn't used to feed people. 40% is used for ethanol and 36% is used as animal feed.

    We grow enough corn to feed 14 people per acre per year. The actual amount of corn that gets used for food is enough to feed 3 people per acre per year. Its a terribly inefficient system propped up by 20 billion dollar a year subsidies. It provides little nutrition and does huge damage to the environment.
