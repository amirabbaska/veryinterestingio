Greyhound65 A:

Most people have a circadian rhythm (internal clock) of 24hours plus or minus an hour. People who's internal clock are shorter tend to go to sleep earlier and feel more awake earlier in the morning, these are your morning people. People who have longer internal clocks are night people tend to have more problems waking up in the morning. There was a study done on mice showing this and that breeding mice with long circadian rhythms could produce offspring with even longer circadian rhythms. It's been a long time since I took neurophysiology though so someone else is going to have to look it up if they care.
