It doesn't matter how you consume it, DMT by itself will never get you high. The MAO in the body instantly neutralise the DMT and restricts it's ability to act as a neurotransmitter. DMT is NOT a complex chemical, and is near identical to almost all neurotransmitters like serotonin, dopamine. DMT is one of the simpler ones, making it a common metabolite, and always present in the body in massive quantities. Monamine oxydase(MAO) is the only reason everybody isn't tripping every second of the day. None of this is simply unknown theory, it is a basic principal in all psychopharmachology. There is no debate about this in the scientific community.

This is also why monoamine oxidase inhibitors can dangerously interact with so many things like milk, soy sauce, and cured meats. The most common side effect of an interaction is serotonin syndrome, where the central nervous system can no longer regulate body temperature. Hyperthernia can take years to fully recover from. You know... if you don't die. This makes ayahuasca one of the most dangerous drugs, and should only be taken under supervision after 2 weeks of a carefully regulated diet.

Smoking DMT will have no effect at all. The only version of DMT that can be absorbed and used by the body immediately and unchanged is 5meo-DMT. It can ingested, insulflated, injected, or freebased. N,N DMT needs to be oxidized before it can be absorbed, and needs to be burned.
