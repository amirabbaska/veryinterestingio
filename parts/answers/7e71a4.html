One other thing to keep in mind (in addition to the already posted stuff) is that solar cells in the 1960s were kind of terrible. Very heavy, and didn't put off much power. Probably a modern day voyage would have some solar cells, but in the 1960s? Forget about it. Especially when you need to go to the moon, and it's easier to carry some fuel cells than a heavy solar panel.
