BrianHotshot A:

Ads.

 

Take a YouTube video for example. Once a YouTuber gets enough subscribers and views on their videos, they have the option to enable ads. Have you ever seen an ad on a video? Yes, those are the ones.

 

What happens is that companies makes ads and pays YouTube to show them on their websites (and videos). When a video is posted, you can enable ads to show on the videos. When a viewer clicks the ad, the creator of the YouTuber will get a certain amount of money, and YouTube itself will get a certain amount of money.

 

In essence, the companies make the ad...pays YouTube to show them...viewers click on the ads....YouTube shares a small commission to the creator of the videos.
