Spitballing:

It's your brain that is keeping you from sleeping. When it is feeling overactive (e.g. due to anxiety, anticipation, caffeine) it is secreting hormones (e.g adrenalin) that are keeping you alert, and thus awake.

(Personally, I find that removing the focus from that inner hamster wheel, e.g. by listening to a podcast, is often helpful.)
