I just watched a documentary the other day on something similar to this. There is a golf course in Australia that has a good size (landlocked) pond as one of the hazards. One day a golfer went into the clubhouse and said he swore he saw a fin in the water. The management chalked it up to just a drunk guy seeing things.

After numerous complaints they finally started to look into it. Lo and behold there were a bunch of bull sharks in the pond.

I guess back in 90's 2011 the course got flooded really badly and a few bull sharks managed to make it from the river next to the course into the pond. Now they have signs warning golfers not to go fishing for their golf balls. Pretty wild.
