The gang activity in this area was crazy. I've personally watched people shoot at police cars, I had seen multiple drive-b,y shootings. I'd never walk home alone, because gangs didn't care what color your skin was, they recruited all kinds of people.

You might think as a non-american that it's excessive but, what choice are they given? They're just preventing gangs from growing.
