My favorite approximation (& mnemonic) is for Mil√º; the best rational approximation of pi with a denominator of four digits or fewer. It's valid for up to 6 decmal places. Goes something like this: Write the first 3 odd numbers (1 - 3 - 5), Duplicate them (1 - 1 - 3 - 3 - 5 - 5) Place the last 3 over the first three (355/113 = 3.1415929203...) Drop the digits occurring after '2' (355/113 ~= 3.141592; pi = 3.1415926535...)
