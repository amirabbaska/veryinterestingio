AngryEEng A:

There is more to it than this, but for all intents and purposes, the ridge-line pattern does not change over time (unless the fingers have been disfigured), but the distance between ridges and other minutiae does change due to the size of your finger changing (growth). That is why most biometric companies have both child and adult algorithms for fingerprint matching.
