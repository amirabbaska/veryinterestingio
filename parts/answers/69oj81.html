TLDR: support prevents dents allows only crushing which is smaller and harder to see

The lanes are sitting on top of concrete. When the ball hits the only part of the board that can move is right under the ball. Most dents (the bigger more noticeable ones) involve big area moving a little little, the wood can't do that. The ball can only crush the fibers right under it, making a smaller (harder to see) thing. smaller dent .
