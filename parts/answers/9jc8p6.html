That would be adrenaline (norepinephrine through your sympathetic nervous system and epinephrine through the adrenal medulla sympathetic pathway) in response to fear stimuli so that you fight or flight. You're blood vessels dilate and blood will go to extremities and skeletal muscle vascularature by Beta-2 adrenergic innervation. The blood filling these areas I do believe is that feeling (in terms of particular sensations, I don't necessarily relate to the particular sensation you describe, but this is the most likely explanation I could see).
