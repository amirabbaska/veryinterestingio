Your skin has receptors that send information through some nerve fibers to centres in your spinal cord and hence your brain. Activation of these centers help releasing endogenous morphine (as endorphin, enkephalin, ...) which make you feel happy. That's why acupuncture works as they aim to specifically stimulate these fibers to relieve the pain. That's also why when a child hit his/her hand their mom trying to relieve the pain by touching that area smoothly which stops him from crying.
