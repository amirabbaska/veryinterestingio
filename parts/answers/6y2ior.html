kagantx A:

The hurricanes that hit elsewhere are usually known as typhoons. And they tend to hit places like Japan, the Phillipines, and Australia. The reason California never gets hit is that the prevailing winds in the subtropical Northern Hemisphere blow from East to West. Systems that form in the Pacific Ocean are therefore pushed away from California.
