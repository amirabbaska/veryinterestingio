gmsteel A:

We have better understanding of how genetic and environmental factors cause cancers e.g. the BRCA1 and BRCA2 genes for breast cancer which allows for genetic screening.

We have better understanding of how different cancers spread and how the immune system responds (or doesn't).

We have better understanding of the efficacy of different drugs for targeting different cancers which allows for the development of better drugs. e.g. Dicycloplatin for prostate cancer and non-small-cell lung carcinoma.
