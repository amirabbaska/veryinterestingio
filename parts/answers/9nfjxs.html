This comment thread is a mess. ELI5:

Take a dry sponge and dip half of it in water. That half will expand, since it soaks up the water. The cork in the wine bottle is the same thing, one side gets wet due to wine splashing on it and the other stays dry. When you uncork it, the part that got wet will expand.
