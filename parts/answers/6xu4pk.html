Japan still has aristocratic families, even if they have a lot less power than they used to and lost their official titles. Edit: With Japan specifically it seems that women leave the royal family regardless of the status of who they marry and join that family. They are only allowed to be a member of the Royal family (with the titles) if they are unmarried or are married to a male of the Royal family.
