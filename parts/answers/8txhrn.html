lavamensch A:

It's mostly because of routing. Your wifi and your mobile data represent two completely different sources on the internet and it is unlikely that their connections (even to the same site) would follow the same path. There is nothing inherent in internet protocols that would allow the two connections to be easily coordinated. This would have to happen, otherwise you'd just be downloading the same data twice, not different parts you could then assemble on the receiving end.
