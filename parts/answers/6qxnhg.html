I suppose our solar system could indeed move into a large area of dust. However even the most dense nebulae are still not particularly dense with particle densities of at max 10^(4) particles per cubic centimeter. Compare that to the 10^(19) particles per cubic centimeter that the air we breathe consists of. Now I don't know if that would actually burn up our atmosphere, i don't think so, but I can't tell you with certainty.
