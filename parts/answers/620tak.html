CheeseCake1125 A:

Not sure about humans but there was a study from my psychology class that showed captive monkeys who've never seen snakes before do not instinctively fear snakes. However, when shown videos of alternating frames of scared monkeys and snakes vs. scared monkeys and flowers, the captive monkeys started fearing snakes but not the flowers. This could be evidence of prepared learning (easier to learn certain things than others)

Edit: the monkeys have never seen flowers prior to the video either
