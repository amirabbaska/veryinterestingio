peacefulpiranha A:

I believe the colors you're talking about are known as "phosphenes." Pressure on your eye causes your retinal ganglion cells to activate in a very similar way to how they activate as a response to light, so when you tightly shut, rub, or press on your eye, it stimulates your retina and causes the illusion that you are seeing little particles of light or different colors.

Source: https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phosphene
