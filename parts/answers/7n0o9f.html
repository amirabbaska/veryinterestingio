cdb03b A:

Auld Lang Syne is "Good Old Times" or "Times Long Past" in Scots, a language spoken in lowland Scotland that is a kind of a hybrid of Old and Middle English with remnants of Gaelic. It has morphed over the years to even include some modern English words and spellings.

It is a song talking about old friends, and the memories you have with them. u/Kotama does a good breakdown of the song verse by verse. It is basically about toasting to old memories and continued friendship.
