Petwins A:

Restaurant competition is a very class based thing. Restaurants that arent in the same class don't feel the need to compete because their customers arent there for the same reasons. Their advertisments are different, as is their food quality.

Mcdonalds direct competitors (varies regionally) are things like burger king, wendy's, etc. and they have kept drink prices down, but non direct competitors haven't felt that pressure.
