LectorV A:

In many cultures around the world, the woman's last name is "lost" when they marry, as they become part of the man's family. So, while a kid would know that his mom used to be Jane Doe, most people would only know her as Jane Smith.

This backfires when you are in a culture in which the names are added, like Hispanic cultures do. In Spain or Mexico the Jane Doe Byrd married John Smith Anderson, and their kid is James Smith Doe, so everybody knows little Jimmy's mom's maiden name.
