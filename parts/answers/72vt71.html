DudeWoody A:

Something about this- if you get an unidentified call and the person/machine on the other end asks "Hi, is this OP?" do not say "Yes." There have been complicated phishing scams where they record your verbal "yes" and create "recordings" of you agreeing to things with your credit card number, birthday, and phone number. Ask who is calling, and only if it is someone who you actually want to talk to, then proceed. Until you get a positive ID on who is calling, say as little as possible.
