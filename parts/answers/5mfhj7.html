Sweating is a means to cool the body temperature. Your skin has a lot of surface area, and sweat acts as a medium that cools when in contact with (usually cooler) air. Producing more sweat covers more surface area of the body, and therefore becomes more efficient at cooling. Individuals who are physically fit typically produce sweat because their bodies need to cool more efficiently. This doesn't exclude individuals who are overweight, however, because moving a lot of mass - whether it be muscle or fat - requires a lot of energy expenditure, produces heat, and requires more efficient cooling.
