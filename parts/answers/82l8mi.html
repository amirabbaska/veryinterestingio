the_real_bonoboboy A:

As several other folks have mentioned, yes many other species get STIs. What makes humans special is that many humans have sex a lot more than other species (i.e. we mate for more than just reproduction). While this happens in other species (bonobos, many other primates, cetaceans), there is still a magnitude of difference so the likelihood and associated risks with STIs are often greater.
