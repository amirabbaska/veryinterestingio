jrm2003 A:

Not a doctor, but my grandmother "died in her sleep" in a hospital. We knew it was coming for days. She didn't really do much for those days aside from occasionally reaching out. But by the last day she didn't move and wasn't conscious. Her pulse just kinda went from 90 to 0 from what I vaguely remember ( I was a little emotional so I wasn't watching the monitor) She was DNR.

I think that's what they call dying in your sleep. It's not an official thing as far as I can tell. Just a way to say that you weren't conscious in the moments before you stopped functioning.
