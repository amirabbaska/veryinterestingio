A lot of the responses I have seen are valid but I would like to mention that drugs have active metabolites that do not penetrate the Central Nervous System. For example, Amphetamine gets metabolized into 4-Hydroxyamphetamine via CYP2D6. The Hydroxy group makes the amphetamine molecule resemble a water-soluble monoamine named Tyramine and act as TAAR1 agonists although only peripheral effects occur as a result of an inability to penetrate the BBB.
