We view them through a lens that distorts their culture for our own purposes. The concept was initially born out of French and British colonization, and the fact that Europeans defined Middle Eastern culture in western terms. The result was that western explanations of non-western culture became the norm. The romanticism you mentioned was really a product of European colonization. The Middle East was a strange and beautiful place, ripe for the taking, and its people were different and exotic: perfect for exploitation. European Orientalism helped to classify and subjugate the natives.
