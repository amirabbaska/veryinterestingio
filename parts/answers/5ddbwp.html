7LeagueBoots A:

If I recall correctly, both centipedes and millipedes have only one gait. They can change the speed of it, but it is always the same gait.

Their legs work in pairs and it's a sequential ripple movement. It's been a long time since I read about their movement, but I seem to recall that they don't actually have a lot of control over the motion. It's a semi-automated movement.
