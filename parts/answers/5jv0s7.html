Both penile erections and ejaculations are under the control of the autonomic nervous system. The erection in a man's penis is controlled by the parasympathetic and ejaculation is under the control of the sympathetic nervous system. Upon reaching a threshold of neuronal stimulation from the penis the sympathetic nervous system is triggered and thus ejaculation commences.

EDIT (People complaining my original answer is too technical so here a simplified answer: Jizzing isn't controlled consciously, Your brain takes care of it for you in the background like it does with controlling your heart rate or breathing.)
