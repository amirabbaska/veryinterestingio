I believe they are the group in the news undergoing a split.

But the Kudokai group of Kitakyushu, whose unrivaled brutality has kept other groups out of Northern Kyushu, have never held such events for their community. Their tactics are so extreme that their very presence has earned Kitakyushu the nickname "The Somalia of Japan" by those living outside of the area.

High-level members of Kudokai were arrested in 2014 by a joint task-force of cops coming to KK from all around Japan, so it's anyone's guess how powerful they actually are right now.
