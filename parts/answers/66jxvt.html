ELI5 answer: In short, it enhances what's already there, which is why experienced recreational drug users always talk about "set & setting". The "set" is your mindset and the "setting" is your surroundings.

If you already have anxiety and stress or if you're in a setting which isn't enjoyable or just negative all-around, all of that is going to get amplified.

Conversely, if you're relaxed and having a good time and in a warm & comfortable place you're more likely to have a positive experience.
