Their survival is based upon swarm tactics. Huge numbers, low survival rates, and short lifespans. It's sorta like asking why bacteria choose to live in the way where you can wipe out millions of them by washing your hands. It is entirely driven by "eat and reproduce before I die."

It's a very viable tactic, and the reason why insects (particularly ants) are so successful. Well, other than the breeding with the ants because that's a bit specialized.
