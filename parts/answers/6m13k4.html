Chookypook A:

There is a link between kidney damage and burns- it can be fatal and/or cause renal failure. Burns and tissue damage flood your flowing blood with "exploded" cells, infection, dirt, general debris and gunk. The kidneys filter the blood of ions and water, and this mixture of "gunk" can damage kidneys. I am not a medical doctor- I only studied biomedical science so speak with your own doctor about this but it may be that you have some scarring and damage to your kidneys. https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3741002/ ( link to study I googled which is about kidney injury in burn patients)
