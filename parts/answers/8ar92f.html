DerCatzefragger A:

The pigment in your skin that gives you your color is called melanin. When you're exposed to the sun, your skin produces more melanin to protect you from the sun's radiation, this is a tan.

Some people don't have their melanin evenly distributed throughout their skin. Instead, it's clumped together into little spots. Freckles are just a tan in people whose melanin is clumped into spots.
