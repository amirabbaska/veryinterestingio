Reese_Tora A:

How would you know that any given business was hiring illegal immigrants in order to boycott them?

1: There are plenty of people who are first generation legal immigrants, you can't go by appearance, language, or mannerisms.

2: companies hiring illegals tend to do so in to positions that are not exactly customer facing (ie: farming, janitorial work, construction, factory workers, etc.) so you probably wouldn't see them anyway.

3: Some illegals use falsified information to appear to be citizens, so even the companies don't necessarily know they hired illegals either.

If it ever comes in to the news that a company has been using illegal labor, it's usually as part of a story where they were caught and the government is either detaining the illegal immigrants for deportation proceedings, or investigating the company in question to see if they can be fined(or both) so there's little point in further punishing them via boycotts.
