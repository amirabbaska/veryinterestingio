LunarXero A:

I see a lot of posts saying Asians are lactose intolerant - I want to add that most animals and (numbers vary on reports) 65-75% of humans are.

Lactose intolerant is the natural state, babies are designed to drink milk with an enzyme called lactase, and it's not normally produced after they grow a bit.

Some people produce it their whole lives, and those people aren't lactose intolerant, but it's actually not the norm. It is genetic so you see it passed along generations and clumped in ethnicities.
