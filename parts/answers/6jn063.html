Your body doesn't want to go without food, and unlike animals that comfortably go long periods of time without eating (like reptiles), going without food is merely possible, not normal. Your body starts to show signs of not having eaten way before it completely kills you. So that's your body telling you "I'd really rather be eating right now: if you can find food, you should."

I think of it in terms of a warning system akin to pain. When we get hurt, our body warns us through pain that something is wrong and that we should maybe address it. Pain isn't a useless reaction that we should wish away if we could.
