GenXCub A:

Conditioning. Have you heard of "Pavlov's dogs?" Ivan Pavlov conducted an experiment where he would feed dogs, but only after a bell rings. Eventually, the sound of the bell made the dogs salivate even if they weren't given food. They associated the bell with food and their mouths anticipated that.

This is also why you feel the need to go to the bathroom even more when you're near a bathroom.

And also why your mouth waters when you think of certain foods.
