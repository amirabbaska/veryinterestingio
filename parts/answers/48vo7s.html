Isleepzzz A:

NYC power plant operator here: Simply put

The district heating system is used for both heating and cooling of buildings where available. Using the steam generated by power plants and large boiler plants makes sense to building owners because they can heat without a boiler(don't have to buy gas, don't need an engineer to run it) and cool the building using an absorption chiller which uses the steam heat to generate chilled water instead of an electric chiller which would have an expensive electric bill for comfort cooling.
