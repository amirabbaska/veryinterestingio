We are now able to go to really cold environments, pull out the microbes, sequence their DNA, and search for better detergents that they make that work in cold water. This is a field known as "functional metagenomics". See, for example, https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3815412/. We are hunting microbes for new detergents! That's pretty cool.
