It's a Schelling point. An intuitive solution. Flashing twice is an unusual and obviously intentional action that'd be noticed, and would cause other drivers to pay more attention and be more alert by snapping them out of the routine of driving. Since it works, it became widespread by memetic infection - people saw others flash, and did it themselves.
