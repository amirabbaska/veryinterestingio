    Q:

    Are our bones sensitive to cold just like our teeth are?

    Snake_Staff_and_Star A:

    Yes. I'm a paramedic and a occasionally have to give fluids via IO (intraosseus/ "in the bone") infusion. Before hanging a bag of fluids a dose of numbing agent is used (Lidocaine) due to the temperature (and pressure) difference of the bone and the fluids causing excruciating pain if unblocked.
