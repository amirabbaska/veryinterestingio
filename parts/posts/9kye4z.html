    Tuatha-an A:

    Swimming isn't particularly easy for bipeds (without practice), it's very dissimilar to our normal walk cycle (unlike a dog/cat/horse/etc.). All great apes have this problem but many have been observed swimming, contrary to popular belief. Some even enjoy it.

    Although humans do have a basic diving reflex it's a bit disingenuous to call it "swimming" because babies obvious can't resurface and tread water with this reflex alone.
