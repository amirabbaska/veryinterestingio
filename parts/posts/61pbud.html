    km89 A:

    Those sites don't actually know that. Google does.

    When you visit sites, cookies are stored in your browser. Other sites have spaces for ads, but don't actually hard-code in the content of that ad. The ad provider looks at the cookies in your browser and serves you up an ad that fits what you've already been looking at.
