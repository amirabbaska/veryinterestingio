    Cockwombles A:

    It's the style. Like manga, you can learn it. The style is quite dated and sketchy, and not terribly artistic. I'm sure it has a historical reason to be like this, almost like graffiti.

    And also, most political cartoonists are older men who use pen and ink.

    You can tell girl handwriting, you can tell old man handwriting. It's a product of the time, medium, and style they feel comfortable doing.

    I notice this in a few mediums also. I work in architecture and all the hand drawers have the same scratchy old man handwriting and pen drawing.
