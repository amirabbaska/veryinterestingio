    Q:

    ELI5: If the greek root “mono” means one/single and the greek root “poly” means multiple/many, what does Monopoly mean when it’s directly translated?

    Red_AtNight A:

    The -poly suffix in Monopoly is from the Greek word πωλεῖν - pōleîn - which means "To sell." Essentially it means a single seller.
