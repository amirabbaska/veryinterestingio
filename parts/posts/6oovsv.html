    fiendlittlewing A:

    I'd just like to point out that adding 40 min to every day is a bigger deal (for humans) than it might seem.

    NASA employees who were on "Mars time" to work with the rovers had a multitude of issues. Basically like worsening jet lag.

    Perhaps living on Mars and syncing up the day/night cycle might help, but it appears that our brains are wired for 24hour days.

    I do know that submariners who work 6hrs on and 12 hours off in an 18hr "day" don't suffer similar effects, even when they lose track of the days and go months w/o seeing the sun. Apparently, just like jet lag, it's easier on the body to subtract hours than add them.
