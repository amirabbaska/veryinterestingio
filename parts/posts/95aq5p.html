    relaxasaurus_maximus A:

    There are a lot of different ways. One of them is that two chromosomes can fuse together and another is that a single chromosome can break into multiple (usually two) pieces. Both of these kind of events requires other things to happen so the newly fused/newly broken chromosomes are passed on and replicated properly, but both kinds of events have been documented in experimental systems
