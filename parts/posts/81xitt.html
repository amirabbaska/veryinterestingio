    Gigazwiebel A:

    Not on Earth. For the same reason that you can dry cloths at sub-freezing temperatures. The partial pressure of CO2 in the atmosphere is something like 0.003 atmospheres. According to this phase diagram: https://www.researchgate.net/figure/CO2-phase-diagram30-1-atm-01013-MPa_fig5_235954077 this requires a condensation temperature around -130°C.
