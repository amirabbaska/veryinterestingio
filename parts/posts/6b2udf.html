    Q:

    What is this newly discovered state of matter called supersolid?

    Hapankaali A:

    You are probably referring to recent experiments with cold atoms claiming to have found a supersolid phase. Such a phase combines crystalline properties with superfluidity. In an ordinary superfluid, such as a cold Helium-4, there is no such crystalline underlying structure.
