• What are we smelling when we smell the "new car smell"?

    NEW
    Q:

    ELI5: What are we smelling when we smell the "new car smell"?

    Whingdoodle A:

    Mostly you are smelling "outgassint" -- the various plastics and adhesives used to make car steadily emit small amounts of gases. They do this a lot when they're brand new, then it gradually tapers off, but the materials continue to outgas even years later when heated, like when the car is parked in the hot sun. The gases are a combination of the material itself and the solvents used in manufacturing. Some of them such as formaldehyde are somewhat toxic.
