    Q:

    ELI5: Why do people develop "crushes" on others and why're they hard to get over?

    melance A:

    A crush is on someone you have not had a relationship with (generally) and this means that you have to fill in any gaps in your knowledge of how the relationship would be. This leads to a fantasy of the ideal relationship with the person rather than a realistic one. This makes it hard to move beyond the crush because it will always seem to be a perfect relationship.
