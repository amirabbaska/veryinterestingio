    pjay321 A:

    Mucus membranes in the mouth and saliva contain antibodies that allow your immune system to recognize and kill bacteria. Your saliva also contains lysozyme, an enzyme which destroys bacterial call walls, and growth factors, soluble proteins that stimulate wound healing.
