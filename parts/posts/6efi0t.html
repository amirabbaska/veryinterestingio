    The testicles are formed in the abdomen where the ovaries are in a woman. They usually descend to where they normally are by the time an infant is 9 months old.

    The testicles still have nerves that are linked to the abdomen where they were originally formed. Therefore, contact with them causes the feelings in your stomach.

    Another reason is that there are a large number of nociceptors, or pain receptors, in and around the testicles and when all of these send the message to the brain at once, a large of amount of pain is perceived.
