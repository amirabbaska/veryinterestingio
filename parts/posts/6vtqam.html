    NewtypeInnovator A:

    Nah. A sleep cycle lasts around 90 mins and has 5 stages. Biological functions differ per stage. Allowing your body to through the entire cycle in maintaining your body's fitness as well as mental performance since it's during deeper sleep that your brain really processes memories.

    The time of day you sleep is important too. At night, you go through the deep sleep stages longer while lasting only a few mins in the earlier stages especially with succeeding sleep cycles.

    15 minute naps are only okay as naps. Taking another nap an hour later won't resume the previous sleep cycle so you lose the benefits of undergoing whole sleep cycles.
