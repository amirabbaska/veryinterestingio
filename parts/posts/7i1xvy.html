    KingOfAssumptions A:

    Yes, it can certainly be done. There is an interesting paper in Physical Review D that discusses the curvature of spacetime around current loops and solenoids: ArXiv:1504.00333.

    To do this, they study a numerical solution of the coupled Einstein-Maxwell equations, assuming cylindrical symmetry. The authors even propose an experimental setup that would be able to determine the effect of the gravitational field induced by the magnetic field by measuring the phase shift of light.
