    mcrn_marine A:

    The PirateBay specifically uses Cloudflare as their front-end. So you communicate with Cloudflare, not ThePirateBay web servers directly.

    Anyone who wants can download the entirety of the TPB site and host it themselves. So when one TPB back-end "mirror" is shut down, the IP is simply updated in Cloudflare to point to another back-end mirror hosted somewhere else. When that is shut down, another springs up. It's like playing whack-a-mole.
