• Physics Is there a maximum size for a raindrop?

    NEW
    Q:

    My 9yo joked about a really big raindrop and asked me what I thought about that. When i thought about it, I didn't had the answer to a maximum raindrop size.

    ramk13 A:

    Yes, because big raindrops will get broken up into smaller drops by the air flow/pressure at the front of the drop. Above about 5 mm, drops will break up.
