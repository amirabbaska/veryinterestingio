    Q:

    Why are lightning bolts blue?

    gnawthcam A:

    Our atmosphere is primarily composed of Nitrogen and Oxygen. Those gasses, when ionized, produce a purple and blue color respectively. This is the same effect that makes Neon gas glow red in signs.
