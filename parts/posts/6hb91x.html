    Q:

    How are feathers grown?

    skieezy A:

    They grow like this. They basically grow in like hair (the tip comes out first and the newest part is at the bottom), but are wrapped in a protective sheath. Once the feather grows out and matures the sheath deteriorates and the feather unfurls.
