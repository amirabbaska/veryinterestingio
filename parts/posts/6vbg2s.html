    Q:

    ELI5: in the statement "here's my 2 cents", why is it "2 cents"? not 1 or 3?

    AndyMandalore A:

    It's from the Bible. A widow offers 2 coins as an offering, compared to the other followers who offer larger sums. Jesus was more interested in the widow because the 2 coins were the total sum of her money, while the others offered larger sums, but were smaller portions of their wealth. I think it's supposed to imply "my ideas aren't worth much, but it is all I have, and I hope it's helpful". Hope this helped.
