    Q:

    ELI5: Why do we start to imitate how other people speak the longer we spend time with them?

    MrBrannfjell A:

    Because we want to be understod. We want the recipient to be able to percieve our message as we want them to. The better we match the language of those we speak to (like the accent/dialect, slang) the easier it is for both members of the conversation to understand each other.

    If you travel to another location where the majority speaks different than you, you are encouraged to adapt their way of talking, because it is easier to adapt to the culture than to make the culture adapt to you.

    The better you are at adapting your language to fit the recipients language, the better they will be able to percieve it the way you meant for it to be percieved.
