    Q:

    To clarify my point: Sometimes, when I wait tables, I think of throwing the dirty dishes into the air, creating a huge mess or just punching someone. I dont hate the job at all. These kinda thoughts also come up in my everyday life, like pushing someone in front of a train. I would never, ever do such a thing, I am far from being a violent person. But why do these things sometimes pop up? Does someone else have those kinda thoughts?

    aragorn18 A:

    These are called Intrusive Thoughts and are very common. I would say that most people think them from time to time. If they aren't otherwise interfering with your life then you can safely ignore them as just another part of the human experience. If they are causing you distress then you might want to see a professional about them.

    More information: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Intrusive_thought
