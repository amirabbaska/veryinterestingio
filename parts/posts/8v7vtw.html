    ELI5: Is there a reason we sometimes feel overwhelmingly depressed and/or alone after a mid-day nap?

    jamietbrown A:

    This happens because your sleep wake cycles are controlled by the hormone melatonin (as well as cortisol), when you have just woken up the level of melatonin in your blood is high a side effect of which is mood changes as melatonin is also associated with regulating mood and emotions.
