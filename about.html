About

Hi, my name is Michel. I love to surf on Reddit and especially enjoy reading the questions and answers of the Subreddits askscience and explainlikeimfive (ELI5).

Reddit can be overwhelming for beginners and very time consuming in general because you get lost in the tens of thousands of witty comments and all the embedded links carry you to parts of the internet you can escape from even less.

So as a weekend project I developed this website that intends to make you smarter by reading the most upvoted content from those subreddits and presenting them in an easily accessible manner.

If you want to ask a question or answer some yourself, please be referred to the according subreddit (see above).


You can reach me at michel@veryinteresting.io. Feedback is always highly welcomed or just say hello if you'd like to :)
